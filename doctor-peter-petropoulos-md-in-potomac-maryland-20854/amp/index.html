How soon after pregnancy can CoolSculpting be done?
Coolsculpting after pregnancy
Dec 16, 2018
Hi, great question. There is no defined time period but my advice would to await until you are at least 3 months after pregnancy, and 6 months post c-section if we are treating the abdomen. The other areas have no time constraint but there are so many metabolic and physiologic changes after pregnancy that you may wait until your body has returned to pre-pregnancy status to figure out what areas may need it the most. Good luck and thanks for your question.
