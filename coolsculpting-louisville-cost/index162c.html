Advantages

Fat deposits in problem areas can be removed by many methods, including surgical ones, but coolsculpting has many advantages over them.

Cryolipolysis does not require violation of the integrity of the skin, so the risk of infection of the skin and blood is significantly reduced. The procedure is absolutely painless, the patient feels only a slight cold in the area of exposure.

Coolsculpting leaves no traces and scars, does not require recovery procedures, and the patient can return to the normal rhythm of life immediately after the end of the procedure.

Coolsculpting improves skin condition and allows the skin to contract naturally, while liposuction often requires excision of skin folds. But the most important advantage is that cryolipolysis gives a lasting result, the cells continue to break down after the procedure.

Effect

Coolsculpting is used to correct body contours and reduce body fat. It can be carried out in the abdomen, hips, back, forearms, chin. Cryolipolysis helps to get rid of the second chin, wrinkles on the abdomen, back and sides, fat pad on the back and abdomen.

Cryolipolysis reduces the appearance of cellulite. This procedure is particularly indispensable, when you need to reduce the volume of a separate area, saving the figure as a whole.

For example, in women with asthenic physique, fat can be deposited with age in the region of the hips and abdomen, and diets can significantly reduce the chest. In this case, cryolipolysis will be the optimal solution.

How is Coolsculpting Done?

The essence of cryolipolysis is that the fat cells are destroyed by the action of cold. Fat cells can not generate heat on their own, so prolonged cold is harmful to them. During the procedure, the fat fold is collected and seized by a special device with cooling elements.

A vacuum is created in the applicator, , and the fat tissue is tightly held and treated with cold. The procedure lasts for forty minutes, after that the device turns off and the skin is treated with serum to improve the effect.

During the procedure, some of the fat cells are completely destroyed, the exhausted substances are removed from the body naturally.

To achieve the effect of losing weight, several procedures are needed, and the procedures are conducted no more often than once a month. Within a month, the destruction of adipose tissue and the removal of dead cells continue.

Results of cryolipolysis. Recovery period and possible complications.

The first results from coolsculpting treatment can be observed at the 3rd week after the procedure. The final effect should be expected about 3 months.

The number of procedures will be determined by the parameters of the problem area – but, in general, you should complete 3-8 sessions.

Breaks between sessions should be 7-10 days. In this period of time the patient needs to adhere to a certain diet, and drink more water.

Negative consequences after coolsculpting are quite rare, and are manifested as follows:

  • Decreased sensitivity on the skin in the area of exposure to vacuum.
  • Traces from the nozzle – disappear within a few hours.
  • Redness of the skin and the formation of small bruises.
  • The first couple of hours after the procedure, chills are possible: take warm clothes to the clinic.

At the end of the cryolipolysis session, one should adhere to certain recommendations:

Do not take baths (including sunny), a warm shower. This can adversely affect the final result.

Physical activity should be minimized: exercise will slow down the metabolic processes. But lymph drainage massage and pressure therapy will not only improve your well-being, but will also enhance the effect of cryolipolysis.
