Can I get emsculpt on my butt if I’m 4 weeks pregnant
Cosmetic Procedures While Pregnant
Sep 5, 2019
Dear gpappas4,
Your health and the baby's health is our first priority! Elective cosmetic procedures like Emsculpt are generally not recommended while pregnant. Keep in mind, however, that Emsculpt is a great way to get your abdomen back into shape after pregnancy. It can be performed typically 6 weeks after a natural delivery or 6 months after a C-section.
