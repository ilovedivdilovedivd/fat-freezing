I had laser resurfacing under my eyes. They look baggy and wrinkly. What can I do?
Residual pigmentation and wrinkles
May 4, 2019
Based on your photos, I see irregular texture and pigmentation. I recommend starting with some filler like Volbella, which can be injected very close to the surface of the eyelid to improve the texture. You may need some bleaching cream, chemical peels, and fractional laser resurfacing to improve the pigmentation.

What Are the Unusual Lines Starting from the Corners of my Eyes and Extending to my Cheekbone?
Tear Trough Treatment with Restylane
Nov 4, 2012
Tear troughs on the cheeks can be treated with a dermal filler. For you, I would recommend Restylane.

1) Restylane is long-lasting, but not permanent. Lasts about 1 year.

2) Doesn't seem to swell excessively like Juvederm sometimes can.

3) Is reversible with Hylauronidase, in case you don't like it.
