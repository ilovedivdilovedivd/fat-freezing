CoolSculpting Pricing

CoolSculpting pricing will vary office to office but I can provide you with information about how it is that we price CoolSculpting in our nonsurgical beauty boutique: CoolSculpting is charged by the area.

We have 7 different CoolSculpting applicators including the CoolFit, CoolCurve+, CoolCore, CoolSmooth, and CoolMax.

For the smaller applicators meaning all but the CoolMax, and CoolSmooth we call two small applicators one treatment area. For the larger applicators like the CoolMax and CoolSmooth one applicator is equal to one area.

We charge $1,650.00 for the first treatment of one area. (again two smalls or one large applicator = 1 applicator)

We recommend that patients receive two treatments per treated area and offer a significant second treatment discount. So for the second treatment of one area we charge $550.00.

The idea is that for $1,650.00 you will receive up to a 20% reduction in the fat layer but by adding an additional $550.00 you can receive up to a 50% reduction in the fat layer. The consultation then obviously becomes extremely important to the pricing and the result.

There are further discounts as you add more areas and or if you have previously had surgery with me. (Paul Vitenas, Jr., MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)
