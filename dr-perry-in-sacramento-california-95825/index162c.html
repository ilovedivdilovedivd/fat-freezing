Which filler is better for the lips: Juvaderm or Versa?
Lip filler
Apr 18, 2018
Hyaluronic fillers are the best options for your lips for a few reasons. First, hyaluronic acid is found inside of you already, so it’s unlikely that your body will try to reject the filler.Secondly, the cushion-like volume the hyaluronic fillers create inside your lips looks very natural. Finally, hyaluronic attracts water to your lips. This gives them a plump and fresh look that no other filler can provide.
