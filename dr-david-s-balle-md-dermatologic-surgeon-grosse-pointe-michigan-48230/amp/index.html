Is Kybella permanent?
Kybella Is Permanent
Jul 18, 2015
Yes, the results of Kybella are permanent. Kybella destroys fat cells and our body is incapable of regenerating these fat cells or creating new ones. Patients will definitely need two treatments and some may need a third. Kybella is a a unique one of a kind treatment.
