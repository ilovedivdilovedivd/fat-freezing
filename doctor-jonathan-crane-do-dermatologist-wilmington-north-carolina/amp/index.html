How Much Does Fraxel Laser Cost?
Cost of Fraxel
Sep 25, 2012
The cost of Fraxel depends upon how aggressive the treatment is performed. A non-aggressive Fraxel treatment may run $700. An aggressive Fraxel treatment may run as much as $3,000. The most important point to consider when having laser treatment done would be the reputation and experience of the dermatologist performing the treatment.
Fraxel Acne Scars- Does it Work?
Fraxel Laser Works
Sep 25, 2012
Technology has advanced significantly in the past several years. There are now lasers which use Fraxel technology and penetrate deeper. These newer, more aggressive, lasers do surprisingly well in experienced hands. The key is to go to someone who uses lasers on a regular basis !
Zeltiq (CoolSculpting) to Treat Fat On Upper Outer Thighs?
CooLSculpting Works on the Outer Thighs
Sep 25, 2012
CoolSculpting helps get rid offat in any area in which it is used. In many patients, it is difficult to perform CoolSculpting on the outer thighs because the skin and fat are unable to be drawn up into the CoolSculpting apparatus. Therefore, patients who want their thighs treated should be evaluated by a dermatologist experienced in CoolSculpting and not be disappointed if the physician does not recommend treatment.
Will Cool Sculpting (Zeltiq) Address Skin Laxity?
CoolSculpting Does Not Address Skin Laxity
Sep 25, 2012
Younger people who have CoolSculpting will find that their lax skin often comes back to its natural appearance. Older people do not have as much luck with lax skin normalizing. Because of this, in many of our patients, we will do laser skin tightening in addition to CoolSculpting so that when the fat is gone the skin is not as lax.
CoolSculpting Vs. Zerona
CoolSculpting vs. Zerona
Sep 25, 2012
I have found that CoolSculpting results in a significant improvement in the vast majority of patients. On the other hand, I have not been impressed with Zerona at all. As someone who is doing a tremendous amount of CoolScuplting, I have seen quite a few patients who have been unsuccessful with Zerona and have done well with CoolSculpting.
How Much Does CoolSculpting by Zeltiq Cost?
Cost of CoolSculpting
Sep 25, 2012
The cost of CoolSculpting is very variable and depends on the size of the area being treated. For a very small area the cost may be as little as $625. When multiple applicators are used on multiple areas of an abdomen the cost could run as high as $5,000. Some dermatologist combine laser skin tightening with CoolSculpting. This often adds 10-20% to the cost.
Does Tazorac Get Rid of Wrinkles?
Wrinkle Creams
Sep 25, 2012
Tazorac and Retin-A are both tiopical Vitamin AZ derivatives that are used to help minimize fine lines and wrinkles. Both of these are helpful, but not miracle workers. The Vitamin A derivatives often help with irregular pigmentation as well.
Wrinkles Around Eyes
Eye Wrinkles
Sep 25, 2012
Treating wrinkles around the eyes often involves Botox injections into the crow's feet area. By injecting on the sides of the eyes it often causes muscles to relax and the overlying wrinkles then disappear. This is why Botox has become so popular for removing wrinkles.
Is Restylane the Best Wrinkle Treatment?
Fillers for Wrinkles
Sep 25, 2012
Lines in the upper lip are often bothersome. These unwanted lines can be filled by using a small amount of Restalyne or Juvaderm. Often these lines cannot be fully corrected as too much Restylane can make someone look fake. We, therefore, recommend considering injection of Restylane or Juvaderm combined with Laser Treatment.
How Long Does Botox Last?
Botox Lasts Differently Depending Upon the Area Injected
Sep 25, 2012
Botox usually lasts about three months. When used in the upper third of the face to help minimize facial motion.

When Botox is injected in the underarm area for sweating it can even last a year.

How Much Does Botox Cost?
Botox Cost
Sep 25, 2012
The cost of Botox typically depends on the amount of Botox that is being injected. The cost for 30 units is often around $380. This would be a low dose for the forehead, crow's feet and glabella area. Fifty units of Botox can often treat the upper third of the face and usually cost around $550. - Jonathan Crane, D.O., F.A.O.C.D. Board Certified Dermatologist
