When Can I Have Coolsculpting Again?

Repeated CoolSculpting Zeltiq treatments

In the last year we have been treating more patients with repeated CoolSculpting per site as we see continued improvement.

Typically we had suggested waiting every three months when we first offered Zeltiq but now we have our patients waiting at least two months between treatments, and two to three treatments is not uncommon. (Ronald Shelton, MD, Manhattan Dermatologic Surgeon)

Retreat an area with CoolSculpting

It is optimal to wait 12-16 weeks for the second treatment so that you can be sure that you have seen the full results from the first treatment.

Patients do not always need to retreat an area however, it just depends really on how large the bulge is and what the patient is trying to achieve with CoolSculpting. (Joe Gryskiewicz, MD, FACS, Minneapolis Plastic Surgeon)

Timing of second coolsculpting treatment is variable.

Timing a second treatment should probably be done with your physician. Typically I ask patients to wait 2-4 months before retreating an area. (Laurie A. Casas, MD, FACS, Chicago Plastic Surgeon)

Second Coolsculpting treatment

Typically in our practice we suggest to patients to not undergo a second treatment to the same fat area before 2 months and generally it is between 2 and 4 months. (Steven Hacker, MD, West Palm Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

Second treatment of Zeltiq

We advise our patients to plan on their second treatment no sooner than 8 weeks following the first. Most patients should expect to do 2-3 treatments per area. (Grant Stevens, MD, Los Angeles Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting and time between treatments

It takes 60-90 days to see results from one CoolSculpting treatment. It is recommended that the same area not be re-treated for at least 60 days.

Additional areas can be treated at any time. (Lori Stetler, MD, Dallas Dermatologic Surgeon)

When Can I Have Coolsculpting Again?

What we have noticed from performing hundreds of treatments is that the optimal time to do your second treatment is between 2-3 months.

What we do is we have our patients return in that time period for after photos and to assess whether they need a second treatment.

Many of our patients are pleasantly surprised that they find they are so happy with one treatment in a given area, they don’t need another one!

What they do I find is that once one area looks great, they want to work on other areas.

With patients that have more fat, I usually counsel them in the beginning that they are probably looking at two treatments. (Grace Liu, MD, Newport Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

Repeat Coolsculpting?

Repeat coolsculpting can be effective in further adipose tissue reduction; the average time between treatments is 2 to 3 months. (Tom J. Pousti, MD, FACS, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Fort Lauderdale

According to the Continuing Medical Education (CME) training provided by the company, Dr. Anderson clearly states that there is no harm in cooling the same area for more than one cycle on the same day.

This can be necessary during “pop-offs” should the patient move or jar the device applicator. Based on the physiology, it is optimal to wait the full 2-3 months before your next treatment.

I think further studies will need to be conducted to investigate a variety of protocols. (Will Richardson, MD, Fort Lauderdale Dermatologic Surgeon)

Zeltiq time between treatments

If you are treating the same area again you can have treatments 2 months apart. If the area has not been treated previously it can be done at any time such as upper abdomen and Lower abdomen. (Ariel Ostad, MD, New York Dermatologic Surgeon)

Wait at least 2 months before additional treatments

Because Cool Sculpting is a non-invasive procedure which triggers a natural cell death process it can take 2-4 months to see optimal results. I have my patients wait at least 2 months before having an additional treatment, but have recently started advising a full 4 months because I was seeing more changes with the additional time.

Depending on the amount of fat you have to lose, many of my patients have been happy with one treatment. (Rosalyn George, MD, Wilmington Dermatologic Surgeon)

Optimal time to retreat an area with CoolSculpting

It is true that additional far reduction can be achieved by repeating CoolSculpting treatments but unlike other non-invasive fat removal procedures, one does not necessarily need to perform the treatment more than once in order to see fat reduction.

Because the full effects of CoolSculpting will be not be seen until the second to fourth month after the treatment it would seem prudent to wait at least four months before retreating the same area.

The need to repeat the treatment will most likely depend upon the size of the bulge you wish to have treated and your final goals. (Ted Brezel, MD, Long Island Dermatologic Surgeon)
