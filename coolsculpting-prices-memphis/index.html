Coolsculpting Prices Memphis

CoolSculpting By Zeltiq is the only non-invasive method to reduce unwanted fat deposits by cooling fat tissue. The principle of cryolipolysis consists in selective and strictly controlled cooling of fat cells without their physical destruction. Thus surrounding tissues (muscles, nerves, vessels) during and after procedure are not damaged.

As a result of cooling, apoptosis (programmed cell death) of fat cells begins.

The CoolSculpting procedure carried out on the ZeltiqÂ® unit is characterized by a high level of safety, comfort and does not require rehabilitation.

Reducing the volume of fat tissue occurs gradually, as close to normal physiological process.

Non-invasiveness and lack of anesthesia are a guarantee of the safety of the cryolipolysis procedure for the body as a whole and the absence of side effects typical for surgical and laser liposuction.

CoolSculpting Procedure Is Non-invasive, Painless, And Involves No Downtime

The vacuum applicator is applied to the area of the intended effect, previously covered with a gel bandage. The dressing protects the upper layers of the skin from overcooling and at the same time allows achieving uniform cooling of the entire fat fold to the full depth.

Vacuum nozzle captures the fat fold into the applicator, resulting in maximum tight contact with the cooling elements. Cooling occurs not locally, but in the entire thickness of the fold, thanks to a constant vacuum.

The procedure lasts for 1 hour, during which the patient can simply lie or sit on the couch, while monitoring the doctor is not required. Controlled cooling of the tissue site is accompanied by a constant change in the level of the vacuum, which allows maintaining adequate blood supply in the area of exposure.

EFFICIENCY

The volume of adipose tissue is reduced by 20-30% for one procedure in the area of exposure.

SAFETY

Controlled cooling the fat folds with no side effects and complications.

COMFORT

The procedure does not require preliminary preparation, is easily tolerated and does not have a rehabilitation period.

Apoptosis fatty tissue under the influence of cold is associated with a paradoxical feature of their structure. Despite the fact that adipocytes contain a huge number of substrates for energy metabolism (triglycerides), their ability to effectively maintain their own energy needs (synthesis of ATP) is much lower than in other cells.

When cooling, because of rapidly developing energy deficiency, apoptosis of a significant part of the adipocytes begins. As a result there is a reduction in their number and mass. The apoptosis of fat cells and their subsequent phagocytosis occur within 2-4 months after the procedure. During this time, the volume of fat tissue decreases by 20-30%.

Coolsculpting favorably differs from traditional methods of hardware and injection lipolysis, as it excludes trauma to the skin surface, fibroplasia and scarring. As a result of a gradual soft decrease in the volume of adipose tissue, there is no systemic effect on the body in the form of a load of degradation products of adipocytes on the internal organs.

DESCRIPTION OF THE PROCEDURE

The doctor carefully examines the area to be corrected and, in accordance with the individual characteristics of the tissues, plans a procedure for cryolipolysis.

We combine different (according to the configuration, bowl size, with vacuum and without vacuum) applicators, we combine different techniques of cryolipolysis. Thanks to this approach, we form attractive and natural contours of the body.

Before placing the applicator in a specific area of the body or face, a special gel mat impregnated with propylene glycol is applied to the skin – to protect the skin from hypothermia. The vacuum applicator captures the skin-fat fold in the inside of its bowl.

The applicator, which is not equipped with a vacuum, works by imposing on the area requiring correction (not every zone has such excess fatty tissue that can be captured by the apparatus nozzle).

We have at our disposal all existing types of applicators for Coolsculpting by Zeltiq:

  • CoolCore – to remove excess fat in the abdominal area.
  • CoolCurve – to eliminate fat deposits on the sides, forming attractive bends of the body.
  • CoolSmooth – for correction of the external surface of the thigh.
  • CoolFit – for grinding beautiful outlines of the upper part of the hands, the inner surface of the thigh.
  • CoolMax – for working with large “girths” of the body.
  • CoolMini – to eliminate the second chin, jowl (excess fat in the line of the lower jaw – a characteristic manifestation of age-related gravitational ptosis of tissues).
  • NEW! Cool Advantage is a new and improved applicator for a faster and more comfortable procedure! The procedure lasts only 35 minutes instead of 1 hour!

The standard duration of the cryolipolysis session is 1 hour. This is the optimal standard, determined by the specialists of Zeltiq. Immediately after removing the nozzle, a 2-minute manual massage is performed – intensive mechanical kneading of the treated area.

It has been statistically proven that massage improves the effectiveness of the procedure – it helps to remove more (in percentage terms) the amount of adipose tissue. Also for this purpose, lymphatic drainage effects can be applied.
