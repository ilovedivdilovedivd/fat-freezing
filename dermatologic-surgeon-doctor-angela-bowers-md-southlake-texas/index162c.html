How long does it take to see results after Sculpsure, and how soon can you have a second procedure?
6 weeks to slimmer body
Jun 2, 2017
Generally, SculpSure heated fat tissue resolves over 6-10 weeks.  By the end of six weeks, we retreat the area if patient desires further reduction.   In summary, 6 weeks to see a difference and time between treatments.
How does the SculpSure applicator work?
Safe SculpSure treatment
Jun 2, 2017
This treatment is very safe and has been designed to keep the epidermis from heating to a detrimental level.  The heat is from a 1060nm laser which is directed to the fat cells.  Our bodies naturally remove this disrupted fat and has no known adverse effects.
Can Sculpsure treatment be used for inner and outer thighs?
Trim your Thighs!
Jun 2, 2017
We can definitely use SculpSure to treat any part of the thigh that bothers patients.   This area is a common complaint; we do see very good results!
What risks are involved with Sculpsure? Does it provide any amount of skin tightening and should area
Skin tightening and scars in Sculpsure
Jun 2, 2017
Generally speaking, we avoid treatment overlying any large scars.  The device allows us to easily attach and accommodate to unusual body contours.  The procedure is not promoted for skin tightening but there likely is some improvement.  The younger your skin, the more tightening you can expect.  Overall, SculpSure is very safe with no major risks.
What can be expected from the first SculpSure treatment?
SculpSure treatment experience
Jun 2, 2017
Many find the treatment to be very tolerable but can illicit some very warm to hot sensations intermittently with some cooling periods.  Afterwards, the area may be a bit sore for a few days but won't limit your activity.  We do offer patients to take some ibuprofen right before the procedure if they have a lower pain threshold.   We always have patients return for a second treatment or area which is a great sign that patients find it a very tolerable procedure.
Who is the ideal candidate for Sculpsure?
Sculpsure ideal patient
Jun 2, 2017
Most patients will qualify as a candidate for SculpSure.  This 25 minute treatment generally reduces the fat in that treatment zone by 25%.  The treatment field is best used, however, for areas such as the love handles and thinner abdomens. We can treat any body part and find great improvement when we do 2 treatments about 6 weeks apart.
