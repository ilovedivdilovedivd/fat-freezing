Coolsculpting St Louis Cost And Photos

Cryolipolysis is the most popular and effective procedure for the non-surgical removal of cellulite and fat. The procedure has all the positive properties of cold methods and is absolutely safe.

When cryolipolysis, fat cells are destroyed not at the time of exposure to cold, but for 2-3 months after the procedure.

As the number of fat cells in the hypoderm reduces, its thickness will decrease from 20 to 25%. Fat tissue is very sensitive to cold, other tissues are not exposed to the action of coolsculpting.

Fatty tissues are destroyed by cooling to -5 degrees. Due to the crystallization of fat cells, lipolysis is achieved.

The mechanism of action of coolsculpting treatment

Research is underway to determine the mechanism of cryolipolysis, there are not so many scientific data at the moment. It is suggested that the transition of the lipid phase or crystallization may be a releasing apoptotic stimulus.

Apoptosis (natural cell death) after lipocrilysis is not easily confirmed, its relationship with crystallization is least understood.

Cool Technology

The version existing for today says that cold affects adipocytes of subcutaneous fat and causes energy starvation of cells, as a result, the process of apoptosis is launched.

In comparison with necrotic changes, apoptosis does not occur so quickly, more physiologically, does not provoke inflammation and tissue scarring. The products of decay of fat cells are eventually removed from the body through blood and lymph.

Types of devices for coolsculpting treatment.

There are two types of apparatus for cryolipolysis:

  • With vacuum nozzle.
  • With a flat metal surface.

Apparatus for coolsculpting treatment with the vacuum nozzle.

Apparatus with a vacuum attachment from the American firm Zeltiq is one of the first devices for cryoliposuction, advertising activity in the cosmetology market began with its appearance. The device CoolSculpting, ZELTIQ (USA) has 4 applicators, different in size bowls, each bowl is designed for different zones. The fat fold is under the negative pressure of the apparatus and creates a partial resistance to the blood flow.

In the treated area, the fat tissue is heated inefficiently, when the necessary temperature (-5 degrees Celsius) is reached, the process of apoptosis of the fat cells starts.

The duration of the procedure in one zone takes about 1 hour. The disadvantage of the vacuum technique is the occurrence of hematomas in the treatment area. The cryolipolysis procedure on the CoolSculpting apparatus was approved in the USA (FDA) and the European Union.

Devices for cryolipolysis with a flat metal surface

Such devices for cryolipolysis include the device CosmeStar CRYO X (Germany), CooLipo Twin (South Korea). In these devices, the principle of effective heat removal from adipose tissue has been technically implemented by contact cooling of the surface of the skin with heat-removing plates.

The cooling process is strictly controlled by a multi-level protection system that does not allow the generation of thermal traumas. The device has 4 nozzles: 1 large nozzle and 2 medium sizes for cryolipolysis and one for RF lipolysis with cooling on the surface. And one and the other technology can be used separately. Exposure time is 40-45 minutes.

Indications and contraindications for coolsculpting treatment

Indications for Coolsculpting treatment

  1. excess fat deposits on the abdomen, back, waist, hips, legs and arms;
  2. cellulite and flabbiness of the skin;
  3. body correction and weight loss.

The procedure can be performed on any parts of the body, with the exception of the face, neck and decollete zone. The main condition for carrying out the cryolipolysis procedure is the presence of a fat layer with a thickness of 1-2 centimeters, if the thickness of the fold is less, the procedure is not advised to be carried out.

Contraindications to Coolsculpting treatment

Coolsculpting Addresses Your Problem Areas

  • trauma of the skin;
  • abundant scars or hernias in the treated area;
  • individual cold intolerance;
  • exacerbation of skin diseases;
  • pregnancy and lactation;
  • mental disorders;
  • diabetes;
  • use of pacemaker;
  • renal and hepatic impairment;
  • Obesity II and III degree;
  • diseases of the circulatory system;
  • predisposition to bleeding;
  • thrombosis.

Advantages and disadvantages of cryolipolysis

Benefits

  • Liposuction without surgery, does not require time to recover after the procedure.
  • Procedure without punctures and incisions.
  • The procedure is painless.
  • Removes cellulite and local fat deposits.
  • A minimum of side effects.
  • Does not harm internal organs.
  • Does not require preparation, the procedure is carried out on the day of treatment.
  • Combines with other procedures.
  • Does not cause tissue death.
  • No need for special underwear after the procedure.

The effect of coolsculpting treatment

The result of the procedure is noticeable after 2-4 weeks after treatment, the maximum effect occurs after 2 months. The amount of adipose tissue in the problem areas decreases from 2 to 4 cm in volume.

Disadvantages of coolsculpting treatment

  • The procedure does not start fat burning processes and is useless for obesity.
  • It is effective only in localized areas of fat accumulation.
  • The result is visible only after a few weeks, and sometimes months.
  • It is not effective on strongly stretched skin.
  • Expensive procedure.

Coolsculpting treatment

Before the procedure, a specialist assesses the size of the fat fold and, depending on the problem zone, makes a choice of 4 applicators. The gel patch and applicator are put on the problem area. The applicator captures the fat fold under the action of vacuum and cools the subcutaneous fat.

The entire session takes 1 hour, at first the patient experiences discomfort (tingling, pressure). During the procedure, the patient can listening to music, reading, working on a laptop.

FDA-cleared Procedure To Use Controlled Cooling To Safely Target And Eliminate Diet- And Exercise-resistant Fat

Sensations during and after the procedure

Pain and inflammation during the procedure is absent. After removing the applicator, the skin in the “capture” area turns red, it can turn blue. The skin comes to normal after 20-30 minutes. The patient can feel numbness and a slight chill that lasts for 30 minutes.

Important! It is recommended to drink plenty of water after the procedure, from food intake it is necessary to abstain for 2 hours.

  • Possible complications and side effects
  • In the first minutes of the procedure, discomfort may be felt.
  • Bruises and swelling.
  • Feeling of burning and tingling.
  • Loss of skin sensitivity for several weeks.
  • Convulsions.
