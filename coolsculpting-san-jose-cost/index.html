Coolsculpting San Jose Cost

Coolsculpting is a procedure for weight reduction by applying low temperature to fat cells.

If diets do not bring results, and a woman is not ready for surgical liposuction, alternatively you can use this method. Manipulation is carried out on all parts of the body, except for the decollete and face.

The main difference between cryolipolysis (second name) and ordinary liposuction is the absence of surgical intervention, after which the recovery period always follows.

With this method, there are absolutely no damage to the skin, no injections and no scalpel.

Coolsculpting

The procedure is as follows. Vacuum apparatus is used for carrying out cryolipolysis. A special nozzle captures the fat fold in a thickness of 3-6 cm and inside the nozzle on the adipose tissue is affected by special cooling panels.

The device is equipped with a special sensor that accurately shows the cooling temperature and allows you to achieve the most accurate its value.

Coolsculpting is used to remove fat deposits in the following places:

  • stomach;
  • thighs;
  • arms;
  • back;
  • buttocks.

Fatty tissue begins to break down at a temperature of 25 degrees. When cooled, the lipids within the fat cells begin to crystallize, the cell walls lose their strength and are destroyed.

In addition, when you overcool one part of the body, the body begins to burn fat tissue in order to warm the chilled zone. During the procedure, there is no need to carry out anesthesia.

The effect lasts about an hour, during which only pressure and numbness in the area of exposure are felt. After the session, a woman can return to her usual lifestyle.

Since the fat cells respond equally to cold exposure, waves and cavities do not appear on the body after the session. Bruising, hematoma, inflammation and swelling does not remain on the skin after the procedure.

How to enhance the effect of Coolsculpting?

Any physical activity, exercise and rational nutrition will help to achieve the best effect. In a complex, this will give an excellent result. So you can get rid of 40% of body fat.

Contraindications

The method is contraindicated during lactation and during pregnancy. Also it is impossible to do it with diseases of the nervous system, heart and blood vessels.

It is also not recommended to carry out in places of damage to the skin.

Cryolipolysis can not be done for people who have cold allergies. Specialists who perform cryoliposuction should select an individual weight loss program.

Exposure does not give any side effects. Unlike liposuction, after it does not need to take antibiotics and there is no danger of contracting the infection. Consequently, cryoliposuction is safer for the female body.

Therefore, the popularity of this technology increases with great force. Coolsculpting is an excellent option for women who do not want to use the services of a surgeon.
