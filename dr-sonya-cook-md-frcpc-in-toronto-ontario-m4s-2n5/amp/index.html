How can one treat skin atrophy from a cortisone injection on the face?
Management of atrophy due to corticosteroid injection
Sep 28, 2020
The good news is that, even without treatment, the corticosteroid-induced atrophy will gradually resolve. Flushing the affected area with saline will help to decrease the concentration of any residual corticosteroid at the site. The area can also be injected with hyaluronic acid filler to reduce the appearance of the atrophy until it resolves.

Botox vs. Filler to enhance the jawline
May 9, 2020
Based on your photo, filler would be a better option to enhance your jawline. Botox in your masseter muscles alone would likely accentuate your jowls due to loss of volume at the angle of your jaw.

Neck lines and marionette lines; already tried microneedling, will fillers be a better option? (Ph
Marionette and neck lines
Feb 2, 2020
Microneedling is most effective for the treatment of fine lines, acne scars, and stimulating collagen to rejuvenate the skin. Marionette lines result from volume loss and the descent of facial tissues, and are best addressed with hyaluronic acid fillers. Combination treatment with Radiesse and Ultherapy is an effective treatment for improving neck lines and skin quality on the neck and chest.
