The cold acts destructively on the fatty deposits. This is the basis for the procedure of cryolipolysis.

The essence of the procedure

During cryolipolysis, fat is exposed to a temperature of -1 to -15 degrees. Vessels, muscles and nerves do not suffer from such influence.

And fat tissue for a session has time to degrade so that the volume of the problem area is reduced by 25%. The process of decay of fat is not only during the procedure. It starts the reaction, which continues afterwards.

Mechanism of action

The effect on fatty tissue is carried out through the surface of the skin. The components of the dermis and epidermis are resistant to cold.

And the fat under its influence breaks up into fragments.

In liquid form, they enter the lymph and are eliminated naturally.

Do not puncture or cut in order to get to fatty tissue, transform it into a more liquid state and pump.

Contraindications

The safety of the procedure does not exclude the ban on its conduct. Coolsculpting has the following contraindications:

  • cold allergy;
  • obesity of a high degree;
  • impaired blood clotting;
  • severe diabetes;
  • skin lesions in the zone of non-surgical liposuction;
  • pacemaker;
  • infection;
  • hernia, if the treatment is assumed on the abdomen or back;
  • pregnancy and lactation.

Preparatory stage

The procedure does not require special preparation. It is necessary only to be convinced of the absence of contraindications, to appear on the session in good health. For the entire period of passage of cryolipolysis, it is necessary to refuse from taking anticoagulants. In between sessions, you need to drink plenty of water.

Procedure

Coolsculpting by Zeltiq begins with the fact, that a specialist measures the magnitude of the exposed area of the body and makes a photo. Then the process goes like this:

  • the skin in the area of contact with the nozzle is treated with gel;
  • the nozzle draws the problem area with the vacuum, and it is under the influence of the cold for an hour;
  • After this time, the nozzle is removed and the skin is cleaned.
  • During the session, discomfort, numbness, tingling, and mild pain can be felt.

Number of sessions

The length of the course is determined by the patient’s individual parameters. It can consist of 3 – 10 sessions. Between them there should be pauses in 7 – 10 days.
