How Many Botox Units for Horizontal Forehead Lines?
Forehead injections
May 6, 2018
There are multiple factors that will determine the number of Botox units required to treat forehead including severity of wrinkles, overall facial features (including brow positioning) and reconstitution of the product (we vary mixing techniques depending on which part of the face we treat). Over the years, many of us have reduced the overall forehead doses to ensure that our patients remain natural and still expressive. Many of my patients only require 6-12 units to accomplish such results. 
Can You Exercise After Botox or Will It Decrease the Results?
Exercise after botox
May 6, 2018
Despite numerous instructions, the exact science of exercise restriction after botox injections remains unknown. Most of us recommend waiting at least 4 hours after your treatment (although some of my colleagues are more cautious and suggest waiting 24 hours before resuming strenuous exercise).

Do lip fillers like Juvederm increase gap between nose and mouth?
Juvederm
Apr 29, 2018
Typically lip augmentation with fillers can be used to decrease the gap between the tip of the nose and the lip and it would be very unusual for you to notice the opposite result (based on your question). You should wait at least 1 week post treatment to determine if such "gap" is truly real. In the future if you still have such a concern, additional Juvederm filler can be injected right in the upper cutaneous lip (lip area above the red of your lip) to shorten such a gap.
