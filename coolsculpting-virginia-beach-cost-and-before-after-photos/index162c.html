Why cryolipolysis?

Cryolipolysis allows you to get rid of the most “stubborn” fat folds, which are especially difficult to correct: the “lifebuoy” at the waist, fatty rollers on the inside of the hips, “ears” in the lower back, the second chin.

Men find it difficult to get rid of fat on the abdomen, flabbiness of the hands and due to excess weight of the breast. These zones often remain imperfect even if you have managed to reduce the total body weight. It is this effect that provides cryolipolysis on the apparatus Zeltiq CoolSculpting.

Before the procedure of cryolipolysis, a compulsory consultation with a doctor is necessary. Based on these data, the physician selects suitable cooling applicators and calculates an approximate number of procedures.

How does the procedure work?

You are comfortably located on the couch, the doctor installs an applicator that matches the chosen zone. The vacuum applicator captures the fat fold, and the cooling panels with liquid nitrogen deliver the cold to the tissues. Applicator apparatus is designed in such a way that provides high-precision cooling, aimed only at the fat fold.

The procedure lasts 60 minutes.

At the end of the procedure, the doctor performs a massage of the affected area. Massage restores blood circulation and relieves light numbness after exposure to cold.

How does cryolipolysis work?

83% Of Patients Were Satisfied With Their Results

Intensive cooling causes the crystallization of lipids in fat cells, provoking their death.

Fat cells die within the first 2-3 days after the procedure and in the course of natural metabolism are eliminated from the body through the lymphatic system. Result: the fat layer is much thinner, the silhouette becomes more clear, and the problem areas are reduced in volume.

How many procedures will be needed?

The number of procedures for achieving maximum effect is very individual and depends on the weight, volume and density of subcutaneous fat, as well as a number of other factors.

You need a doctor’s consultation to determine the number and frequency of procedures specifically for you.

  7. The absence of recovery period: immediately after the procedure you can go home and do the usual chores.
  8. Comfort. The procedure takes place in comfortable conditions: you are conveniently located on the couch, you can read or watch TV.
  9. Easy. The procedure of cryolipolysis does not have a sharp toxic effect on the body, since the process of fat destruction occurs naturally and harmoniously.

How long does the effect last?

Physicians have proved that the effect of coolsculpting is much higher than that of diets and starvation. The fat cells in the process disappear, but do not become smaller. A long period of preservation of the effect, it is required to fix additional amendments in the habitual way of life. If you do not adhere to the rules, eat large portions, eat harmful foods, then fat will return within 3 months.

It is necessary to observe the regularity of physical activity and to eat right. Refuse overeating, harmful, fatty foods. Do not abuse alcohol.

Repeating the course of cryolipolysis every six months, the effect will be constant, and the result will only become stronger. Of course, if you adhere to a balanced diet. To keep the duration of the result for many years, you need to resort to additional massages of problem areas.

What temperature is used for cryolipolysis?

During the procedure, fat dissolves at a temperature of 16-19 degrees. Special nozzles help to gradually reduce the temperature to -5. At this time, the crystallization of fat deposits occurs, they harden, and their membranes die.

Cryolipolysis causes the body to turn on apoptosis – the protective function of the fat deposits, toxins. With apoptosis, unnecessary trash is eliminated from the body with the help of lymph, naturally.
