How long before I’ll see results from a CoolSculpting procedure I did three weeks ago on love handle
Results after CoolSculpting
Jul 3, 2017
Each patient varies but best results are typically seen 3 months after treatment. Initially it is normal to have swelling/bloating to the the area due to the inflammation of fat cell death. Your body will naturally eliminate the dead cells & each person does so at a different rate. We schedule all our CoolSculpting patient for a followup consultation 1 month post treatment. At this point patients are typically able to see results when comparing 1 month photos to their before photos.
Coolsculpting on abs after breast lift and augmentation, how long should I wait?
CoolSculpting Abs after breast surgery
Jul 3, 2017
It all will depend on which area of the abdomen you are CoolSculpting. Women tend to have 3 abdominal areas: lower, mid, & upper. The upper is very close to the under breast area & should be avoid for at least a month after surgery. Otherwise you are safe to proceed with CoolSculpting anytime!
