Does Cryolipolysis Really Work?

CoolSculpting Works for Fat Reduction

Yes, Coolsculpting uses cryolipolysis, a specialized technique that freezes fat cells without damaging the surrounding tissues. These cells are then broken down by the body’s own natural metabolic processes over a period of two to four months.

With each treatment you will see a noticeable reduction of fat from the treated area. (David L. Robbins, MD, FACS, West Des Moines Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Effectively Reduces Fat

Yes, CoolSculpting really does work. CoolSculpting by Zeltiq uses a patented technology called cryolipolysis (TM) to freeze 20 to 30% of fat cells in a targeted area, instantly killing them.

The body then takes time to get rid of the dead fat cells through the liver and other normal elimination processes.

Generally, the areas treated have “pinchable” fat that is easily sucked into the CoolSculpting applicator, where it is subjected to a temperature that is low enough to freeze fat, but not to freeze water in the surrounding skin, muscle and other tissues.

Recently, Zeltiq came out with two new applicators: CoolSmooth, which can be used on areas without pinchable fat, such as the outer thighs, and the CoolMini, which can be used on small areas, such as the chin.

In our practice, we apply the Z-wave technology to the frozen fat, which causes even more cell death and even better results. After the fat cells have been killed, they do not return. Providing you do not gain weight, the results you obtained with CoolSculpting will be permanent.

Please call a board-certified dermatologist to schedule a consultation that will answer all of your questions. (Kimberly Butterwick, MD, San Diego Dermatologic Surgeon)

Zeltiq Coolscultping works great for removing local fat bulges

Zeltiq or Coolsculpting works great for removing localized fat bulges such as love handles and abdomen. It is safe, easy, natural and effective. (Steven Hacker, MD, West Palm Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

Zeltiq is Safe and Effective

Yes Zeltiq does work in patients who have small “pooches” of fat that are difficult to effect with diet and exercise. It is an excellent non-invasive, non-surgical device in properly selected patients with realistic expectations. (Michael A. Persky, MD, Encino Facial Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting by ZELTIQ works and is now FDA approved

In September, 2010, the FDA officially approved CoolSculpting by Zeltiq for non-invasive (non-surgical) fat layer reduction of the love handles/flanks. I was one of the early adopters of the technology, because I saw for myself how well the procedure works.

It will not replace liposuction, but it can help remove small areas of stubborn fat in people near their ideal weight that have been resistant to exercise and diet. We use it mainly on the love handles, abdomen and back fat.

The abdomen and back fat areas would be considered an FDA “off-label” use of CoolSculpting. By freezing the fat in these regions, it permanently kills the fat cells to lead to a 20 -25% fat layer reduction with each treatment.

It can take 2-3 months to see the full results. We usually reevaluate the patient at 2 months to see if another treatment session would be beneficial. Overall, patient satisfaction has been very high in our Naples, Florida office. (Kent V. Hasen, MD, Naples Plastic Surgeon)

FAT FREEZING DURING THE COOLSCULPTING PROCEDURE

Permanent Fat Removal

Developed by Harvard doctors, Zeltiq destroys fat cells by a process called cryolipolysis. It basically freezes fat cells. The fat cells are cooled until they develop ice crystals within the cell that destroys the cell.

Over 8-12 weeks the body removes the dead cells and the fat layer shrinks. It is guaranteed to remove at least 20% of the fat cells in the treated area. This fat cell loss is permanent, and the area can be re-treated several times. (David Finkle, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)
