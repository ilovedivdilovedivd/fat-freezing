Doctors

Oscar A. Aguirre, MD, FPMRS

Name: Oscar A. Aguirre, MD, FPMRS
Statement: Dr. Oscar A. Aguirre has been a board-certified urogynecologist in Denver, Colorado for over 22 years. The founder and medical director of Aguirre Specialty Care (ASC), he is a leading expert in cosmetic gynecology and specializes in vaginal rejuvenation procedures such as vaginoplasty, labiaplasty, and perineoplasty. A preeminent pelvic surgeon, Dr. Aguirre is known for his success in treating urinary incontinence.

Dr. Aguirre was also an early adopter of nonsurgical vaginal rejuvenation treatments, including radiofrequency (ThermiVa), CO2 laser (FemiLift), and Nd/YAG lasers (IntimaLase). In 2006, he studied with gynecologist Dr. David Matlock of Beverly Hills, the developer of Laser Vaginal Rejuvenation (LVR) and Laser Reduction Labiaplasty.

After receiving his bachelor’s degree in molecular, cellular, and developmental biology from the University of Colorado, Dr. Aguirre remained in Boulder at the university’s Health Sciences Center for medical school. Upon graduation, he completed a residency in obstetrics and gynecology at the University of Kansas Medical Center in Kansas City.

Dr. Aguirre returned to Colorado to complete a fellowship in urogynecology and reconstructive pelvic surgery under the training of Dr. G. Willy Davila at the Colorado Gynecology and Continence Center in Denver, Colorado's first urogynecology practice and fellowship training center. At the end of his training, Dr. Aguirre took over as the practice’s medical director.

Over the years, Dr. Aguirre has added a variety of cosmetic gynecological procedures at the request of his patients, including the latest in vaginal surgery and body contouring, transforming the practice into the full-service center for women’s wellness that it is today.
