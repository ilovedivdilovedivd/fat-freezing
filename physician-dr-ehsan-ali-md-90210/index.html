Coolsculpting for people with coronary stents
Oct 14, 2019
Hello
Coolsculpting will not cause problems with your coronary stent, as the coolsculpting machine is treating the subcutaneous fat (fat under the skin). It will not affect the stents that are deep down inside your heart.
