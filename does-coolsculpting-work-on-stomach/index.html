Does Coolsculpting Work On Stomach?

Yes you can get CoolSculpting for the stomach. CoolSculpting is one of the most effective devices for noninvasive body contouring.

It is important to go in for a consultation with an experienced provider to see if you were a candidate.

Most clients will require anywhere from 1 to 3 sessions to get optimal results depending on the amount of fat that’s there.”

CoolSculpting is not meant to be a weight-loss device. It is meant for body contouring of localized areas of fat.

Most clients will either see or feel a difference after one treatment and I would say that in our practice 90% of her clients will see if visible improvement after two sessions.

Cool sculpting can also be performed on other areas such as flank, bra fat, arms, inner and outer thighs.

Massage after CoolSculpting on stomach

The recommendation from Zeltiq, the maker of CoolSculpting is for a two minute massage immediately at the end of each CoolSculpting cycle. This has been shown to dramatically increase the success of the procedure compared to when no massage is done.

The effect of a vibration table was not part of the initial trials where all current protocols have been developed and thus its true effect is unknown. Will it hurt your results? No. Could it help? Maybe.

Bottom line is any therapy that can help with the initial “breaking of the fat cells right after therapy will be helpful. Others can help with post treatment discomfort. (Paul Pietro, MD, Greenville Physician)

CoolSculpting Effects On Fat Cells

CoolSculpting causes a natural cell death in the fat cells. It reduces the number of fat cells. Through diet and exercise you can reduce the size of your existing fat cells which can further improve your results. (Michael A. Zadeh, MD, FACS, Sherman Oaks General Surgeon)

Coolsculpting and Fat Cell Death

Coolsculpting kills fat cells permanently. (Jason Emer, MD, Los Angeles Dermatologic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Kills Quantity

The CoolSculpting procedure selectively applies cooling to treatment areas to trick the body into using its own process to destroy fat cells in the treatment area. The cells get confused when they are exposed to such low temperatures and begin pre-programmed cell death called apoptosis.

The process generally takes two treatments per treatment site and best results are generally seen about 4 months after the first treatment. (Paul Vitenas, Jr., MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

coolsculpting is a FDA approved, non-surgical treatment of stubborn fat in areas including the waist, under arms, chin, thighs and back. The process is controlled cooling/freezing of fat cells causing a reduction in the number of fat cells (20-25%) and destruction of the frozen fat cells.

The frozen at cells are natural removed by the body over several weeks. This is a great therapy for targeted fat reduction but not as a weight loss technique. (Omar J. Barakat, MD, Munster Radiologist)

CoolSculpting: Is it for Weight Loss?

CoolSculpting iS an amazing treatment that permanently removes 20-25% of the fat from the areas that are treated. The results are smooth and natural looking. However, CoolSculpting is not designed for weight loss. It is designed to be most effective on those who are within 10-20 pounds of their goal weight.

The majority of patients will need to treat a minimum of 2 times in order to achieve optimal results. For those who do not fit the ideal criteria as good candidates, CoolSculpting can help to reduce the various bulges that keep clothes from fitting well.

Keep in mind that results do take about three months to fully show. You can treat again after one month . If you treated now, and then again in one month, you would see full results of both treatments by the end of 4 months.

Additionally, you would want to schedule an in-person consultation to see if you are a candidate for CoolSculpting.

They would need to see and feel your fatty tissue to know if CoolSculpting is right for you with everything! (Edward Stokes, MD, West Hollywood Physician)

Is CoolSculpting able to help lose belly fat?

We see so many ads on TV and media for CoolSculpting and it sounds like everyone should do this. However, the reality is that while CoolSculpting is a great treatment to reduce localized fat deposits, it is not a weight loss treatment.

Best results are seen in patients who are within 10-30 lbs of their ideal body weight. Without seeing you and knowing your height, I would not be able to tell if you are a good candidate.

One thing to consider is that CoolSculpting results are not seen for 2 months after the treatment with final results 4 months after the treatment session.

I would suggest working with your family physician to further improve your lifestyle with diet and exercise.

For instance, low carb/high protein/low fat diets such as South Beach diet with properly caloric amounts as well as 5-6x weekly exercise including cardio and weight training will help you lose fat more quickly.

The other option would be to consider possible liposuction or LaserLipo for more rapid fat loss which would be more noticeable. (Mary Beth Mudd, MD, Columbus Physician)
