Cryo Machine For Weight Loss

Cryolipolysis and cold RF-lifting – cryo machine

Cosmetic cryo machine, combines two technologies in one system – cryolysis and RF technology. Cryo machine offers a new medical solution for non-invasive elimination of excess body fat in certain parts of the body and removal of cellulite by the method of localized and strictly controlled cooling of the treated area. As one of the most effective technologies for fat removal, cryolipolysis defines a new standard of safety, speed and efficiency.

The use of cryolipolysis completely excludes the possibility of damage to the nerves, skin, internal organs and vessels by cold. Coolsculpting is performed with selective cooling of adipose tissue without physical destruction of all other cells.

The main feature of the cryo machine is the use of mono-polar radio frequencies, which allow creating a greater temperature difference between the maximum heating and cooling.

This, in turn, significantly increased the efficiency of the cryolipolysis procedure. The presence of RF technology makes it possible to carry out the procedure on a small part of the body, if there are not enough indications for carrying out the cryolipolysis procedure (a small amount of excess fat deposits). High-frequency electric field in the areas of action removes or reduces the appearance of cellulite.

How does the cryo machine work?

Whole Body Cryosauna

For the cryolipolysis procedure, the cryo machine combines both monopolar radio frequencies and cryolysis in one system. These technologies can be used both together and separately.

Cryolipolysis

Recent scientific developments have shown that lowering the temperature of the subcutaneous fat layer to +25 degrees can lead to natural death (apoptosis) of fat cells.

This technology formed the basis for the creation of the cryo machine, which allows you to lower the temperature of a certain area of subcutaneous fat to the desired and maintain it for the necessary time.

Controlled prolonged local cooling can lead to selective damage and loss of subcutaneous fat without injury to the overlying layer of skin, eventually solidification of triglyceride in fat cells begins.

The solidification process activates the process of extinction and aging of fat cells, and stimulates their lymphatic circulation and normal metabolism.

As a result of this kind of impact, the programmed death of only fat cells begins, since for a long time adipose tissue was without adequate nutrition. The duration of this physiological process lasts for 60-90 days. The result is a decrease in the number of fat cells by 20-40% in the zone of exposure for one procedure.

Monopolar radio frequency.

The cryo machine equipment can also be considered for RF lifting and RF lipolysis.The combination of RF technology with cryolipolysis, in the field of heat exposure and further cooling of surface fat cells, increases the temperature difference between the maximum heating temperature and the maximum cooling temperature.

This in turn allows a much faster start of the process of apoptosis of fat cells, that is, a natural programmed cell death. RF technology allows you to perform a procedure for correcting a figure on a small part of the body, if there is not enough indication for carrying out cryolipolysis (for example, a small amount of fat deposits).

Under the influence of radio frequencies as a result of heating fat cells, fat is split into fatty acids and glycerol, and then the removal of lipolysis products from the area of exposure. As a result, the volume of superficial adipose tissue decreases and the uneven pressure of some parts of adipose tissue on the skin, which, as a result, leads to the elimination or reduction of the appearance of cellulite.
