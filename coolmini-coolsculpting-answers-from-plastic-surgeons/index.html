CoolMini Second Treatment

CoolMini lowers the temperature of the fat cells to under 4 degrees Celsius for a predetermined period of time (just under 45 minutes). This prolonged exposure to the cold results in the death and natural absorption of the treated fat cells.

Under normal circumstances, adults do not have the ability to create new fat cells, so your CoolSculpting results will be permanent. That is not to say the fullness under your chin may not return since the remaining fat cells in that area can increase in size with weight gain.
