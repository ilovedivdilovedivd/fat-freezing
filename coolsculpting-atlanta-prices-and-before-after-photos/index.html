Coolsculpting Atlanta Prices And Before After Photos

Cryolipolysis is the “burning” of fats without violence over themselves. The essence of the method is to “burn” the fat cells with cold. Cryolipolysis is a body correction and a method of fighting fat, which is becoming increasingly popular among doctors and patients. Among other services for getting rid of cellulite, cryolipolysis is distinguished, first of all, by the fact that it does NOT cause pain, does not require medication and is NOT associated with any self-restraint.

In what cases is Cryolipolysis effective?

It must be remembered that cryolipolysis is not a means for overall weight loss, you will not lose 22lb at once, but it will help to tighten the body shape – on the hips, abdomen, in the sides and on the second chin.

If you need to get rid of a large amount of fat, you will have to combine coolsculpting with other methods.

To date, the course of cryolipolysis is the safest and most comfortable procedure for correcting body shape, suitable for people who want to look beautiful without harming their health.

If you are tired of exhausting yourself with diets and getting disappointed day after day without seeing the result in the mirror, you now have coolsculpting.

More detailed information you can get for free consultation of specialists of our clinic. To obtain a noticeable effect, it is recommended to undergo a course of cryolipolysis.

The rate of cryolipolysis averages from 3 to 8 sessions, the exact number of procedures is usually chosen individually for each person and depends on the initial condition of the skin, weight and place on the body in need of correction.

For example, in order to lose weight belly area may need from 6 to 10 procedures of cryolipolysis, and for the area of the hips – from 4 to 6 sessions. Usually coolsculpting is carried out with an interval of 5-7 days, while drinking regime and diet should be observed.
