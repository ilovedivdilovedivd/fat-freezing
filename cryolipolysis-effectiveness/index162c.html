Coolsculpting Inquiry

Stubborn fat that does not go away with diet and exercise can be treated with CoolSculpting technology. The procedure uses controlled cooling to target fat cells and eliminate them. The results are permanent. There are fewer fat cells in the area of the body that has been treated.

However, if you gain weight, the remaining fat cells can still enlarge. The best way to maintain your results is with a healthful diet and a good exercise program. I encourage you to visit a medical professional who has specialized training in Coolsculpting to work with you on the best treatment plan for your specific needs. (Eric C. Cappiello, MD, Chestnut Hill Anesthesiologist)

Cool Sculpting to reduce muscle mass

Cool Sculpting was designed to selectively reduce localized areas of fat. Since muscle and skin have more circulation and are more resistant to cooling effects, they are not injured. The traditional Cool Sculpting cups suction into them a fold of skin and fat but not muscle, so the freezing plates of the applicator have no impact on the muscle.

Lack of muscle use, ie disuse of your muscles will also atrophy their bulk. Muscle relaxers have been used off-label to reduce the size and mass of the masseter muscle with reasonable results. You may wish to contact a well-qualified and expert Botox / Dysport injector to consider using these muscle relaxers to reduce muscle mass recognizing the side-effects, limitations, risks and complications of their use. (Dean P. Kane, MD, FACS, Baltimore Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting effect on muscle

Fat cells are more susceptible than muscle cells to injury from low temperatures and coolsculpting was designed to freeze fat cells selectively. Therefore, coolsculpting will not eliminate muscle bulk. (Suzanne Kim Doud Galli, MD, PhD, FACS, Washington Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Side effects

The minimal to no side effects with Coolsculpting make it a great procedure to get rid of stubborn areas of fat. Coolsculpting results usually take 4 -12 weeks. Some areas may require 2-3 sessions to achieve the desired effect. (Patrick M. Moore, MD, Murrieta General Surgeon)

Effective treatment

Everyone’s body reacts differently to the Coolsculpting treatment. Not everyone will experience the same post-procedure side effects. Not having any side effects does not mean the treatment will not work. Full results can take up to 90 days and you will see a fat reduction of 20%-25% in the treated area.

I would recommend following up with your provider 60 days after your treatment to re-evaluate and see how your results are progressing, and to see if you will need another treatment. (Renee Burke, MD, Barrington Plastic Surgeon)
