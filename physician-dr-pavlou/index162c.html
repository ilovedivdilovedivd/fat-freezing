Dermal Fillers
Jul 7, 2022
Slow and steady replacement of lost volume will help minimize the appearance of deep folds and under eye bags. Hyaluronic acid fillers of different densities will help lift and restore volume.
Nurse told me that the success rate of PRP was only 10%, is this true?
Success Rate as high as 80% for the Right Candidate
Jul 7, 2022
In our clinic we see a large amount of patients for PRP Hair Restoration. Patient selection is important to achieve good results. If a patient has advanced hair loss we don’t usually recommend this treatment. Our success rate in patients with early to moderate thinning is as high as 80%. It is also important to note that different aesthetic providers have different protocols, devices and topical recommendations, so it’s difficult to give a blanket answer.
What treatment can help with my acne scarring?
Microneedling and HALO Laser
Jun 9, 2022
Our top three treatments to help minimize the appearance of acne scars are the Intensif (microneedling with RF), microneedling with PRP and HALO fractional resurfacing. All three of these treatments will help stimulate collagen and with regular maintenance help minimize the appearance of acne scars.
When can I get CoolSculpting again in the same area (lower abdomen)?
CoolSculpting and Emsculpt
May 5, 2022
CoolSculpting sessions can be booked four weeks apart, for the same treatment area. CoolSculpting and Emsculpt can be done on the same day, however some patients do experience tenderness and numbness after CoolSculpting and therefore could wait a week.

Best facial filler enhancements for hollow tear troughs and fine lines?
The TiredEye™ Treatment
Mar 31, 2022
At Skin Technique we perform The TiredEye™ Treatment for patient’s with volume loss around the eye area. This often involves using a more dense product for the mid-cheek, like Juvederm Volift and a thin product for the tear trough area like Belotero Balance.
What’s the best treatment for acne scarring
A Combination Treatment
Mar 31, 2022
At Skin Technique we address textural concerns like acne scarring using multiple approaches. Acne scars can improve but will never fully disappear and maintenance treatments should always be a part of the long-term treatment plan. We use HALO Fractional Resurfacing to target deeper scars, and combine this with either Microneedling + RF (Radio Frequency) and / or Microneedling with PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma).
