I had Juvederm Volbella last Thursday to fill lines around my mouth, now they are raised, will this go
Not to Worry
Feb 7, 2018
You may still be swollen from the injections, especially if you have any bruising. With any filler injection, swelling can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks after the procedure. After 4 weeks, if the lip lines still look swollen they can be treated with hyaluronidase to dissolve the excess filler. Can I use Bellafill to firm my jaw line?
Bellafill vs. Other Jawline fillers
Feb 7, 2018
Bellafill can be used to define the jawline, and will last for about 5 years. If it is your first time treating the jawline I would suggest using a filler such as Juvaderm Voluma or Restylane Defyne. Both of these products can be broken down with an injection of hyaluronidase if you did not like the final result.
