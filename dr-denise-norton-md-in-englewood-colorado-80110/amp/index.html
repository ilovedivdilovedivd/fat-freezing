Statement: Dr. Denise Norton is a board-certified general surgeon who has been practicing in Denver, Colorado since 1989.

As cofounder of Evexias Medical Centers, her priority is to give patients a novel approach to healthcare, with a focus on balancing hormones. She also provides laser aesthetic services including Botox, Juvederm, Voluma Kybella, Coolsculpting and other procedures to treat issues like loose skin, wrinkles, age spots, sun spots, hair removal and overall skin rejuvenation.

Earlier in her medical career, Dr. Norton specialized in treating vein disease, and cared for patients who were battling breast cancer and breast disease, including spending more than 10 years as medical director at Saint Joseph Hospital Breast Care Center—an experience that gave her first-hand knowledge of the devastation a cancer diagnosis can cause for both patients and their families.

Dr. Norton is expanding her horizons even further with the integrative healthcare fellowship she began in 2019. A companion to patients’ existing healthcare, integrative healthcare is focused on making sure healthcare interventions meet patients’ overall needs: physical, social, and emotional.
