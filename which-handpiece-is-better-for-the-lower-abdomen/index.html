Which Handpiece Is Better For The Lower Abdomen?

CoolSculpting has handpieces designed for various shapes.

The choice of handpiece in treating any area, including the abdomen will depend on the individual contours of the person.

Your physician will assess the areas of greatest fat and recommend the single large handpiece if the your hips are wide enough and the fat is central.

If you have narrow hips the smaller hand piece may be the right fit. If, as many men and women do, you have two areas of fat with a thinner area just below the belly button you may do best with two small handpieces one on either side of the tummy. As you can tell, the body tells us the right fit. (Susan Van Dyke, MD, Paradise Valley Dermatologic Surgeon)

I always tell patients that with the template I can see whether the fat can be sucked into the applicator. The more fat that can be sucked in, the better the result. How many CoolSculpting applicators can be applied at once?

It is perfectly safe to have multiple different areas treated with CoolSculpting at the same treatment session. Some patients would rather have all areas treated the same day while others may want to split it into different days.

If the office you are going to for your CoolSculpting has one device, only one applicator will be placed at a time. If the office has more than one device, then you could have more than one applicator placed at the exact same time. (Michael I. Echavez, MD, San Francisco Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting is a safe procedure to do no matter how many applicators during your treatment. We have the newest applicators (35 minutes) and two machines so that we can cut treatment times in half. (Jeffrey B. Wise, MD, FACS, Wayne Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting how many areas at once

it depends on how many machines the physician has to perform the procedure and the positioning of the patient. Usually one to two applicators are applied at one time.

You can do as many areas on one day as the patient can tolerate. We usually limit our treatments to 4 hours per session for patient comfort. (Jean M. Casello, MD, Northborough Physician)

Coolsculpting treatment times

It is safe to have multiple areas treated in one session. The number of areas that can be treated is dependent of the number of treatment head within the office. There is usually one head per machine and each and it usually takes one hour per treatment application of each head (newer models require only 35 minutes per application.

For patient comfort, we usually do not extend the treatment beyond 4 hours in one patient visit but it is safe to do so. (Jack Berdy, MD, New York Physician)
