Can CoolSculpting be done on the buttocks?
Coolsculpting on the Buttocks
Sep 12, 2018
Thank you for your question. Coolsculpting on the buttocks is not encouraged, however areas around the buttocks such as the "banana roll" which is the area under the buttocks or outer thighs can be treated with Coolsculpting. The best thing to do is to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss your options and the results you are seeking.
