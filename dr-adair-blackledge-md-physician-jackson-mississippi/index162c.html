Is there a non-surgical way to fix a nose-covered smile?
Nose covered smile
Sep 5, 2017
Great question!! I see this so often now that teenagers are averaging 200+ selfies a day. There is a small muscle at the base of your nose that pulls the tip of your nose down when you smile. A small, small drop of Botox will alleviate this problem and allow for a much more visible smile!
Do facial exercise and massage, improve face appearance?
Facial exercise
Sep 5, 2017
Facial exercises actually do the reverse – they age the face by creating unnecessary wrinkles. Think about other areas of the body that we use continuously such as the knee and elbow. They are covered in wrinkles and stretch marks. Over use of any skin (even by exercise) causes wrinkles.
What are the signs someone has had fillers in the nose? Can a plastic surgeon/dermatologist tell?
Injectable rhinoplasty
Sep 5, 2017
With good injection techniques, you really shouldn’t be able to tell if someone has had a liquid rhinoplasty. The correct patient and the appropriate filler are the two most important decisions to be made with injectable nose jobs. In my hands, I like Vollure in the nose. It’s easily moldeable, doesn’t leave bumps and lasts up to 18 months. It’s also great for correcting the smallest of assymmetries.
Lip Fillers: bumps from Juvederm Ultra Plus, am I botched?
Lip fillers
Sep 5, 2017
Start
Start massaging immediately. You’re not botched but it’s going to take a lot of massaging on your part to get your lips smooth. Remember there are also injectables that reverse Juvederm if the massaging doesn’t help!
How long does Juvederm XC last as a lip filler?
Lip fillers
Sep 5, 2017
Juvederm XC is FDA approved for up to a year. My experience in the lips is that it lasts about 8 months. The newer fillers, Vollure and Volbella last much longer and feel much more natural!
Is this a blood vessel damage? 1 year post lip injection.
Injection site after juvederm
Sep 5, 2017
This is a small capillary that may have been burst during your injection. It is now growing close to the surface of the skin. This can be treated with a laser treatment that removes capillaries with very little downtime. Hope this answers your question.
Is Botox of hooded eyelids availiable? My eyes are so droopy. I can’t do any kind of eye makeup
Droopy eyelid Botox
Aug 31, 2017
Botox works nicely to raise the brows. With enough brow elevation, some of your eyelid droop will be relieved.
How long will 5 units of Botox take to wear off in upper forehead?
How long does 5 units of Botox last
Aug 31, 2017
Even though you’re not pleased with it, 5 units of Botox is actually a very small and conservative amount. It more than likely will not last the full 3 – 4 months. Practice using your brows and elevating them as much as possible so that it will wear off sooner.
