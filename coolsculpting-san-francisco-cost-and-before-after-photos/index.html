Cryolipolysis is a non-surgical procedure that can quickly and easily get rid of local fat deposits in problem areas (on the forearms, sides, abdomen, inside and outside of the thighs).

The essence of the procedure is the effect of low temperatures on problem areas of the body: under the influence of cold, fat splits and a gradual reduction in body volumes.

This contributes to the cleavage of fat cells and the removal of toxins and toxins from the body.

Indications for cryolipolysis

The procedure is effective in the treatment of cellulite, obesity.

Contraindications

Coolsculpting is contraindicated in case of individual cold intolerance.

What does cryoliposuction or cryolipolysis mean?

Cryoliposuction (cryolipolysis) – (Greek kρυο – “cold” lat. Lipolysis – “fat dissolution”) is an innovative painless technique that allows you to get rid of fat deposits under the influence of cold.

The local cooling of fatty folds will allow you to absolutely physiologically, naturally, without the burden on the body to lose weight quickly and effectively. You do not have to resort to surgery experience discomfort and expose themselves to the risk of complications.

What is the principle of coolsculpting?

The principle is to cool the fat cells, which is achieved without surgery, thanks to the LipoCryo apparatus. During the procedure, these cells are strongly affected – cooling – while the surrounding tissues are not affected.

To do this, the applicator is fixed to the problem zone, the purpose of the hardware treatment is strictly the fat layer, while the skin, muscles, nerves remain under protection.

This contributes to the gradual withering away of fat cells, in the future they are transported with the help of the lymphatic system for processing, like the fats that we get from food.

Cooling? And is it dangerous?

Lipids from fat cells crystallize at higher temperatures than other water-containing cells. Therefore, it is possible to act strictly on them, without harm to the nervous, muscle fibers, skin.

The latest equipment – LipoCryo – controls the strength and speed of the impact, so the procedure is completely safe.

What is the effect of cryolipolysis?

The result is noticeable after the first session and grows within 2-4 months, the fold gradually decreases in volume. As a result, you will lose 20-50% of the volume of fat tissue in the abdomen, back.

How long does the effect last?

As noted above, during coolsculpting, the fat cells in the problem zone are gradually split. In the future, much depends on the patient – if he will maintain his weight with the help of proper nutrition, moderate physical exertion, the achieved result will remain stable.

Does cryolipolysis suit you personally?

  1. Do you have an average amount of unwanted fat (8-15lb of excess weight)?
  2. Would you like to eliminate visible fatty folds?
  3. Do you expect a gradual effect that will look natural?
  4. Do you choose a procedure that does not involve long-term preparation, pain during the session, risk of side effects?
  5. Do you prefer natural physiotherapeutic procedures to surgical intervention, medicines, chemical effects?

If you answered positively to at least 4 questions, you should consider carrying out cryolipolysis.

How many procedures are necessary?

The number of procedures varies from patient to patient. Your doctor can build an individual plan of procedures, depending on the results you expect. Depending on the area, the procedure lasts 20-40 minutes. As a rule, 4-6 procedures are necessary in 2-3 weeks.

Which parts of the body are suitable for cryolipolysis?

Most often, coolsculpting procedures are performed on the abdomen, hips, hands. Procedures do not hold in the neck, face.

What happens during the procedure?

The doctor will explain the procedure. During the procedure, a special gel is applied to the treated area. The specialist applies the manipulation to the treated area. Then in the maniple, pressure is created and suction of skin and adipose tissue occurs. In the area of exposure, pain can occur, and then the patient adapts during the first 5 minutes of the procedure. In general, the procedure of cryolipolysis is comfortable and does not cause feelings of discomfort and unpleasant sensations.

What can I expect after the procedure?

The result will appear gradually over the course of a month, the cosmetic effect will manifest itself after 1 procedure. Are there any contraindications for the procedure?

  1. circulatory disturbances in the area of exposure to cold (blood clotting pathology, thrombosis, etc.), use of anticoagulants;
  2. violations of skin sensitivity;
  3. recent trauma in the treatment, including with the damage of blood vessels (bleeding, hemorrhage);
  4. open and / or infected wounds, inflammatory processes, recent surgical manipulations in the area of cold treatment;
  5. severe skin lesions (extensive scar) or subcutaneous tissue (hernia) in the affected area;
  6. pathological sensitivity to cold (cold urticaria, Raynaud’s syndrome);
  7. neuropathic disorders;
  8. Pregnancy and lactation;
  9. the presence of a pacemaker in the patient.
