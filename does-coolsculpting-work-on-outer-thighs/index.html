CoolSculpting does not yet work on the outer thighs

CoolSculpting works by using suction to pull the fat and skin into an applicator that then applies cooling. Since the fat on the outer thigh usually can’t be gathered or “pinched” quite like that of the abdomen or inner thigh, CoolSculpting does not yet work well in the outer thigh.
