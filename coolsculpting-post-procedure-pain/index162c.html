What are the after effects of CoolSculpting?

As with any procedure the degree of discomfort can vary from patient to patient. No two people experience things exactly the same. Even things such as how well prepared the patient is explained to what to expect will affect their interpretation of the experience.

Finally, the size of the applicator will also impact on the patient’s experience. There are three different applicators avaialbe and the use of the large applicator seems to result in a higher incidence of post treatment discomfor.

Pain during and after CoolSculpting

During the treatment you could experience discomfort with the pulling, tugging feeling of the suction. However, severe pain should not be reported. After the treatment, bruising and tenderness are normal expectations that can last for a few days.

Patients experience pain differently because each person’s tolerance is different. Also, how the body responds is different. (Robert G. Aycock, MD, FACS, Bay Area Plastic Surgeon)

Why Do Some Coolsculpting Patient Report Little Pain and Others Report Severe Pain?

Just like anything else, people have different pain tolerances. For instance, patients that we see for breast augmentations, typically can have surgery done on a Thursday and be back to work on Monday.
