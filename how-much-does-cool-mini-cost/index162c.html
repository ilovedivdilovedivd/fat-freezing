CoolMini by Zeltiq Can “Freeze” a Double Chin

By using a patented technology called cryolipolysis(TM), the CoolMini applicator can freeze up to 20 to 30% of fat in a double chin. Freezing kills the fat cells, which are then eliminated from the body over time.

Because fat freezes at a higher temperature than does water, surrounding tissues are not affected by the treatment, making it virtually risk free. Side effects are minor and include swelling and redness, which generally resolves within a couple of hours to weeks.
