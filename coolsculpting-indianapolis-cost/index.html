Coolsculpting Indianapolis Cost

Cryolipolysis is the newest technology of non-surgical removal of local fat deposits and correction of the ideal silhouette with the help of a special devise. Coolsculpting is a patented technique of the main hospital of Massachusetts and Harvard University. Based on clinical trials, scientists have proved that fat cells are very sensitive to cold.

This discovery formed the basis of the cryolipolysis technique.

How Does Coolsculpting work?

The procedure of cryolipolysis is non-invasive (does not damage the skin) and is absolutely painless. The device for cryolipolysis works as follows: a special vacuum nozzle captures the fat fold and cools the subcutaneous fatty tissue.

After this, the natural process of death (apoptosis) of fat cells starts, as a result of which the fat layer is reduced by 20-30% within 1-2 months after the procedure.

Additional Sessions May Further Enhance Your Results

Unlike other methods that destroy fat cells and require additional lympho-drainage procedures for their removal from the body, the cryolipolysis procedure leads to a natural gradual destruction of fat cells and their processing in the process of metabolism. The procedure does not require any special conditions or the use of additional hardware techniques.

Cryolipolysis is safe for the body, because completely excludes the impact on internal organs, does not violate the nervous, vascular and muscle tissues, is comfortably tolerated and does not require additional anesthesia, corrective linen and additional procedures.

Cryolipolysis works well on the most difficult to correct areas – the anterior and lateral surfaces of the abdomen. Their formation is caused by the hormonal system, so the fat layer in these areas decreases more difficultly than in others.

With the help of cryolipolysis, it is possible to significantly reduce the volumes of these zones. Also the procedure is effective in the area of the knees, back, inner surface of the hands, inner and outer thighs.

The procedure for cryolipolysis is easily tolerated by patients, during which the client can read a magazine, work on a laptop or watch TV. It is also allowed to do other procedures of “beauty” – manicure or face masks.

The specialist places the maniple on the treated area, the vacuum absorption of the fat fold occurs, after which it is gradually cooled. Each zone is treated for 40 minutes. After the procedure, the client immediately return to the habitual way of life.

The patients see the first results already 3 weeks after the procedure. The final result is shown after 2 months. Reducing the volume of the fat layer occurs gently and gradually, and the result is persistent and prolonged. Today, the method of coolsculping is one of the most effective methods of combating excess fat deposits. With it help, a specialist can sculpt an ideal contour of the client’s body in just 2-3 procedures.

The effectiveness of the coolsculpting technique has recently been confirmed by the FDA (USA) medical certification organization and has already gained high popularity and popularity in aesthetic clinics and beauty salons around the world.

The popularity of this technique is largely due to its painlessness and comfort, as well as the principle of impact – cryolipolysis solves the problem of local fat deposits, in contrast to the body shape correction programs, in which the volume is uniformly reduced throughout the body.

This technique is perfectly combined with the course of weight loss in those cases where the reduction of the fat layer is difficult in certain areas.

Contraindications to cryolipolysis

  • high sensitivity to cold;
  • skin diseases in the treated area;
  • excess obesity;
  • area of bleeding;
  • pregnancy and lactation;
  • open and infected wounds;
  • hernia in the treated area;
  • presence of an electrocardiostimulator.

After completing the course of procedures, the volume of the fat layer is reduced by 50%. This result is very high in comparison with other methods, if we take into account its safety and non-invasiveness.

The device for cryolipolysis works equally effectively on patients of any age, the technique is suitable for both women and men. Its great advantage is off-season. The procedure does not affect the subsequent impact of the sun.
