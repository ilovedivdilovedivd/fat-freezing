The founder of Youthful Image Cosmetic Surgery Clinic in Edmonton, Dr. Sapijaszko has dedicated his professional life to pursuing excellence in several disciplines of medicine and surgery, to help his patients achieve their aesthetic goals.

Dr. Sapijaszko’s areas of specialty include liposuction, eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty), cosmetic laser treatments, nonsurgical rhinoplasty, and injectables such as Botox and dermal fillers like Juvéderm.

He also treats common dermatologic issues such as acne, scars, eczema, and psoriasis. Trained in Mohs skin cancer surgery, a topic he lectures about at national and international events., Dr. Sapijaszko can identify skin cancer and effectively remove the affected areas in his office.

Dr. Sapijaszko obtained his medical degree from the University of Calgary in Alberta. Today, Dr. Sapijaszko is board-certified by the American Board of Dermatology. He is also a member of the American Academy of Cosmetic Surgery (AACS), the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery (ASDS), and the Canadian Society for Dermatologic Surgery (CSDS). His excellent work in the field of aesthetic and medical dermatology has earned him the Award of Meritorious Service by the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Alberta.

In addition to treating patients at his private cosmetic surgery clinic in Edmonton, Dr. Sapijaszko is the medical director of the Western Canada Dermatology Institute. He also serves as a clinical associate professor in the Division of Dermatology at the University of Alberta, where he plays an active role in educating dermatology students.

Dr. Sapijaszko has over 22 years of practice in the field of cosmetic dermatology. He and his experienced staff are dedicated to providing patients with the latest state-of-the-art surgical procedures and noninvasive cosmetic treatments.
