Coolsculpting method

Long-term international research has helped scientists come to the conclusion that energy consumption is significantly increased in cold conditions.

All this is due to the fact that the level of ATP (a compound that participates in heat exchange) decreases in the cells of the body. At the same time, fat cells pass into the phase of energy starvation, after which they die.

It should be noted that this exposure to the apparatus for fat cells is distributed locally without damaging the surrounding tissues, without affecting the internal organs.

Cryolipolysis Zeltiq triggers a natural process of removing fat cells in problem areas: the temperature acts directly on the areas that excite the patient. Then gradually achieved a visual effect, the severity of which only increases with time: the maximum result is noticeable three months after the procedure!

How does Coolsculpting work?

The doctor invites you to a consultation, during which we discuss your goals and objectives, following the meeting, we determine which zones, in your opinion, require the impact of the cryolipolysis technique. First of all, the doctor specifies whether you have any contraindications. If you are a suitable candidate for the procedure Coolsculpting (and the procedure is suitable for both men and women with different types of figure), then we negotiate the cost of the procedure and select the nozzle suitable for your individual parameters.

During the procedure, we prepare the selected area of the body, after which the patients feel unusual sensations in the first minutes of the session, and a little later, in complete relaxation waiting for the completion of the procedure.

After removing the nozzle, a manual massage of the affected area is performed.

Leaving our clinic you can safely return to the usual rhythm of life: the procedure does not require recovery!

Are there any contraindications to carrying out cryolipolysis?

Of course. The procedure is really radical, although safe. But we, as doctors, always warn our patients that there are contraindications, for example: individual cold intolerance, skin sensitivity, the presence of a pacemaker, trauma and skin infections, diseases and allergies in the treatment area, bleeding, peripheral circulation disorders, severe obesity, hernia, pregnancy and the period of breastfeeding.

You need to warn the doctor in advance about possible contraindications.

Can fat return after coolsculpting treatment?

The fat cells, which are affected by the methods of Coolsculpting Zeltiq, die off as a result of exposure to cold. Then they are removed from the body. They will not return.
