Will Rhinoplasty get me a smaller nose?
Would I get a smaller nose from a rhinoplasty?
Sep 12, 2014
An exam will be necessary to determine how much of the shape and size is determined by the skin/ fat and how much of the cartilage and bone. Bone, cartilage and fat can be reduced but skin cannot without producing a scar. Sometimes augmenting the bone or cartilage with cartilage grafting can help give the nose a more defined shape. Hope this helps.
