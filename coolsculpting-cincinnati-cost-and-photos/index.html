Coolsculpting treatment

Local fat deposits, familiar to many of us, bring not a small discomfort. Cosmetologists and fitness trainers know, that it is these zones that are most difficult to correct.

Not everyone can torture themselves for hours in the gym, and not everyone can change the usual diet. And the result is more than modest. The reason is simple – hormonal influences do not allow to leave fatty deposits in these zones.

What, then, should be done? How to cope with this?

Any problem can be solved. And this is not an exception! Cryolipolysis is the newest patented technique for correcting a figure.

Fast, efficient and no pain!

What is its main advantage? You may ask.

  • No side effects
  • Selective effect on fat without damage to other tissues.
  • All processes launched during the procedure are organic and natural.
  • Under the action of cryolipolysis, the mechanism of natural death of fat cells begins to work.
  • Removes the “warehouse”, in which the body lays fat in reserve.
  • The physiological process of destruction of fat cells starts, while the contents do not enter the blood, the liver and internal organs are not damaged, everything is disposed of in special macrophage cells within 2 months.
  • The process of fat cell death continues even after the procedure is completed.
  • Complete painlessness.
  • Just one procedure lasting 1 hour allows you to reduce the thickness of fat folds by 25%!
  • The minimum of contraindications.

How does it work?

The skin-fat fold is captured with a nozzle-applicator under vacuum, which first heats up to 42 degrees for 5 minutes, then it is controlled cooling. Heating and subsequent cooling create a thermal shock for fat cells, which accelerates their natural destruction. The technique allows for 3 sessions to achieve a steady reduction of even the most persistent fatty deposits. The effect is only on fat deposits.

Cool Mini Before And After By Dr. Kurtis Martin, Cincinnati Plastic Surgeon

In the first few minutes, you may feel some discomfort due to the tension of the tissue with a vacuum applicator. Also, there may be a slight feeling of cold.

After the end of the cryolipolysis procedure, slight redness on the skin and an increase in sensitivity on the treated area are possible. These manifestations take place within a few hours.

Immediately after the procedure, you can continue the usual cases without any discomfort. You can apply the device on any parts of the body, where it is possible to grab the fat crease with a nozzle.

The best results are noted in the abdomen, the external part of the thigh, on the buttocks, back! The entire process is controlled by a patented system that eliminates hypothermia and, especially, frostbite of the patient’s skin.

The whole mode is programmed and strictly controlled by the computer system of the device in the process of execution.

The effect of the procedure is not only visible to the naked eye, it is very stable. If you follow the principles of rational nutrition during the course of procedures – the results will be great!

Within three days after the procedure, experts do not recommend a massage, take a hot bath for 4 days, limited to a warm shower.

Drink regimen should be followed, drinking 2 liters of liquid per day.

Advantages of the cryolipolysis procedure

Adipose tissue dissolves, the circulatory and lymphatic system removes toxic products formed after cell death. There is a redistribution of adipose tissue in the area of carrying out coolsculpting, no depressions are formed at the site of impact, the whole area will gradually decrease in volume.

  • Removal of unwanted body fat occurs without surgery. There is no toxic effect on the body of medicines, there is no risk of infection.
  • There will be no scarring at the site of the surgical sutures, there is no need to wear special pulling underwear;
  • This is really the dream of women, the technique allows you to lose weight locally, only on the stomach or only on the hips;
  • There is no time-binding procedure for the season of the year.
  • The procedure is financially profitable!

It is enough only 3 sessions for one zone, performed with a frequency of once every 2 months, so it is ideal for busy people.

After 2 weeks you will already see the result – the problem areas will decrease. The best form will be achieved in 6-8 weeks, when all processes are completed. How long does the effect of the procedure persist?

The thickness of the fold does not increase for two years. The only thing that doctors recommend to eat right and exercise to save the results for a longer period!

A unique technique of coolsculpting is not only for women, but also gives an excellent result for men who also want to reduce their stomachs in three sessions, remove excess fat in the waist area. Cryolipolysis is characterized by the absence of any side effects that are characteristic of many other methods of getting rid of fat deposits. In addition, there is a selective effect on fatty tissue, while other tissues of the body are not damaged.

One procedure of the coolsculpting treatment (duration 1 hour) is able to start the processes of natural death of fat cells, resulting in a 20-25% reduction in the thickness of the fat fold.
