Coolsculpting is a procedure without surgical removal of cellulite.

Advantages, principles of influence.

Coolsculpting is carried out on an apparatus that allows selective cooling of the folds of fat, which leads them to a stage-by-stage dying out.

The required area of the skin is captured with the help of the applicator by a vacuum, on which the cold is directed.

Neighboring tissue during the procedure is not damaged, for their crystallization requires a higher temperature. As a result, the creases on the abdomen, hips, sides are eliminated without a trace.

This procedure has many advantages:

  • control of cold with equipment;
  • low threshold of trauma;
  • without damage to the skin of the capillaries, etc .;
  • non-invasiveness;
  • the possibility of outpatient holding;
  • the session lasts an average of 50-60 minutes;
  • high level of safety;
  • Cryolipolysis can be carried out in problem areas without a system;
  • Do not need preparatory processes before the sessions;
  • easy tolerability after the procedure;
  • practically without contraindications and side effects;
  • positive reviews, especially those clients who do not tolerate surgery and injections.

Efficiency. What results can you expect after Coolsculpting?

As one of the most modern procedures for effective disposal of fatty deposits on different parts of the human body, cryolipolysis has received numerous reviews, both among patients who tested the technique, and among the doctors who conducted it. According to photos of the bodies of patients before and after the procedure, the results are visible to the naked eye. Some clients note the results after the first session.

Especially high effectiveness is noted in people who performed the procedure on the chin. Fat deposits in this zone are the most liquid, so they can be quickly reduced. The average index of fat reduction is about 33%.

Fat deposits in the abdominal area and waist are reduced by about 26%, most difficult to reduce folds on the buttocks. After several sessions, fat in this place is reduced by about 14-15%.

Indications for the procedure

Most often, coolsculpting is carried out on the abdomen and thighs. However, sessions can be done on the face, chin, knees, buttocks, legs, back. The effect of cold on subcutaneous fats is considered drug therapy. Therefore, before the procedure, it is absolutely necessary to consult a specialist.

The procedure is prescribed for people struggling with excess weight, people who spend more time in a sitting position, lead a passive lifestyle. Side effects, contraindications, precautions

Each procedure has a number of contraindications, cryolipolysis is no exception.

Contraindications:

  • genetic intolerability of low temperature;
  • pregnancy;
  • diabetes;
  • lactation;
  • asthma;
  • hernia in places of problem areas;
  • Raynaud’s syndrome;
  • problematic coagulation of plasma;
  • excessive obesity.

Carrying out the procedure at home

Attention! Cryolipolysis has side effects of an individual nature, the use of equipment can be used only after obtaining permission from the attending physician. Before the procedure, you need to carefully study the instructions attached to the instruments. At the very beginning of the session, the sensations of cold in the area of holding on the body are possible. You do not need to worry, the skin quickly adapt.

During the procedure, you are so relaxed that you can read, use the Internet, play games. During the session, some clients manage to take a nap. The first procedure does not need more than 30 minutes. After the session, bruises can appear on the skin, which do not linger for a long time. At home, you can conduct sessions on those parts of the body that hide clothes, and a session on the face is best performed in the clinic.

After acquiring a special device, you can start the session and at home:

First, the required place on the body is treated with a special gel.

The selected nozzle is then applied to the machined site. The nozzle is selected taking into account the size of the required zone for cryolipolysis.

The nozzle by the vacuum method captures the skin of the fat fold, the procedure begins. The effect of cold lasts about one hour. During this time fatty tissue gets an energy hunger, which leads to its destruction. To remove the risk of blood flow disturbance, in addition to cooling by the device, vibro-massage is provided.

After 1 hour, the nozzle is removed, the remaining gel is removed. The recommended course of the procedure depends on the amount of fat and density. As a rule, 3-4 procedures are sufficient to achieve the necessary effect with a monthly break.

Modern equipment and equipment, which is used in cryolipolysis.

For the cryogenic liposuction, the Zeltiq device has been developed, it has no analogues. Procedures are allowed only on this equipment. The whole name sounds like this: Zeltiq Coolsulping. The device received medical approval, after clinical trials, which proved its safety and effectiveness.

  • CoolMax is a large area, the nozzle is used on large folds;
  • CoolCore – the characteristics are similar to the nozzle number 2;
