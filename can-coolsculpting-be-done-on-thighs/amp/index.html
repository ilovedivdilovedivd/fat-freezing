Can Coolsculpting Be Done On Thighs?

Yes, depending on the patient, you can get great results on the thighs! The area has to be “pinchable” and able to fit into the applicator.

Best to have an in person consultation to see if you would be a good candidate for this procedure. (Tom J. Pousti, MD, FACS, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting can be very effective for the reduction of fat at the thighs. The applicator that is used for this are is the CoolFit applicator.

In order to see if you are a good candidate for this treatment, you should have a consultation with your doctor where you can go over treatment details and determine how many treatments you will need for best results. (Steven H. Williams, MD, San Francisco Plastic Surgeon)

Thigh Fat Reduction Using CoolSculpting by Zeltiq

Fat deposits in the thighs are a very common, especially for women. Many of my CoolSculpting patients are active, healthy individuals who live a healthy lifestyle but cannot lose that stubborn fat on the upper part of the inner thigh.

Patients such as this are ideal candidates for the CoolSculpting fat reduction treatment. There is no down time with CoolSculpting so my patients are able to continue their exercise routine after treatment. We have seen excellent results on the thighs after CoolSculpting treatments.

Some patients will need more than one treatment in an area to achieve optimal results.

It is most important to have a personal consultation with a trusted CoolSculpting provider. The provider will review your medical history, explain the treatment in detail, and assess your body to determine if this is the right treatment for you.

The top CoolSculpting providers are listed on the CoolSculpting website and they may be able to offer special pricing because of their status. My practice offers Instant Rebates for patients to receive a free CoolSculpting treatment with a treatment paid for.

This can be used for one area or for multiple areas (thighs and love handles.) CoolSculpting results are usually seen after three months. You and your provider may determine that additional treatments on the same area are needed to achieve your desired results.

A customized treatment plan is essential with CoolSculpting, as every body is different. (Michael Law, MD, Raleigh-Durham Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting for thighs

Coolsculpting will work for thighs however it all depends upon how well the chamber connects to your thighs. There may be a chamber in the future that fits thighs better than what is currently available. (Steven Hacker, MD, West Palm Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting Inner Thighs Treatment

Yes, CoolSculpting can be used on the thighs with great results. It is important to consult with your Doctor first to make sure you have enough fat in the area to be treated. (Brian C. Reuben, MD, Salt Lake City Plastic Surgeon)

We have the Coolfit for thighs

I suggest that you schedule a consultation with a Coolsculpting provider to evaluate your thighs to see if you would be a candidate for the CoolFit applicator on your thighs. It does indeed work and we have seen very promising results in that area. (David P. Rapaport, MD, FACS, Manhattan Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting & Thighs

CoolSculpting will work on thighs. As long as the skin and fat can be compressed in the CoolSculpting device. (John J. Edney, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)

We have treated patients thighs with cool sculpting and have had mixed reviews. What we find is the thighs tend to carry a more resistant fat. This means it typically requires more then one treatment to get desired result.

The other thing you must be careful about with working with the thighs is placing the applicator so it does not leave a groove in your thighs. (Frederick Lester, MD, Dallas Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting works well for thighs but you need a consultation first to see which applicator fits best for your individual area. The key is that you have to have enough fat to fit comfortably into the right size applicator to get a good result.

This is a great area for people who are wanting to fit into certain skinnny jeans! (Grace Liu, MD, Newport Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

Zeltiq Coolsculpting Inner Thighs Procedure

CoolSculpting is FDA-cleared for the flanks (love handles) and abdomen and is proven to be a safe and effective treatment. Unfortunately, at this time it is not approved for use on the thighs. (Daniel Shapiro, MD, Scottsdale Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting on thighs

If the applicator can be applied to the area, it can be done. There must be enough fat distribution for the applicator to fit the area. (Michael Gold, MD, Nashville Dermatologic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting and the thighs

Unfortunately, Coolsculpting is only FDA approved for the abdomen, back, and love handles. When it gets FDA approval for the thighs, I’m sure it will be a great area to treat. (Jeffrey E. Schreiber, MD, FACS, Baltimore Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting and fat reduction of thighs

The inner thigh area may sag more than other areas after liposuction. As CoolSculpting has not yet manufactured an applicator for the thighs and we don’t have results of the safety, I would be cautious to do this on the inner thigh as an off-label procedure. (Ronald Shelton, MD, Manhattan Dermatologic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting works well on inner thighs

The lower extremtity region including the inner thigh area are popular area to use coolsculpting. The treatment may be focussed on these regions and the results work well. Patients are typically satified with coolsculpting performed in this region. (Jon E. Mendelsohn, MD, Cincinnati Facial Plastic Surgeon)
