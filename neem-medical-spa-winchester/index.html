Doctors

Rosy Sandhu, MD

Name: Rosy Sandhu, MD
Statement: Dr. Rosy Sandhu is the founding director of Neem Medical Spa, with locations in Somerville, Lynnfield, and Winchester, Massachusetts. A board-certified internal medicine physician with over 11 years in practice, Dr. Sandhu is particularly well-known for her expertise in dermal filler injections and neuromodulators such as Botox and Dysport, to rejuvenate the face and improve symmetry and beauty.

After earning her medical degree from the Guru Gobind Singh Medical College and Hospital in India, Dr. Sandhu completed a residency in Internal Medicine at Rochester General Hospital in New York. She is board certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine and a member of the American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine.

Since early in her education, Dr. Sandhu has been keenly interested in the art of aesthetic medicine, especially as it pertains to anti-aging procedures. She spent many years studying and training in advanced cosmetic procedures, in addition to her hands-on experience since opening her three medspas in Massachusetts.

Her cosmetic treatment centers offer a range of state-of-the-art modalities, including Y Lift non-surgical facelifts, HydraFacials, body contouring procedures such as CoolSculpting, Botox, dermal fillers, cellulite treatments, and laser skin rejuvenation, including resurfacing, vein removal, acne treatments, hair removal, and tattoo removal. In addition, she specializes in physician-directed weight loss and bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT).

Dr. Sandhu also brings the art of Ayurveda, an ancient Eastern holistic medical science, to her practice via wellness treatments and holistic medical philosophies.
