Is the O-Shot painful?
O Shot not as painful as you might think
Apr 17, 2018
Most women are quite comfortable during the treatment and we take the upmost care to ensure the area is numb before starting. In fact, the discomfort is often described as a mild pinch or mild sting, lasting only a few minutes. The numbing feeling may last 30-60 minutes after the procedure.
Of course, we all have differing tolerance to pain, however, we are confident it will not be extremely painful. We also offer a chaperone service during the treatment to calm any anxiety you may have.
Is Ultra Femme 360 painful?
Ultra Femme 360 is not Painful
Mar 28, 2018
None of our patients have experienced any pain during or after their treatment. There is no downtime and you can return to normal activities immediately.
How many Ultra Femme 360 treatments are needed to see results?
Number of treatments for Ultra Femme 360
Mar 28, 2018
Patients may notice an improvement from the first session. Patients usually require 3 treatments a week apart for lasting results.The full results are achieved 3 months after the 1st treatment.

Side Effects of Ultra Femme 360

There is no down time. Side effects uncommon,some patients may notice mild redness or swelling that usually dissipates within a few hours.
