Efficacy of CoolSculpting after previous liposuction

Cool Sculpting can be used as an ancillary treatment after previous surgery including liposuction surgery. Generally CoolSculpting can remove upto 25% of fat present under the skin. Previous liposuction will not have removed all the fat you had and if there has been some weight gain with increased volume and profile convexity the yes CoolSculpting is a perfectly reasonable method to address it.

It would be good to discuss this with your original surgeon if possible. (Tariq Ahmad, MBBChir, FRCS(Plast), Cambridge Plastic Surgeon)
