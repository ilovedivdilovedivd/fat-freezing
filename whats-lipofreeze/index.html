LipoFreeze Does Not Require A Repeat Treatment Programme

All processes, launched during the procedure are completely organic and natural. Under the action of cryolipolysis, the mechanism of the natural death (apoptosis) of fat cells comes into play – a genetically programmed process of cell self-destruction. In part, aging cells that have completed the cycle of their development undergo apoptosis. So the body is self-healing.

Scientists have found that prolonged cooling of fat cells also starts a program of cell death, as a result of which a part of fat cells self-destruct within 2-4 months. That is, the process of fat cell death continues even after the procedure is completed. Simply unrealistic, but the method allows you to reduce the thickness of fat fold by 20-25%!

How it works?

Under the influence of vacuum, a skin-fat fold is drawn into the applicator nozzle, which first heats up to 42 ° C for 5 minutes, then its controlled cooling is carried out. Heating and subsequent cooling create a thermal shock for fat cells, which accelerates their natural destruction.

During the cooling, the temperature of the adipose tissue is reduced to 20-25 ° C. The technique allows for 2-3 sessions to achieve a steady reduction of even the most persistent fatty deposits. Using a special vacuum nozzle allows you to completely eliminate the effect on the internal organs, since only the fat fold is drawn into the applicator, which means that the effect is only on the fat deposits, and not on the internal organs.

Coolsculpting at home: is it possible?

Experts warn that the devices for cryolipolysis and the mechanism of procedure are unique, and it is simply impossible to achieve the desired effect at home. When performing the procedure, the fundamental factor is the precise maintenance of the set temperature and exposure time, which is achieved due to the vacuum nozzle and the parameters that the device supports. It is not possible to achieve a similar result at home, and if you simply apply cooling of skin areas with improvised means, you can get frostbite and other complications.
