Coolsculpting Back Fat Before And After

Coolsculpting can rightly be considered one of the safest and most effective non-surgical techniques aimed at combating excess fatty deposits.

Coolsculpting is the effect on the fat tissue with the help of cold. The technology of “freezing” fat was developed by the American company Zeltiq.

Cryolipolysis is performed on a patented Coolsculpting device, thanks to which, after the first session, up to 30% of the fat cells are destroyed and removed naturally from the body.

Coolsculpting fights local fat deposits that form in the abdomen, back, forearms, hips, on the back, on the knees, on the inner surface of the hands and in the region of the 7 cervical vertebra.

Does Coolsculpting Work On Back Fat

The treatment of each zone takes from 1 to 2 hours, depending on the applied applicator.

So, the Cool Max nozzle is designed to correct the abdomen and hips, the Cool Curve is ideal for more delicate areas – the “riding breeches” and the subscapular area.

CoolFit aims to eliminate excess fat in “hard-to-reach” places – on the knees, on the inside of the hands and thighs.

The procedure starts with the markup. Then a special film is applied to the area of exposure or a gel is applied.

Then, using the applicator, the fat fold is fixed, which undergoes controlled cooling. The safety of cryolipolysis lies in the fact that the cold only affects the adipose tissue without damaging the skin or vessels.

The procedure has a cumulative, prolonged effect. This means that the first results you can assess not earlier than a month.

The most pronounced effect of cryolipolysis will appear after 2 months. Coolsculpting is painless.

During the procedure, the patient does not feel discomfort, which gives him the opportunity to relax, read a magazine or listen to music.

Coolsculpting does not require a recovery period, so immediately after the procedure you can start the usual for you cases.

The only thing, that can be encountered in the first 20 minutes is reddening of the skin in the area of the nozzle’s impact.

Cryolipolysis is a new effective non-surgical technique for removing adipose tissue by cooling. Allows you to significantly reduce body volume and cellulite manifestations without a long period of recovery. The procedure is carried out using a special apparatus.

Its vacuum nozzle captures the fat fold and cools it to the optimum temperature, which acts solely on the fat cells, without damaging the surrounding tissues.

As a result of such a temporary “freezing”, fat cells begin to decrease in size and are gradually eliminated from the body naturally.

In one cryolipolysis procedure, the volume of adipose tissue in the affected area can be reduced by 25%. Decreases by 40-50% for 2 procedures.

The skin does not sag in the problem area due to a gradual decrease in fat.

The procedure of coolsculpting does not require anesthesia and surgical intervention, does not load the liver and other internal organs. Immediately after the procedure you can return to normal life!
