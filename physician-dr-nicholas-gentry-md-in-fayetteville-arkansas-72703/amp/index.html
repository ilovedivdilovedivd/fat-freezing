Could an oculoplastic surgeon put a filler in my hollow and sunken eyes?
options
Apr 25, 2021
You could do fat grafting but this is a difficult procedure to predict outcomes and symmetry because it is difficult to predict what percentage of the fat cells will survive on each side etc.

I would consider a combination of treatments: 1) cheek filler 2) tear trough filler 3) platelet rich plasma. These techniques are somewhat advanced so you do not want to see a novice injector but does not require an oculoplastic surgeon.

What fillers/botox should I get to look younger? Or should I get a lower face lift?

What is the best treatment for under eye wrinkles - Botox, fillers or something else?
other options
Apr 17, 2021
Botox of the lower lid may help some but is an advanced technique so be careful who you go to. Botox for Crows feet may also help. Platelet rich plasma (PRP) injections +/- filler are better for loss of volume in the tear trough.

For just fine lines/wrinkles of the under eye region, I like using PDO threads such as Les Encres. You can also do chemical peels or laser peels to the under eye region but these should be done prior to the absorbable PDO threads.

Does getting an O-shot hurt?
