Although extremely rare, skin cancer recurrences after Mohs surgery can occur. If a skin cancer comes back, it can present as a scab or bleeding area around the surgical site. Skin graft on side of nose after mohs keeps bubbling up.
Skin Graft on the Nose after #Mohssurgery for #skincancer
Sep 6, 2016
There are a number of reasons why your skin graft might bubble after Mohs surgery for skin cancer. Often, the skin graft will cover a pore or follicle on the nose. This can literally create an acne cyst under the graft. If the graft did not heal completely, there might be granulation tissue present. Scar tissue can also behave in a similar manner. Ultimately, You should check with your surgeon to ensure the skin cancer is not reoccurring.

Isotretinoin plus microneedling?
#Accutane and microneedling
Jul 31, 2016
In medical studies, Accutane has been shown to impair wound healing and worsen scarring. At my clinic, I do not recommended patients do any resurfacing procedures including Laser, chemical peels or microneedling within six months of taking Accutane.
Is it safe and possible to cool sculpt under eye bags?
#Coolsculpting and Under Eye Bags
Jul 31, 2016
Coolsculpting is not currently recommended for treatment of under eye bags. Surgical removal by blepharoplasty is probably the gold standard. Non-invasive treatments such as laser skin tightening and dermal filler injections  to soften the appearance of the under eye Skin can also be performed.
What is the correct time to schedule a second Coolsculpting appointment?
Scheduling #CoolSculpting treatments
Jul 31, 2016
The maximum results of a coolsculpting session are visible at 2 to 3 months after treatment.  While some physicians will stack treatments, I typically recommend about 4 to 6 weeks between sessions.  I am not aware of any added benefit of doing the treatments more frequently. 
Is there a non-surgical solution to paradoxical adipose hyperplasia resulting from Coolsculpting?
Paradoxical Adipose Hyperplasia after #CoolSculpting
Jul 31, 2016
Paradoxical adipose hyperplasia is an extremely rare adverse effect that has been reported with coolsculpting. At this point, liposuction is the primary recommended modality for correcting this outcome. There are some other body contouring devices available, including Vanquish and Sculpsure. You should speak with your physician to see if you are a candidate for these other non invasive treatments 
Will prescribed Prednisone pack affect my recent Coolsculpting?
Prednisone and #CoolSculpting
Jul 31, 2016
While long term prednisone use has been associated with weight gain, water retention, and increased abdominal girth, a short course of steroid, such as a dose pack, should not have significant effects on your coolsculpting procedure.
