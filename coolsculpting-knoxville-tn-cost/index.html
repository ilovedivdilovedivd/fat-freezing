Description of the technology

Cryolipolysis is a progressive procedure of fat splitting by cooling. The principle of the method is contained in a selective and strictly controlled effect on subcutaneous fat in the treatment zone up to 25 degrees, which leads to loss of vitality of fat cells. Cells of adipose tissue gradually die and are subjected to natural utilization during metabolism.

During 2-3 months the number of fat cells decreases and the volume of adipose tissue in the field of action is gradually reduced by 20-30%.
In the process of cryolipolysis, only the fat cells are affected; hypothermic damage to skin, vascular, nervous, muscular structures, internal organs is excluded. After a course of 1 to 3 procedures with a gap of 1.5 months, there is a steady reduction in body fat. The result is increased for 3 months.

Pros of Coolsculpting treatment

Cryolipolysis is a procedure of the highest degree of safety and comfort, which does not require anesthesia and rehabilitation period. Freezing is localized selectively in subcutaneous fat, while the surrounding tissues (muscles, nerves, vessels) are not damaged during and after the procedure. The reduction in the size of adipose tissue occurs evenly, the maximum is close to the usual physiological process.

The procedure of cryolipolysis is not dangerous to the organism in general due to local effects, the absence of tissue necrosis and the release of lipolysis products into the blood, is not associated with the introduction of certain chemical preparations.
