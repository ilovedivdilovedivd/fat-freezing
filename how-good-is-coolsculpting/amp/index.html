CoolSculpting Works!

CoolSculpting is a great option if you are a candidate for it. There are pros and cons to both CoolSculpting and liposuction. Difference in price depends on your treatment plan for each. CoolSculpting is safe, non-invasive, non-surgical, and requires no downtime.

CoolSculpting’s Non-surgical Cryolipolysis Technology

CoolSculpting is very effective at the permanent removal of approximately 25% of the fat cells in the area(s) that are treated. If more fat loss is desired then additional treatments would be required.

It accomplishes these results by placing a special applicator over a fat pocket and freezes the fat cells; depending on the applicator used and area treated it will take 35-75 min per cycle.

We have all the fat cells in our bodies, for life, by the age of around 20 and how big or small those fat cells are depends on our diets, exercise and general health.

When fat cells are frozen and crystallize they are permanently damaged and removed from the body by natural processes already in place to accomplish the task. Results post treatment are typically seen around three months.

Gaining weight after CoolSculpting or any fat reduction procedure will increase the size of the remaining fat cells in the body hence good dietary and exercise habits are strongly recommended after these procedures. The only way to know if you are a candidate is to be seen in person for a consultation to assess your areas of concern.

A center of excellence from CoolSculpting University typically will have the most experience and expertise you seek. (Paul Pietro, MD, Greenville Physician)

Coolsculpting for belly fat/what is it

Coolsculpting is a procedure where the fat cells are frozen, form crystals and rupture. These ruptured fat cells are then reabsorbed and eliminated by the body. The fat cells that are destroyed are permanently gone.

We do not continue to grow fat cells throughout our lives, If one were to gain weight however the surrounding/remaining fat cells would expandd to accommodate the weight gain.

We have been doing coolsculpting for about 10 years and the majority of our patients are very happy with the results.
