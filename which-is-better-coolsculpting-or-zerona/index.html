Coolsculpting and Zerona are different

Coolsculpting and Zerona are very different technologies, with different goals and indications.

Coolsculpting uses cryolipolysis, with a specific cold temperature used to trigger elimination of fat cells. This reduces fat in targeted areas, and permanently removes the treated fat cells.

Coolsculpting has predictable and lasting results. Zerona is used by some for reduction of body circumference. It is combined with diet modification, and does not target specific areas. In addition, since the fat cells are shrunk and not eliminated, the results may not be permanent.

So the two treatments are not interchangable. They work differently, and should be used differently. In my Albany, NY practice, I favor Coolsculpting, as I have been consistently impressed with the results in properly selected patients. (Jeffrey Rockmore, MD, Albany Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting and Zerona comparison

Although both modalities reduce volume, the methods are completely different. Coolsculpting permanently destroys fat cells. This means you no longer have the same amount of fat cells in a treated area. Zerona draws the energy out of the fat cell, in other words it shrinks the fat cells.

Many Zerona practitioners advise patients to follow specific dietary guidelines in order to maintain their results otherwise the effected fat cells can swell again. Zerona does not offer a permanent means of eliminating fat from the body. (Pamela Gallagher, MD, Long Island Plastic Surgeon)
