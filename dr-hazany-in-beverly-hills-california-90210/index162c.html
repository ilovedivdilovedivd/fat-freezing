Is it possible to reverse forehead wrinkle at 28?
yes!
Aug 27, 2021
Deep forehead wrinkles require three scheduled sessions of dysport spaced 3-4 months apart to soften forehead wrinkles completely (or almost). it is like a piece of paper that has a crease, you have to unfold it and lay it flat for about a year to get rid of the crease.
Asymmetrical smile – Botox, surgery, or orthodontics?
common issue
Aug 27, 2021
Like right or left handedness, people are dominant on one side of their face. Some more than others. Botox or dysport uses to balance the facial muscles will be your best option. I would start with injecting the levator labii aliquae nasii on the side that pulls harder.
