Can I get CoolSculpting on my marionette lines on my face? Thank you
Facial Coolsculpting

Fat transfer may also be an option . Finally , a mini facelift or full face might be indicated .Evaluation by a board certified plastic surgeon is recommended.

Can CoolSculpting be done on your belly if you have a hiatal hernia?

Aug 12, 2018
