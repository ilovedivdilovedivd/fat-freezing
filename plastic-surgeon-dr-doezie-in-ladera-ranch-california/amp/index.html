Implants After Infection

How is a saline implant removed via armpit incision? Can the capsule from the implant spread to the
Implant Removal
Jul 20, 2020
With proper retracting tools the incision is reopened in the armpit and the implant can be deflated or removed without deflation depending on the size of the implant. The capsule is your own body’s natural scar tissue around the implant, it does not spread. It can be removed but is not necessary if it is thin. If it is very thick it may need to be removed, otherwise removal of the scar tissue can possibly cause deformity to the breast.
Is there any treatment for my folded areola that causes the nipple to point down?
Scar
Jun 17, 2020
This is from scar tissue pulling inward and making the nipple point down. I have seen this a lot after breast implant removal and it can be corrected with a scar revision. Sometimes it can only improved but not completely corrected. Fat grafting may be an option to provide some thickness to the thin area that has been separated from surgery.
