Zeltiq Coolsculpting works for Fat Reduction

The medical community has been waiting for a fat reduction treatment like Zeltiq for a long time. Benefits: No surgical incisions or anesthesia is required and the procedure involves few side effects and virtually no recovery or downtime.

One treatment per area is all it takes to see 20-25% fat reduction, unlike other fat reduction devices that require multiple clinic visits for the same treatment area. And best of all, the treatment affects fat stem cells, so improvement is permanent.

If you gain weight, it will likely not go to the Zeltiq-treated area. Limitations: The process takes 1 hour to treat each area. So both love handles and the lower abdomen will require 3 hours to complete.

CoolSculpting by Zeltiq works but patient selection is key

Patient selection for CoolSculpting is key. The ideal patient is near goal weight, physically fit, exercises, eats a healthy diet and still has stubborn areas that bother them. CoolSculpting is not a substitute for weight loss, larger volume liposuction or a tummy tuck and when used in these situations disappointment will likely follow.

That being said, in a good candidate, CoolSculpting™ uses controlled cooling (Cryolysis) to bring the targeted fat cells to just above freezing without harming the overlying skin. Within a few weeks your body’s natural healing process then removes the damaged fat cells, resulting in safe, gradual fat reduction.

On average, each CoolSculpting™ procedure results in a 20% reduction of fat in the treated area, and patients can start to enjoy a toned, slimmer look three weeks following treatment, with the most dramatic results occurring over a period of two to three months. (Lawrence W. Shaw, MD, Arizona Plastic Surgeon)
