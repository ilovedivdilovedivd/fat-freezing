What should I do after failure of miraDry for armpit sweating?
Miradry failure
Aug 1, 2017
There are some patients that require a 3rd treatment even after being treated on level 5 twice before. In my experience, I have seen patients that have failed treatment due to the fact that the area treated was not sufficiently wide enough or did not cover the entire axilla. Upon using a gride that actually covers the enitre axillary region that is sweating, the procedure is subsequently successful.
Perhaps try a different Miradry center.
I had miraDry performed 6 days ago. Is it OK to go under plastic surgery in a few days?
Plastic Surgery after Miradry
Aug 1, 2017
You may certainly have a surgical procedure soon after the Miradry procedure. You do need to let the surgeon know about the Miradry procedure. It is also important to make sure that there is no inflammation in the axillas especially if any sort of implant is going to be placed.
Is the starch-iodine test effective at determining the treatment zone or could it cause problem spots to
Miradry treatment zone
May 30, 2017
Thank you for your question. I do believe that you are correct in your inquiry regarding the effectiveness of treating the entire area rather than a small localized area. Even if you are not sweating so much I would have recommended treating the entire axilla. In those cases it only takes one treatment instead of the 2 treatments that are required for actual hyperhidrosis. At this point you may need the second treatment to get the desired effect.
