I have a bald spot on beard in shape of circle. What should i do to get rid of this?
Alopecia areata
May 19, 2016
You are having alopecia areata. It’s an autoimmune process damaging hair roots. If its a single patch and non progressive it may subside on its own but better to consult a dermatologist.  I usually treat such cases with a intralesional injection of 5mg/ml triamcinolone and usually there is 100% recovery.
