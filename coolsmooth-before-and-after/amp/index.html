New CoolSmooth Applicator for Coolsculpting

In the spring of this year, Zeltiq Company introduced another, already the sixth applicator for the Coolsculpting device, called CoolSmooth. The possibilities of Coolsculptuing expand even more with its appearance, now it is possible to remove undesirable fat deposits practically on any parts of the body and carry out the procedure practically to any patient.

About CoolSmooth

The applicator is based on a plate consisting of three cooling panels. Each panel can change the angle of inclination relative to the neighboring one.

CoolSculpting Has A Range Of Vacuum-based Applicators

Thus, the entire plate, when in contact with the body, fits it well and each of the cooling panels densely contacts the skin.

Unlike all other applicators, the new CoolSmooth applicator works without a vacuum and its presence does not necessarily require a pronounced fat fold.

This property gives a new important advantage: now it is possible to process areas of the body where it is difficult or impossible to capture the fold (for example, excess fat on the lateral surface of the thigh, the so-called “ears”), in addition, it was possible to handle areas where the fold was difficult to capture from – the fact that the fat is very tight (for example, “fibrous” fat on the front wall of the abdomen in men).

Since the vacuum does not work for CoolSmooth, the applicator is held in one place by a reliable fastening system.
