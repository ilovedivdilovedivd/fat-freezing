Can I wear makeup on my face during my liposuction procedure on my abdomen and flanks?
Liposuction and make up
Jun 19, 2019
If you are having general anesthesia liposuction, generally, make up is not going to be advised.  With tumescent or local anesthesia, it is probably ok.   However, you aren't going to be going to lunch right after surgery anyway.  So, best to be all natural anyway.  Best of luck.
It's been a year since I had a (successful) knee replacement. Is it too soon to do lipo around the kne
liposuction of the knees after knee replacement
Jun 19, 2019
Liposuction of the knees can safely be performed after a knee replacement as long as all the swelling from the surgery has subsided.   Usually, a year would be sufficient in most cases as long as there has been no complications with the healing of the replacement.  Best of luck.
Is Ultherapy compatible with Botox and fillers?
Ultherapy in combination with fillers and botox
Jun 19, 2019
Ultherapy is a non-invasive skin tightening device that can lift and tighten skin.    Skin tightening devices can be a great adjunct to injectables and skin care for any anti-aging regimen.  Combination treatments that target different signs of aging tend to have a greater effect than when done alone.    There is no risk to doing the treatments and in fact there can be a great benefit.  Best of luck.
Vbeam safe on tattoos?
Vbeam and tattoos
Jun 19, 2019
Vbeam works well for redness from blood vessels.  However, the way the laser fires would not be good for a scar under a tattoo as the vbeam could pick up color in the tattoo and cause blistering or a burn.  If the scar is raised, possibly an injection of steroid could work.   Non-ablative lasers that don't attract color like the fraxel could be used as well.  But I would avoid using a pulsed dye laser over a tattoo.  Best of luck.

Is there a difference between how deep voluma vs. Radiesse is injected in the temples?
Voluma vs Radiesse
Jun 19, 2019
Voluma and Radiesse are both deep fillers used to provide volume.   Both can provide significant lift to the temples, cheeks and jawline.   As far as depth of injection, the products are injected in the same plane of tissue which is deep.  If these fillers are placed too superficially, a noticeable lump or bump may be present.  As far as which is better, it depends on the person.  Thicker skin individuals would generally do better with Radiesse while thinner skin may do better with Voluma.

Coolsculpting and breast implants would not cause a systemic rash.   Rashes are difficult to tell from photos without more information.  However, looking at the picture the rash could be a yeast rash or eczema.  Seek out a Dermatologist for evaluation.  Best of luck.
I had Botox 5 days ago. I noticed one eyebrow going higher than the other on day 2. Will it correct it
Asymmetry after botox
Feb 8, 2019
Botox treatments can take up to 14 days to take full effect; however, if there is asymmetry after a week, it will likely continue.   Often, asymmetry of the brows can be fixed with a minor tweak of a small amount of botox.  If the asymmetry is socially obvious, return for treatment; if not you could wait a few more days.  Best of luck.
Are my symptoms Botox related? I’m 31. made mistake of going to a medical training to get Botox/Juvede
Botox side effects
Feb 8, 2019
Generally speaking, the amount of side effects from Botox treatments are very low.  From cosmetic doses, there is no risk of systemic symptoms.  The biggest side effects are local issues such as asymmetry, heaviness in the eyelids or eyebrow, dropping of the brows or lids.  I would highly doubt this has anything to do with the treatment; keep an eye on it.   It sounds like its more muscle related pushing on a nerve.   Best of luck.
Top lip filler: I’m looking to even out this part of my mouth and wondering what I should even be look
Correcting lip asymmetry
Feb 8, 2019
Lip asymmetry is fairly common.  It can be volume related, related to the underlying bone or teeth or even muscle related.   Treatment will depend on the cause.  In your case, a small amount of filler to the smaller side will be able to balance out the volume.   Lip filler isn't really about the product as much as the injector.  Some fillers give more volume, some are more smoothing.   For correcting mild asymmetry almost any hyaluronic acid filler will do.

Sculptra for enhancement of the hips and buttocks
Feb 8, 2019
Sculptra can be a good option for those seeking non-invasive augmentation of the hips and buttocks.   The best candidates for this procedure are those who are very thin, who need small volume improvements or who have focal areas of concern.  Generally, most patients need 2-3 treatments of high volume sculptra to see results.   The main areas this is beneficial is the upper lateral buttock and hips to provide smoother contours.
