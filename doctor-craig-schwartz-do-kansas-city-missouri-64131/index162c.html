Tattoo removal.....dermatologist or tattoo removal center?
Remove your Black Ink Tattoo
Aug 5, 2021
The wavelength of a tattoo laser determines what colors it can treat, as each color absorbs different wavelengths of laser energy. The most common color in tattoos is black, and the best wavelength for treating black inks is 1064nm. This wavelength also is the safest for darker skin types. You could choose a laser tattoo removal center that either has a 1064nm laser or a PicoSure laser that has three wavelengths, 755, 532, and 1064, which are capable of treating all colors and skin types.
