How many Clear & Brilliant laser treatments are needed?
Number of Clear + Brilliànt treatments
Jun 11, 2016
Clear + Brilliant is a fractional laser at 1440nm. Fractionated lasers use multiple columns of laser light all over the face, so only a portion of the skin is treated at a time. We find a slight improvement in texture and tone after one treatment, but it is minimal. To see the full benefit most patients need 3 to 5 treatments.
