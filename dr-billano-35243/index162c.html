I am only 22 years old, and would like to start Botox injections of the forehead. Is 22 too young
Botox at 22
Aug 27, 2014
Botox works by relaxing the muscles in your forehead. When you see a wrinkle, it is due to a repetitive movement of a muscle therefore creating a crease. We can prevent that crease from happening by going ahead with Botox injections to help smooth out your forehead. Each person metabolizes Botox at a different rate, depending on expressiveness, activity level, etc. An experienced physician will be able to determine the amount that you will need and how often.
