Normal for tenderness and numbness at the same time 10 days after CoolSculpting?
Coolsculpting Question
Dec 18, 2017
Thank you for your question! All of these symptoms are completely normal after a Coolsculpting treatment. Stretching will help with the discomfort. If symptoms persist, see the doctor that provided your treatment.
Will CoolSculpting have any affects on fresh healing tattoo?
Coolsculpting Question
Dec 18, 2017
Thank you for your question! A fresh healing tattoo is still an open wound and very sensitive. I would recommend waiting at least 2 months until the tattoo is completely healed to have a CoolSculpting treatment done.

What Are Signs of a Hematoma After Breast Augmentation?
Hematoma after a breast augmentation
Aug 26, 2016
The principal signs of a hematoma after breast augmentation is acute swelling, which could happen overnight. Bruising could be present and the patient could experience pain when they extend their arms away from the body. Please see your board certified plastic surgeon so he can diagnose you.
How Soon Should I Start Massaging After a Breast Augmentation?
Massaging after breast augmentation
Aug 26, 2016
I tell my patients to start massaging whenever they get comfortable with the implants. This time frame is usually 1-3 weeks after surgery. Each surgeon has their own different techniques on how to massage and how many times a day so the best thing to do would be to consult with your board certified plastic surgeon.
How Long Do I Have to Sleep on my Back After Breast Augmentation?
Sleeping on your back after breast augmentation
Aug 26, 2016
I allow my patients to sleep on their sides as soon as right after surgery if that is more comfortable to them. Although, some people do like sleeping on their back after breast augmentation for the first 6 weeks due to the pressure. Please see your board certified plastic surgeon as every doctor is different.

Exercising post surgery
