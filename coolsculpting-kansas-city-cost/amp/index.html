How Safe is CoolSculpting procedure?

CoolSculpting are leaders in their field, mainly due to continuous clinical research and development of technology. A special temperature control system provides instant shutdown of the system, if uneven or inadequate temperature is fixed, preventing the risk of frostbite and scarring. Compared with other devices for cold therapy, whose settings are adjusted by the doctor, CoolSculpting excludes errors due to the human factor.

For each procedure, a sequence of actions is specially developed, and the procedure is performed only by qualified specialists who have passed specialized courses under the guidance of experts in cryotherapy (courses are held at CoolSculpting University).

More than 60 clinical trials have been published about the procedure that justifies the effect, safety and long-term results of the procedure.

During the existence of the device, already 4 million procedures have been carried out with a particularly high customer satisfaction rate (79% of satisfied customers, in case of unsatisfactory results, patients are offered alternative programs to achieve the desired results).

Currently more than 5000 CoolSculpting devices installed in 75 countries; in 30% of the clinics are installed 2 or more devices, 99% of the clinics use them actively.

Does CoolSculpting procedure is suitable for me?

The procedure for reducing fat is suitable for almost anyone who can easily find a layer of fat.

Coolsculpting is great for areas of the body that can not be corrected by physical activity or diet. Areas that can not be corrected with the help of sports and diet:

  • Reduction of the double chin,
  • Back area,
  • Sides,
  • Inner thighs, etc ..

Also, CoolSculpting is suitable for:

Controlled Cooling That Targets Only Your Fat Cells

  • Hand,
  • Belly areas,
  • Buttocks,
  • Hips.

The procedure CoolSculpting is prohibited if you are exposed to the following:

  • Cold intolerance (a rare disease – cryoglobulinemia, or a rare blood disease – paroxysmal cold hemoglobinuria),
  • Period of pregnancy or lactation.

Particular attention should be given if you are exposed to the following:

  • Hypersensitivity to cold
  • Violated circulation in the treated area
  • Neuropathic diseases
  • Sensitive skin is broken
  • Open or inflamed wounds
  • Use of medicines for blood coagulability
  • The recent operation in the problem area
  • Hernia in the treated area
  • Certain skin diseases (eczema, dermatitis, etc.)
  • Using a pacemaker or defibrillator
  • The client’s medical history and its physiological conformity to the procedure are clarified during the consultation.

Each person’s body and desires are different. A personalized procedure plan is produced by the doctor, a general assessment of the body is carried out.

The problem areas are treated with precisely controlled cooling of fatty tissues; At the end of the procedure, deep tissue massage is performed.

Evaluation of the treated zones to achieve the desired result is performed 2 months after the procedure; if necessary, conducting a repeat procedure.
