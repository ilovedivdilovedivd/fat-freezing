When one is affected with PAH post CoolSculpting, is it obvious?
PAH I've seen is obvious
Nov 2, 2019
I've seen two cases, one of my own and another performed elsewhere.  In both cases there was obvious fullness and firming of the treated area.  Treatment is liposuction, and the fat can be more difficult than surrounding areas to remove.
What happens if I do CoolSculpting and then treat over part of the area with Kybella?
Kybella shortly after Coolsculpting
Nov 2, 2019
Getting Kybella in an area treated by Coolsculpting a week earlier will not yield either treatment useless.  Multiple treatments of either are often needed, but it's best to wait between treatments.
Can I wear makeup on my face during my liposuction procedure on my abdomen and flanks?
Face makeup during body lipo
Nov 2, 2019
Wearing face makeup during your body lipo should be ok and not interfere with the procedure, but best to follow the advice of your surgeon.
How to get rid of redness, pigmentation, and dry dull skin?
IPL, Clear + Brilliant, Rx treatments, and chemical peels
Nov 2, 2019
To help your skin and keep it looking great, use a prescription skincare program which includes tretinoin and sunscreen, and do a series of IPL, Clear + Brilliant, and chemical peels.  Maintain with skin care and these treatments every few months.
I'm hoping Smart Lipo will help mother’s apron?
Smart Lipo, Renuvion, BodyTite
Nov 2, 2019
Smart Lipo, Renuvion, and BodyTite all tighten tissue.  They will provide some improvement, but are unlikely to maximally tighten your skin.
Do I need more filler in my lips to correct the unevenness?
Unevenness not from swelling
Nov 2, 2019
At this point, any unevenness between the sides is probably not from swelling.  Even though you "chew up" the filler, I would recommend a soft filler to finesse and get the symmetry. 
What kind of medical procedure can fix my acne scarring between my eyebrows?
Both filler and Botox will help, but filler best
Nov 2, 2019
Both filler and Botox will help improve your scar - filler to replace the lost volume, and Botox to release the downward muscle pull.  However, filler will do most of the heavy lifting.  Another subcision may be needed if the skin is tethered down.
Is it possible for the nose to grow as a side effect of fillers and laser treatments?
Nose won't grow from fillers or lasers
Nov 2, 2019
Your nose would not have grown from the filler and laser treatments you had.  Certain skin conditions like rosacea can thicken your skin and change the shape of your nose.
Can I still get Lipo under my chin if I've already had Kybella?
Lipo after Kybella ok
May 8, 2017
Yes, you can get lipo under your chin even after having Kybella.  However, it would probably be prudent to wait until the swelling after Kybella has resolved.
Three weeks after abdominal Liposuction my stomach looks exactly same as before?
Too early to see results
May 8, 2017
It's too early to see results 3 weeks after abdominal lipo.  Even though you feel that there is no more swelling, there is.  It can take 3 - 6 months for it to resolve.
How long for a scar to fully heal from a mole removal on your nose?
Scar healing
May 8, 2017
Scars can take 6 -12 months to fully mature.   To help improve the results, you may wish to consider using a silicone based gel or silicone pads.  Other treatments including fractional resurfacing, microneedling, and dermabrasion can also help improve the appearance of scars if need be.
How can I get rid of acne, black spot and dents on my face please?
Treating your acne and acne marks/scars
Apr 24, 2017
Firstly, you must get your acne under control.  I would likely recommend a combination of creams and pills.  Some of the creams not only treat acne, but also help brighten and even the tone of your skin.  Chemical peels can also help with tone.
For the scars, microneedling would be best after your acne is controlled.
What would be best to do to get rid of my double chin?
Double chin treatment options
Apr 24, 2017
If you were to pick a single treatment, liposuction would probably get the best results.  For even better results, it can be combined with ThermiRF, Ultherapy, or Thermage.For non-surgical options, Kybella would probably get you very good results too.
How much it cost for a jaw reduction using Botox?
Cost of jaw muscle reduction using Botox
Apr 24, 2017
Hi,You are referring to injecting Botox into the bite muscles which can lead to a squared appearance when enlarged.  Usually 20-30 units are injected per side.  Most providers charge by the number of units used.  In my area, it would generally fall in the range of $500 to $700 for 25 units per side (50 units total).
