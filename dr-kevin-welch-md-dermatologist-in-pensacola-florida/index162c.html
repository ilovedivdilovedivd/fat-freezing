Will the burn marks on my arm from IPL ever go away?
IPL "Burns"
Aug 9, 2016
"Burn" marks from IPL are not uncommon. They usually result from treating skin that is too tan, or recently exposed to the sun. The "burn" mark is sometimes called waffling, because of the uneven appearance. It will absolutely resolve on its own, without further treatment. It is rare for it to scar, unless it is very severe.
