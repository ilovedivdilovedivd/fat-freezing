Isotretinoin
Mar 21, 2015
It is not uncommon to continue to get breakouts for the first two to three months on Isotretinoin. Usually by the 4th or 5th month breakouts diminish significantly. You might ask your prescriber about your dose. Twenty mg is a relatively low dose, but dosing is dependent on weight. If you weigh more than 50kg (110 pounds), your dosing could be increased. There are many factors that can influence dosing decisions besides weight, but I would check with your prescriber.
