Best option for under eye filler?
Best tear trough filler
Oct 25, 2017
Hi Leila,
The area immediately under the eye is delicate and has thin skin. I would not recommend Voluma, as this is quite a dense, viscous filler. Voluma works well for filling deep tissue, such as the chin or cheeks, but in the tear troughs would appear very lumpy. I personally use Volbella under the eyes.
