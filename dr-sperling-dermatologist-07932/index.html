How to fade melasma naturally, is it treatable?
Melasma
Jul 1, 2015
Melasma can occur to women who are pregnant, on birth control, or going through menopause. The most important thing you can do is to use sunscreen daily with SPF 30 or higher with zinc or titanium. There are several bleaching creams, peels, or laser treatments also available to treat the pigmentation.

EpiDuo is a good medication.
