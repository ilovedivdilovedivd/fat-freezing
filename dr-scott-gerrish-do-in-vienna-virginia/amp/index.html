I have a very small umbilical hernia. What could happen if I CoolSculpt the area?
CoolSculpting Over An Existing Hernia
May 20, 2019
The current recommendation is to avoid CoolSculpting treatments over existing hernias. Some physicians will even go a step further and not treat over a repaired hernia. The risk lies in injuring the bowel with the suction itself or by the extreme low temperatures delivered by the applicator

Is paradoxical adipose hyperplasia a risk with non-suction cryolipolysis?
PAH In Non-Suction Cryolipolysis
May 20, 2019
CoolSciulpting is the most studied, most effective form of cryolipolysis. Within the CoolSculpting applicator choices, there is only one non-suction applicator, the CoolSmooth applicator. There have been a few case reports of PAH with the CoolSmooth. With over 10 million treatments world wide, the instances of PAH across all applicators is very low but occurrences with the CoolSmooth applicator is very rare.
