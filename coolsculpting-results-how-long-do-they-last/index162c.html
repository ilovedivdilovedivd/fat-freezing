CoolSculpting Results: How Long Do They Last?

Coolsculpting permanently reduces the number of fat cells you have in the area being treated. Each treatment reduces the fat in that area by 20-25%.

The number treatments depends on how much fat you are wanting to reduce. (Sheila S. Nazarian, MD, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Treatments and Procedure Time

Each CoolSculpting session is completed in one hour. Each repeated session will enhance the results on the body part you had completed.

It is common for people to treat the same areas more than once, about a month apart, to get additional reduction. (David L. Robbins, MD, FACS, West Des Moines Plastic Surgeon)

Depending on how much excess fat you are trying to reduce you may need 1-3 treatments.

CoolSculpting Results: How Long Do They Last?

CoolSculpting is a unique treatment in that it reduces the number of fat cells in the treated area by freezing them to the point of elimination.

So, as long as you maintain a healthy diet and an active lifestyle your CoolSculpting results will be permanent!

How many treatments you will need can be determined through an in-person evaluation with an experienced CoolSculpting provider.

Some people can achieve their desired results with just one treatment, whereas others may need multiple treatments. (Michael A. Zadeh, MD, FACS, Sherman Oaks General Surgeon)

The treatment time depends on how many cycles you need. Generally, an abdomen treatment can range from 1-4 hours. It depends on the individual’s anatomy. We usually recommend doing 2 treatments of the same area. (Lenore Sikorski, MD, Orange County Dermatologist)
Only One Treatment Needed for CoolSculpting

CoolSculpting freezes the fat cells in the area treated, and destroys them so that they don’t return. The remaining fat cells in the area can always get larger if one gains weight, but otherwise, once you have the reduction in fat cells you keep it in that area.

Additional treatments will offer greater reduction than the 20-30% fat reduction that one treatment affords. (Sabrina Fabi, MD, San Diego Dermatologic Surgeon)

Does CoolSculpting really work?

YES! We love the results we are seeing. Every person is different. Therefore we customize plans to each patient. Some people depending how close you are to your ideal body weight will need more then one treatment to get desirable results.

Each treatment takes one hour. You can knock it all out in one day or come back within 30 days to finish your package. We then take pictures at 60 days out and 90 days out. (Frederick Lester, MD, Dallas Plastic Surgeon)
