Cryolipolysis: how does it work?

The mechanism of action is based on cooling the fatty tissue fixed between the two plates in the vacuum packing to -10 degrees, starting with apoptosis is a natural process of cell death, in view of the different resistance of skin cells and adipose tissue to low temperatures.

The program automatically adjusts the duration, temperature and pressure using the American Lipo Crio Pro.

The skin is protected by a special gel-impregnated membrane.

You will see a pronounced effect a week after the procedure. But within two months the effect will partially return, then it will be necessary to repeat the procedure.

Indications

  • Obesity of any localization and degree, except for 3 degrees.

Contraindications

Tell your doctor if you have at least one of these contraindications:

  • pregnancy;
