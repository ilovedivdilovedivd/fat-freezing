How Much Does It Cost To Freeze Fat Away?
Coolsculpting Cost.

The great thing is that Coolsculpting is non-surgical and non-invasive. As a result, there are no other fees involved like anesthesia, facility fees, etc. You just pay for the procedure one-time and that’s it.

Pricing for coolsculpting is based on the applicators used. The applicators are how the machine freezes the fat. There are different sizes and shapes of applicators.

From large to small, to flat to curved. Depending on the shape of the body area and the amount of fat to be treated, different applicators will be needed.

This is where a consultation with an experienced Coolsculpting technician will come in.

They can help you choose the right applicators to give you the best results and the best bang for the buck. Applicators can start at $700 each and run up to $1400.

You should be able to buy packages of multiple areas to save even more.

But compared to surgical fees, downtime and risk, Coolsculpting is truly a cost-effective method to deal with unwanted fat! (Lawrence Broder, MD, Cedar Park Family Physician)

Here’s a range of how much Cool Sculpting by Zeltiq cost

The cost for Cool Sculpting by Zeltiq is usually priced per bulge. When you come in for a consultation, the medical provider would count up how many fat bulges bother you and calculate it from there.

For example, usually, the price is somewhere between $750 to $975 per bulge. The lower abdomen is comprised of two bulges (the left lower and right lower abdomen). Two bulges multiplied by $750 is $1500, or 2 X 975 is 1950. In this example, for the lower abdomen, the price range for Cool Sculpting by Zeltiq costs $1500-$1950.

The price range for the love handles would be about the same being that there’s typically one bulge on the right love handle and one bulge on the left love handle. (Some call this a muffin top, or spare tire 🙂 Assuming that there’s only one bulge per side, because everyone’s different, we can assume the price range to be somewhere in the ballpark of $1500-$1950.

That’s where the consultation comes in-to determine how many fat bulges can be reduced by Cool Sculpting by Zeltiq. Beware that, sometimes what you think may be a fat bulge may actually be just skin laxity.

At times, I do consultations and find that the person’s bulge is not a Cool Sculpting candidate because it’s just excess skin and no fat. Please come in for a consultation because, usually practices like ours offer a discount for multiple areas or multiple treatment modalities to meet your goals cosmetically and financially. (Amir Moradi, MD, San Diego Facial Plastic Surgeon)
