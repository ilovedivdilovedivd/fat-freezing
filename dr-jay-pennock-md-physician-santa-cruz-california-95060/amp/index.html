Is there any embolism risk on CoolSculpting?
There should be no risk of Embolism after CoolSculpting
Mar 21, 2017
Embolisms are usually caused by clots that form after a blood vessel is injured and blood, thickened by circumstances, is allowed to sit and coagulate. With CoolSculpting, there should be no blood vessel injury as the cold only targets the fat cells and we don’t treat over major blood vessels. Because CoolSculpting is non-invasive and non-toxic, we encourage our patients to stay well hydrated and continue with their usual activity!
