CoolSculpting effectiveness

CoolSculpting (CS) is tried and true for focused areas of fat thinning such as:

  • Tummy pooch (abdomen) and love-handles
  • Breast rolls and back rolls
  • Man-breasts
  • Inner thigh and under the butt “banana-roll”
  • Outer thigh and arms

Other areas patients have used CoolSculpting are:

  1. The bulge adjacent to a scar,
  2. Tightness around the arm, waist or thighs in clothing, and
  3. Residual fullness after liposuction or tummy tuck.

CoolSculpting is perfect for the patient on the go who needs no recovery and patients of all colors or ethnicity or size with bulges! When properly applied using the Zeltiq Cool Sculpting machine (yes, there are imitators and they are not as safe nor efficacious), you can expect to lose up to 20% of the fat in the bulge which fits in the applicator.

You may wish to further reduce with stacked procedures on top of a prior CoolSculpted area or “Treat to Transformation” performing CS along adjacent areas to reach a surgical lipocontouring effect. Please consult with a well-experienced, Board Certified Plastic Surgeon to obtain proper evaluation for the options available to you. (Dean P. Kane, MD, FACS, Baltimore Plastic Surgeon)
