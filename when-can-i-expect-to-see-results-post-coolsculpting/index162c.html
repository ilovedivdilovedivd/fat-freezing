When Can I Expect To See Results Post Coolsculpting?

Coolsculpting results can be seen in as early as 3 weeks and will continue to improve for up to 16 weeks. Most people see a peak in reduction at the 8 week mark (with a continued improvement for up to 16 weeks) but some are early to respond while others are slower in this process. It is impossible to guarantee when measurable results will occur since this process depends on the individuals ability to metabolize and remove cell debris. (Marcia Fleming, MD, Vancouver Physician)

CoolSculpting Results

CoolSculpting is effective way to reduce stubborn fat. Simply put, cooling panels are applied to targeted areas resulting in fat cell death. After the fat cells die, it takes time for the body to go through the natural process of finding and eliminating these dead cells.

Typically, patients start to see results in as little as 4 weeks and can expect to see final results at about 3 months. Every patient is different, but based on the clinical trials, that is the average time frame.

Occasionally, multiple treatments (cycles) are needed for the same area, to achieve an optimal outcome. Following up with your provider will give you the best information in respect to your anticipated results. (Umang Mehta, MD, Bay Area Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting is designed to treat fat cells by bringing them to a therapeutic temperature for an appropriate amount of time. Once the fat cells have been treated, they gradually shrink and are eliminated from the body.

Patients start to see results as early as 4-5 weeks, with maximum results at 4 months. This process takes a while since the body naturally metabolizes the fat cells. (Thomas E. Kaniff, MD, FACS, Sacramento Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Cool sculpting technology causes adipose cell death and a secondary inflammatory response to eliminate these cells.
