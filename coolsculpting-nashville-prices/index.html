Coolsculpting Nashville Prices

Cryolipolysis is one of the hardware methods of modern cosmetology, which allows you to eliminate excess fat deposits by exposing them to cold.

The method of cryolipolysis is based on the fact that fat cells are very sensitive to low temperatures: they gradually die from the cold and dissolve.

The purpose of fat cells is the accumulation of nutrients, unlike other cells in the body that tolerate the cold well, the adipose tissue cells do not have protective mechanisms against the cold and, under its influence, gradually disappear.

This low resistance of fat cells to the cold allows you to get rid of extra pounds without much effort, right in the office of a cosmetologist.

The procedure of cryolipolysis is available to people of all ages.

Because during the procedure, adipose tissue is exposed to cold, and muscle tissue, vessels and nerves are not damaged. Also in this case there is no injections into the body of any drugs and a long process of recovery.

The indications for the cryolipolysis procedure are as follows:

  • Excess fatty deposits in the abdomen, thighs (including the inner side), buttocks, back, legs and arms;
  • Cellulite and flabbiness of the skin;
  • The desire of the client to lose weight locally, in any one and several problem areas.

Contraindications to carrying out cryolipolysis:

  • Allergy to cold;
  • Damage to the skin in areas that are planned to be exposed to cold;
  • Asthma;
  • Eczema;
  • Raynaud’s disease;
  • Diabetes;
  • Excess obesity (stages 2 and 3);
  • Presence of an electrocardiostimulator;
  • Poor blood circulation in the area of the planned procedure;
  • Pregnancy and the period of breastfeeding.

Preparation for the cryolipolysis procedure:

Controlled Cooling To Target And Eliminate ONLY Unwanted Fat Cells

There is no need to prepare for the procedure of cryolipolysis. Before the first procedure, a consultation with a physician is conducted, on which a zone is determined that requires action to reduce fat deposits.

Cryolipolysis procedure:

At the beginning of the procedure, the doctor applies a bandage with a special gel to the necessary zone, this bandage will protect the skin from hypothermia and moisturize it during the procedure.

After that the doctor takes the applicator and with the help of vacuum draws the area, which is then exposed to low temperature.

At the beginning of the procedure, the client may experience a slightly uncomfortable sensation: pressure and tingling. Then these sensations disappear. Throughout the procedure, the doctor carefully monitors the temperature of the client’s body. On average, the duration of the procedure is about 60 minutes.

In our clinics, the Zeltiq device is used for the procedure, which has proved itself to be the excellent results of many satisfied customers.

It is important to note that during and after the procedure the client does not experience pain, after the procedure there is no swelling and inflammation.

Only within 20-30 minutes after the conducted cryoplipolysis session in the zone, that was exposed, the skin can acquire a red or purple tint, there may also be numbness, then it quickly passes. The procedure is so painless that during it the patient can do his own things: read, watch TV or work on a laptop.

Unlike other methods of removing surplus fatty tissue, for example classical liposuction, after carrying out coolsculpting, the client can immediately leave the clinic and lead his usual life.

Recovery after cryolipolysis procedure:

You should stop drinking alcohol, visiting the sauna and taking hot baths within a few weeks after the procedure.

The first results from the procedure will become visible after 2-3 weeks. The final effect can be observed several months after the course of cryolipolysis procedures.

The most noticeable results of coolsculpting in the area of the sides and abdomen, the zones, where the accumulation of fatty deposits is caused, in part, by the hormonal factor.

With the help of the cryolipolysis procedure, you can get rid of 20-40% of fat deposits in the most problem areas (stomach, hips, hips, etc.).

For a longer lasting effect, you need to monitor your diet in the future: avoiding large amounts of fats and carbohydrates, and regular physical exercises.
