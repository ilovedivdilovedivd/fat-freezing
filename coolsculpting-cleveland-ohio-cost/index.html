Coolsculpting Cleveland Ohio Cost

A new method for reducing fat deposits in the body proves to be revealed while in terms of body care by non-invasive methods. Women that want to get rid of a few inches of waist circumference or hip, without resorting to liposuction, can try the program known as “cool sculpting” by which fat cells are brought to a temperature that is low enough to destroy them in a few weeks.

Cool sculpting isolates certain body parts (stomach, hips, etc.) and freezes fat cells with a device. During a procedure that takes about 2 hours, fat cells are brought to a very low temperature, without affecting other cells of the body. A single treatment is often not sufficient to obtain the desired effects, but a sustained therapy over three or four months will bring spectacular results.

The layer of fat can be reduced by 20 to 40 percent, but the indications should be followed carefully.
Cool sculpting is suitable for people who want to eliminate extra fat in certain areas, but not face, as a result of problems of overweight or obesity. It is a controlled diet throughout the entire period of procedure, and after their completion.

Another advantage of this intervention is that patients can resume activities immediately after treatment (not experiencing pain or side reactions) and that the latter becomes a viable alternative for women with a hypersensitivity of the skin before they could resort to liposuction or other procedures specific.

The treatment based on melting fat is a normal procedure in beauty parlors. Those wishing to remove a few pounds quickly resort to these procedures successfully. However, in the last years, fat freezing has become more popular, since it does not even require anesthesia and is completely painless.
The cells in the human body react differently to heat and cold, at different levels of temperature. Thus, different stages can use different temperatures to eliminate fat. Through a very advanced device, the area becomes increasingly cold, to a temperature of 1 degree and even less.

During this procedure, fat cells are destroyed and replaced with new, healthy cells. As a response to this treatment, the skin becomes red. The procedure is performed under the supervision of a dermatologist or a surgeon.

This intervention does not require any kind of anesthesia. Patients may feel uncomfortable in contact with low temperature. But the feeling goes away after 10 minutes. After a single session, the patient can resume performing normal activities.

Fat freezing is a noninvasive procedure and is suitable to remove the layers of fat from many areas of the body. However, large amounts of fat cannot be removed, since this can lead to severe consequences.
This procedure does not destroy nerves in the skin, blood vessels or skin in the treated area. The effects of this treatment are visible in time, but fat cells continue to self-destruct after the treatment was completed.

Finally, the side effects of this treatment are mild and are related to more than the body’s reaction to low temperatures. In the first 10 minutes of treatment, the patient will feel a little skin, but this will disappear in time.
