Jan 19, 2015
Hi there,Take a small soft eyeliner brush (that comes to a point). Apply a small drop of Latisse to the brush. Carefully apply the Latisse right to the actual base of your lashes, being sure to avoid touching your eyelid skin. Let Latisse dry before blinking or closing your eyes. Blinking before the Latisse dries is the reason the Latisse is touching your lower lid skin and darkening/irritating the skin. Be careful to let Latisse dry first, before blinking. This ought to help!!Sincerely,Dr. Hui

How Latisse works
