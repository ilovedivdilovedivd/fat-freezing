Is there excess skin to deal with after the Laser Lipo procedure?
Excess Skin after Liposuction
Mar 5, 2018
If you have excess skin after procedure, messages or viora can help. Also wearing your compression garment will help shape the skin.
Are there any negative effects of laser Lipo on the abdomen, if one wants to be pregnant down the roa
Pregnancy after Liposuction
Mar 5, 2018
Liposuction will not affect pregnancy down the road. But pregnancy will affect your liposuction results. We suggest you get your surgery after you are done having children, as you might gain back the weight and reverse your results.
CoolSculpting. Are there any issues associated with having hips and love handles done?
Hip Replacement
Feb 27, 2018
Cool Lipo or Sculpting will not affect a hip replacement. This treatment focuses on freezing the fat cells of the body.
How long does belotero last under eyes?
Belotero
Feb 27, 2018
Belotero instantly binds with the water within your dermis and helps fill in creases, lines, and wrinkles. The changes to your face are immediate. Belotero lasts 6-8 months in most patients. Once a dissolver is injected it starts breaking down, but takes some time.
Is it possible to get smart lipo with a BBL instead of the traditional lipo?
Smart Lipo is not for BBL
Feb 16, 2018
Smart lipo can kill the fat cells during liposuction, so the fat is not able to be transferred later on. Healthy fat is able to be removed with traditional liposuction, and then transferred to the buttocks.
What’s best for smile lines Botox or filler and will it help in the long run?
Fillers for smile lines
Feb 16, 2018
Fillers would be the best injectable when it comes to smile lines. There are many products your doctor can choose from such as hyaluronic acid fillers, suchas Restylane, Juvéderm, These injectables fill and add volume to the creases or wrinkles in your smile lines.
Is there permanent scarring after liposuction?
Scars from Incisions
Feb 14, 2018
There is a very small mark from the cannula after Liposuction. Although this is a scar, for most patients it diminishes over time with help of scare gel like silagen. Peels and Carboxy therapy can also help diminish your incision scars.
