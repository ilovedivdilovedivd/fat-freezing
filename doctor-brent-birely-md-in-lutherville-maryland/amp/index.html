My Doc put Perlane onto my cheekbone & not into the skin. Any suggestions?
Perlane for cheekbone augmentation--how deep
Jan 5, 2016
Although perlane is technically a dermal filler it can be used at a deeper level in certain areas. The injection of perlane deep into the cheeks is actually a nice way to enhance the cheek without looking unnatural. Generally Radiesse is a better option in that area, as you can accomplish more volume with less product. However perlane will do a nice job, and should last between 8-11 months.
Are the results of Radiesse in the cheeks ever permanent?
Radiesse in the cheeks
Jan 5, 2016
While radiesse can stimulate your body to build its own collagen, radiesse itself is not permanent. The product generally lasts about a year, but some patients will have it a bit longer. however, injection of hyaluronidase to help break up some of the product has been successful in several patients. You may want to discuss that option with your injector so you don't have to live like that for a full year. Good Luck!
