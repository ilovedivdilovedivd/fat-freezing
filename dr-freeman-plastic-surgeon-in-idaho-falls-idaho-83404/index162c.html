Fat Leaves Through Kidneys
Aug 11, 2016
This is quite a common question, and a good one. CoolSculpting causes the breakdown of fat cells in a natural process called apoptosis. At this point, the body's waste system takes over, filtering the fat cells out through the kidneys so that they leave the body along with the urine. You are right in that CoolSculpting does not produce a significant weight loss. How Soon After Having Coolsculpting Are Measurable Results Seen?
Final Results Take Time
Aug 11, 2016
Unlike liposuction, which physically removes fat from the body and therefore creates a more or less immediate result, CoolSculpting freezes fat cells, stimulating them to die on their own and be carried out of the body via the kidneys. This process takes time to complete, and it happens gradually. This is why patients see preliminary results after about 3 weeks and more significant results after a few months. Most patients find that the results are well worth the wait

How Long Does the Coolsculpting Procedure Last and How Many Treatments Are Needed?
1 Hour for Long-Term Fat Reduction
Aug 11, 2016
The CoolSculpting treatment itself lasts for about 1 hour, after which there's no downtime or recuperation necessary. While some patients are satisfied with their results after a single treatment, others are better served by a series of 2 or 3 treatments. It really depends on the amount of fat you'd like to treat. You can maintain your results indefinitely by keeping a steady weight and adhering to a healthy lifestyle.
