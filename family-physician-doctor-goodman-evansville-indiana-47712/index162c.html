Can You Exercise After Botox or Will It Decrease the Results?
Exercise and Botox
Sep 5, 2018
It is best to wait at least 24 hours before exercising after Botox injections as it can increase the chance of bruising, swelling, and headaches. Also, exercising immediately after injections will cause the Botox to pull away from the muscles and not be as effective.

What Are the Post Treatment Instructions After Botox?
Botox Post Instructions
Sep 5, 2018
After Botox injections you will want to avoid excessive amounts of alcohol and sweating as this can increase the chance of bruising. Do not use a Clarisonic brush for 72 hours or lie down 4-6 after injections. By doing so, could take a chance of moving the Botox around.
Botox takes 2-3 days to start working, day 5 it does most of its work and in 7 days you'll receive the full results.
