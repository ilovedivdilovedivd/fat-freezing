Coolsculpt
Aug 19, 2017
Thanks for your question! The number of Coolsculpt applicators being used is dependent on your goals and areas of concerns. There actually is skill and even art involved with placing the correct applicator to the correct area. My long torso patients do need more applicators simply because there is more area to cover. Or perhaps they included the flanks as well. During my consultations, I place the templates on my patient's abdomen etc and show them exactly what I will be treating.
