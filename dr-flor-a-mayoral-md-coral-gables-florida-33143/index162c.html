Is Kybella permanent?
Kybella is permanent
Jul 27, 2015
Yes,

Please explain the results of Kybella. How many treatments is the ‘norm?’
Amount of material that needs to be injected depends on the size of the fat deposit
Jul 27, 2015
Kybella™
is a cytolytic medication (deoxycholic acid) that is injected to help improve
the appearance of moderate to severe fullness of the submental fat in adults,
and it helps improve the contour of the neck, and thus decrease the ‘double
chin’ some people have. The amount of material that needs to be injected
depends on the size of the fat deposit. In the studies, most patients needed
four to six treatments, and required less medication with subsequent
treatments.
