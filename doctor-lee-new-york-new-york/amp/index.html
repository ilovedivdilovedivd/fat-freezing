Is it safe to inject the piriformis?
Is it safe to inject the piriformis?
Apr 7, 2021
There are definitely ways to inject the piriformis that make it safer. Injecting into a deep plane in this area is a safer technique, as it avoids the blood vessels that are in that area. Injecting into this area can help rejuvenate the nasolabial fold area in a very natural looking way. Always important to find an injector that is board certified that you trust.

Crooked smile fixable with Botox?
Botox to help with uneven smile
Apr 7, 2021
Botox can be used to relax the DAO (depressor anguli oris) muscle which is the muscle the pulls the corner of the mouth downwards, which may help to alleviate some of that unevenness you are seeing. It’s not a guarantee that it will match up perfectly with the other side though. It’s always best to have a consultation in person to see if this is an option for you.
I just had kybella and want to schedule treatment for Botox and have threading. How long should I wait?
How long to wait after kybella before botox or threading
Apr 7, 2021
Hi! You should have no issues getting botox in the upper face or lip area after kybella and do not have space the treatment out if that is what you are referring to. Same thing goes for eyebrow threading. If you are referring to lower face PDO threads or botox in lower face, like the chin or in the DAO muscle (to help with downturned corners or the mouth) then I would wait about 2-3 months after the kybella settles.
