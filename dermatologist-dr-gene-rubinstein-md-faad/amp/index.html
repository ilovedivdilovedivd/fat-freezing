How Long Between Photo Facial Treatments?
4 to 6 weeks between PhotoFacial Treatments
Sep 2, 2014
It takes about 4 weeks for the cell turnover to complete on the skin of the face, so I find 4 weeks to be the ideal interval to allow the skin to recover but also contribute most effectively to the collagen remodeling process. In the future, a maintenance treatment once a year, ideally after the summer, is recommended to help maintain the results. In addition, skin care products that contribute to continuous cell turnover and light chemical peels are recommended as maintenance.
