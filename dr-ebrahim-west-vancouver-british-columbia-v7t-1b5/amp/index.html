Once I treat my breakout, what should I do to reduce the appearance of my acne scars?
acne scars
May 15, 2022
Acne scars in women of color with rich pigmented skin will respond to Microneedling with RF.
However, it depends upon the length of time you have had these scars.
Looks like they are quite mature and more than 10 years.
This makes them difficult to treat.
Other options are TCA peel for ice pick scars.
Subscision and saline or dermal filler in for rolled scars and fractional resurfacing with MN and RF for boxed scars.
You will require several treatments and several sessions.
good luck!

fillers
Feb 9, 2022
Hi there,
Typically Juvederm based fillers should have dissolved by now.
You may want to check your pictures taken at baseline 2 years ago and compare them to those now.
Comparing your face by taking selfies is not the right way to see if the filler is still present.
you need standardized before and after pictures preferable taken at the doctor’s office where the filler is done.
If it’s still present, you can ask the doctor to use hyaluronidase, an enzyme used to dissolve the fillers.
