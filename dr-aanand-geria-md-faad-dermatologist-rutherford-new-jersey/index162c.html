Which laser for large pores, textured skin, redness and fine lines?
Textured skin
Jul 19, 2020
Thanks for your question. I recommend a procedure called Laser Genesis. This is otherwise known as the "laser facial" and can help with enlarged pores, oiliness, lines, redness, and texture. It's one of the most popular procedures at my practice. Each treatment is done every 2-4 weeks for a total of 6 sessions. Best of all, there is no downtime with this laser. Best of luck to you!
Most effective treatment for scarring caused by cystic acne?
Acne scarring
Jul 19, 2020
Based on your skin color and type of acne scars, I recommend a series of microneedling treatments with PRP. This has proven to be safe and effective for acne scars in skin of color. Also there is minimal downtime with this procedure. Best of luck to you!
Can I successfully get filler after having an adverse reaction to Voluma?
Volume reaction
Jul 19, 2020
Granulomas unfortunately can rarely happen after Juvederm injections - only the Vycross products show this tendency such as Voluma, Vollure, and Volbella. I recommend Restylane or Revanesse Versa as an alternative. Best of luck to you.
Alternative treatments for melasma and rosacea?
Melasma
Jul 18, 2020
For resistant melasma, we sometimes compound a higher strength of hydroquinone (up to 12% with Kojic acid) which is usually effective. Lasers should generally be avoided since this often makes it worse. A series of Jessner's peels in the Fall may be of some benefit of as well. Make sure you're using SPF50+ physical sunscreen and a hat every day.
Which Nd:Yag Laser performs best for laser hair removal from dark skin? Fewest sessions? Least p
Nd:YAG laser
Jul 18, 2020
Cutera is generally known to have the best Nd:YAG technology. Another option to consider is a diode laser like the Alma Soprano Ice which should be safe in most skin of color except Type VI skin. Good luck!
I removed a mole and it left a black spot. How can I get rid of it without going to a doctor?
Mole removal
Jul 18, 2020
I recommend seeing a dermatologist for anything related to moles since you must first verify that the mole wasn't cancerous. Even if it's just pigmentation left behind, a prescription will be needed for a lightening cream. Good luck!
Can chin fillers make your chin wider or do they only increase the side projection?
chin filler
Jul 18, 2020
Filler can be used to make the chin wider without increasing the projection. Precise treatment is needed. The best fillers for this would be Restylane Lyft, Juvederm Volume, or Radiesse. Good luck!
How do I remove dark spots caused by a Fraxel Laser treatment five years ago?
post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation
Jul 18, 2020
If hydroquinone 4% isn't enough, then sometimes we need to compound into 6-12% to get better results. Also a series of Jessner's peels in the Fall (if it hasn't resolved) will be of benefit as well. Best of luck to you!
Can my Botox be corrected or will I have to wait till it wears off for my uneven brows?
Spock eyebrow
Jul 18, 2020
This is a common occurrence when injecting botox into the forehead. The fix is super easy and quick...usually just 1 unit on each side to bring the brows down. It can and should be fixed as soon as possible. No need to wait the full 2 weeks.
Laser hair removal for fine dark hair?
laser hair removal
Jul 11, 2020
As long as the hair has pigment, it can be removed by laser. I would recommend a diode or alexandrite laser in your situation done by an expert. Best of luck! 
Is it OK to use numbing cream before laser hair removal on dark skin in sensitive areas like bik
Laser hair removal
Jul 11, 2020
Some providers may use numbing cream but it should be avoided as much as possible especially during the first session because accurate pain feedback is crucial in determining whether your skin is getting burned by the laser or not. Good luck!  
What can be done to get rid of my breast augmentation scars? I have tried the sheets and creams
breast augmentation scar
Jul 11, 2020
If the scar is raised, a series of cortisone shots will first be needed to flatten it out. For the pigmentation, I recommend hydroquinone cream to be used for 2-3 months. Best of luck!  
Accutane and Oily Acne Prone skin?
Accutane
Jul 11, 2020
Accutane can be safely given in low doses such as 10mg twice a week to control oiliness on a long-term basis. The challenge is to find a dermatologist who will be willing to prescribe it in such an unorthodox manner since it requires lab monitoring. Best of luck! 
I have a burn scar from a hot hair styling tool. Would hydroquinone work for this or make it wor
Hyperpigmentation
Jul 11, 2020
This is post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH) rather than a true scar. Hydroquinone for 2-3 months is the treatment of choice. Sunscreen SPF 50+ is mandatory. Sometimes chemical peels are added if we don't get complete resolution with the hydroquinone. Laser is always the last choice when treating PIH because there's a risk of worsening especially in skin of color. Good luck
Skin tightening on chest. What sort of procedure would be suitable?
Skin laxity on chest
Jul 11, 2020
Hello, another option to consider is radiofrequency microneedling (eg Lutronic Genius) and/or injections of hyper-dilute Radiesse. These two treatments both stimulate collagen production by different mechanisms. A series of treatments are needed but they are usually well tolerated with minimal downtime. Good luck!
