Vaserlipo for calves
Mar 29, 2019
Hi Kellie,
Vaserlipo or any other type of liposuction can be carried out in the calves area to reduce the bulk but one has to first establish suitability in terms of actual amount of excessive fat that can be removed. A lot of the time is is simply musculo-skeletal bulk for which vaserlipo will not work. It is also a very difficult area to treat without causing visible lumps so make sure you go to a surgeon who is well experienced in this area.
