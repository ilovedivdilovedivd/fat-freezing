Is CoolSculpting effective?

The cryolipolysis procedure is not intended for the global removal of adipose tissue from the body. Coolsculpting is effective in small areas and is suitable only for the removal of soft fatty tissue. The most suitable places for the procedure are the abdomen, back and upper and lower thighs. Coolsculpting is not recommended for patients who want to get rid of fat from large areas of the body.

CoolSculpting Is A Great Procedure For Someone Who Simply Doesn’t Have The Time To Be Out Of Work For Even A Day

Separately, it is necessary to note that this procedure, cryolipolysis, is not a means of treating obesity. Although the destroyed cells of adipose tissue and removed from the body. Without properly changing their habits in nutrition and lifestyle, patients risk again to acquire remote areas of fat deposits.

Contraindications to Coolsculpting treatment

Like any procedure, coolsculpting has its contraindications:

  • a tumor, you can not affect the area of the body where there is a tumor;
  • pregnancy, as well as the initial 6-8 weeks of the lactation period, the period of menstruation;
  • cryolipolysis is contraindicated in diabetes and liver diseases;

Also, you can not resort to a procedure if you have hepatitis or leukemia, various gynecological disorders, Raynaud’s disease, or paroxysmal spasm of the blood vessels in the limbs.
