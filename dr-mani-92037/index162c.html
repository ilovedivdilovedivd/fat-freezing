Can I get lip fillers (0.5ml Juvederm) on antibiotics?
No
Jul 6, 2018
If there is an active infection any where in the body the use of filler is contraindicated.  I recommend waiting until you are completely off of antibiotics and cleared of infection to proceed with dermal fillers. 
How much will ThermiSmooth improve my eye bags?
Yes
Jul 6, 2018
In order for ThermiSmooth to work effectively you would need multiple treatments.  Thermage can be an effective alternative.  Of course none of these non-surgical treatments can guarantee one hundred percent removal of your condition.  
Will lip enhancement be more painful with subsequent treatments?
No
Jul 6, 2018
In my experience the number of treatments you do does not make a difference in terms of pain. A nerve block can be injected to make the injection process painless.
