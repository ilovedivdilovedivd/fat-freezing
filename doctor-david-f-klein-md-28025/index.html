Do I need implants or just a lift? Big breasts is not necessarily the goal but perky is. I want them
I want perky breasts!
Jan 14, 2019
Your pictures do show deflated breasts with ptosis (sagging).  Adding an implant to a breast lift will increase size by whatever size the implant is.  A lift without an implant usually has less of an upper pole fullness or roundness above the bra line.  A compromise may be a very small implant if you want this upper pole fullness.  A lift by itself will can make them "perky" but usually the upper pole remains flatter than with an implant.

Should I wear a bra for sleeping to help with gravity?
Wearing a bra to sleep?
Jan 14, 2019
Four years after BA you would expect to see some changes in the breasts.  Gravity never gives up!  Also, as our skin ages, it loses some of its elasticity.  My opinion is that wearing a bra to bed probably doesn't make a difference at this point if your breasts are average in size.  If they are extremely large, I would recommend a bra at all times, including for sleep.
