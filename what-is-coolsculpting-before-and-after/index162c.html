What Is Coolsculpting Before And After

CoolSculpting Treatment Details

The CoolSculpting procedure itself lasts for about an hour. The applicator is placed on the area of the body where the undesired bulge is. The most common areas that we treat in our practice are the love handles and abdomen.

The applicator has a cycle that runs for 60 minutes. That means that if we are doing two applications on the body, then the patient would be here for about two hours. Coolsculpting is not typically something that we recommend a regimen of in the same area.

We ask our patients to come in for their treatment, then come back in about three months to go over the results. If further reduction is desired, we would retreat the area at that time. (Jeffrey W. Hall, MD, Austin Plastic Surgeon)

The CoolSculpting Procedure Is Proven, Safe, And Effective

Cool Sculpting by Zeltiq destroys fat cells so the effect is long lasting.

Coolsculpting is permanent fat removal

Coolsculpting makes use of the phenomenon known as cryolipolysis and permanently removes fat from bulges such as love handles and abdomen. It is safe, noninvasive and effective. (Steven Hacker, MD, West Palm Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

On average each treatment lasts 1 hour. To treat the abdomen area you may need between 1 – 4 cycles.

Also depending on the amount of fat and sculpting that needs to be completed for maximum results could be between 1 – 3 treatments. (Eric P. Bachelor, MD, Pleasanton Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting permanently reduces the number of fat cells present in your body

CoolSculpting utilizes Cryolipolysis, a technique using extreme cold temperatures to destroy the layers of fat cells. The surrounding, healthy tissues are unaffected during the treatment, remaining completely safe. Over the weeks following a CoolSculpting procedure, the body will naturally metabolize the lifeless cells.

A 25-35% reduction in fatty tissue can be seen once the area has completely healed. Generally you would come in for a consultation in order to undergo a 360 degree evaluation and your provider will be able to make a quote for treatment to transformation.

After your consultation if you decide to move forward you would be able to schedule the treatment in the next few days. 30 days after the first treatment we generally retreat the areas that you decide to move forward with at a significant discount to you in order to ensure that you get the best possible result.

The full result can generally be seen around 3 months after the first treatment. The results last a lifetime as the total number of fat cells in the body is being reduced in a strategic way to create a sculpted look. (Paul Vitenas, Jr., MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)
