Shanthala Shivananjappa, MD

Name: Shanthala Shivananjappa, MD
Statement: Dr. Shanthala Shivananjappa, MD is a board-certified anesthesiologist with 20 years of experience. Dr. Shivananjappa is a board-certified, Harvard-trained anesthesiologist, assistant professor at Yale University, and staff at Massachusetts General Hospital prior to opening her cosmetic medicine clinic.
Her extensive experience working with plastic surgical patients in the operating room since 1994 and paired with years of training in non-surgical cosmetic procedures, has enabled Dr. Shanthala to have a unique perspective on Anti-aging treatments.
The owner of Shanthala MD MedSpa & Laser Center in Peabody, Massachusetts, she specializes in non-surgical cosmetic procedures such as Exosome therapy, PDO thread lift, Acne scar treatment, Ultherapy, RF micro needling, All types of Neuromodulators, including Botox, all types of dermal fillers such as RHA, JUVEDERM, VERSA, RADIESSE, RESTYLANE, nonsurgical facelift, skin tightening, vampire facelift, O-Shot, vaginal rejuvenation with CO2RE INTIMA ,P-Shot, and PRP injections for hair loss. Her extensive experience working with plastic surgical patients in the operating room, paired with years of training in non-surgical cosmetic procedures make her an expert in her field.

After receiving her MD at the Grant Government Medical College in Mumbai, India, she went on to complete an internship at the Yale New Haven Hospital, Saint Raphael Campus, and an anesthesiology residency at Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, a teaching hospital of Harvard Medical School, in Boston, Massachusetts. She served as an Assistant professor at Yale New Haven hospital.

She is a member of the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine and the American Society for Laser Medicine and Surgery.
