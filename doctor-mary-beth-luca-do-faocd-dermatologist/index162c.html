Slight swelling and discomfort at procerus after Botox.
Swelling or Discomfort Following Botox
Jul 29, 2015
Because Botox is administered through an injection, it is possible to have swelling, discomfort, or potentially bruising following treatment. These mild side effects usually resolve within hours to a few days following treatment. If, however, you are still experiencing discomfort or have a worsening of symptoms, you should follow up with your administering physician.
Can I have ultherapy if I nave tooth implants?
Ultherapy and Dental Implants
Jun 3, 2015
Ultherapy treatments should not have any affect on your dental implants. It can be said, however, that you may experience a temporary increase in sensitivity when receiving the ultrasound directly over the implants. Be sure to notify your Ultherapist for the best possible treatment.
Is 170 lbs too heavy for CoolSculpting?
CoolSculpting and Weight
Jun 3, 2015
It would be unwise to make a treatment decision based solely on weight. There are several factors that are considered when determining if a person is a good candidate for CoolSculpting, including gender, treatment area, previous aesthetic and cosmetic treatments, and overall health status. It is advised that you schedule a consultation with a trained CoolSculpting provider to be assessed for the best treatment plan.
