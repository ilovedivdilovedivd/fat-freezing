How Much Is One Treatment Of Coolsculpting?

CoolSculpting Cost

The cost of a single cycle of CoolSculpting can vary anywhere from $600-$750 per cycle. The majority of patients will require multiple treatments and usually there is discounted package pricing that brings the cost down significantly depending on how many cycles you will need for an optimal result.

While pricing may be an important factor, also keep in mind that the procedure is dependent upon technique as well.

Make sure you are treated by someone who is well qualified and knowledgeable about the procedure, and that can offer you alternative treatments if you are not a candidate for CoolSculpting or if another procedure would be more beneficial for you. (John Diaz, MD, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Is A Body Contouring Procedure You May Want To Consider

Coolsculpting Costs

The costs associated with coolscultping will vary siginificantly based upon the size of the area to be treated, the skill of the practioner, and your location. We charge approximately $750-900 per cycle in our office in Southlake, Texas.

I would emphasize that not all coolsculpting results are the same. I believe that it is critical that your coolsculpting is performed under the medical direction of a board certified plastic surgeon who can contribute his/her aesthetic vision to the procedure, and who is experienced to know if coolsculpting, liposuction or tummy tuck is the best procedure for you. (Sacha Obaid, MD, Dallas Plastic Surgeon)

The Price of Coolsculpting in Albany NY

Most pricing structures for coolsculpting are based on the number of applicators and type of applicator with the small applicators nationally in the $750 for small applicator and $1250 for the larger applicator, and then discounts are applied with the number of cycles applied.

However, I am not convinced this is the best way to structure pricing as you will be dissatisfied with the results unless there is a sense of treating to completion of an area.

One can see excellent results as in the company promotional materials when costs are no option.

In our practice we want people to be successful with the treatment by careful selection, honest appraisal and fair pricing.

It is my judgment that the cost of Coolsculpting should be less than a comparable treatment with liposuction which is a far more complex and skill driven procedure.

if you are getting quotes that approach this range, than you may be an inappropriate candidate or should look for another facility who can put this in a better perspective for you. (Arthur N. Falk, MD, Albany Facial Plastic Surgeon)
