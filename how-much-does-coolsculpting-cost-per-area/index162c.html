How Much Does Coolsculpting Cost Per Area?

Pricing for coolsculpting depends on the area. Small applicator is $750 per area per session.

So lower abdomen could be 2 small areas or 1 large area. A large applicator is $1500 per area.

Double chin treatment is $1000 per treatment. Outer thigh is $1500 per treatment per area.

Most clients require anywhere from 1 to 3 sessions depending on how much fat they have.

I recommend clients to come in for a complimentary consultation to determine how many areas and treatments will be required. (Hardik Soni, MD (not currently practicing), Summit Emergency Medicine Physician)

Generally, the cost of cool sculpting depends on the area(s) and the number of cycles you purchase. A cycle is defined as one freeze/thaw session.

The number of cycles needed depends on the number of areas to be treated, the size of the areas and the number of treatments to each area–we recommend a minimum of two cycles for each area.

The cost for a single cycle is usually $750, but is less if more cycles are purchased as a package. (Jeffrey D. Wagner, MD, Indianapolis Plastic Surgeon)
