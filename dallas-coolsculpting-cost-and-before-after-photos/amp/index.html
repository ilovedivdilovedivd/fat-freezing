Cryolipolysis is getting rid of fat deposits with the help of cold. In 2008, a method was invented to eliminate fatty tissue without traumatizing the skin – non-surgical liposuction using low temperatures, called coolsculpting.

The fat burning process is based on prolonged exposure to the subcutaneous lipid layer by cold. The destruction of fat with the help of cold seems unreal, but it is a physiologically natural process scientifically confirmed by R. Anderson and D. Manstein, who became the developers of the method.

By the way, the development of Harvard scientists became the foundation for the invention of the device for cryolipolysis – Coolsculpting.

How it works?

So arranged by nature – fat cells react to the cold much faster than other somatic cells. All that we call “fatty tissue” is solid triglycerides, i.e. fats, and mitochondria, which provide energy to the vital activity of the cell, is very small. Therefore, at low temperatures, energy in cells quickly runs out and they become unviable and die off.

This process is called apoptosis. Apoptosis in this case is selective, because only fat cells die from cold. Dermal and muscle cells remain intact, because the device works with the fat fold in isolation. The regulation and adjustment of the optimum temperature is very important, since the safety of the procedure depends on this factor. The doctor should not only make the session effective, but also avoid supercooling of the skin. Due to cell death, the apoptotic bodies into which the cell itself decays are absorbed by the phagocytes and removed from the treated area into the lymph. This process is gradual, therefore excessive intoxication of the body with the products of the decomposition of adipose tissue is excluded.

The result of the procedure appears after a 3-4-month course. Coolsculpting can reduce fat fold by 40%. On the one hand, it comes from the reduction of the cells themselves, on the other – because of their active decay. Fatty tissue is burned gradually, so that the physiological naturalness of the result is achieved. From the outside, this manifests itself in the elasticity of the skin in the area of cryolipolysis.

How is coolsculpting performed?

Coolsculpting, like all serious cosmetic procedures, should begin with a doctor’s examination. He must confirm the patient’s readiness for the procedure, make sure there are no contraindications. Then a zone is planned for which the cryolipolysis procedure will be carried out.

There are no restrictions – coolsculpting is carried out on the hands, legs, back, abdomen, buttocks. At the same time, working with any area of the body is equally comfortable, because there are 6 nozzles of different shapes and sizes, from which you can choose the most convenient. The doctor then measures the fat fold, and marks the cryolipolysis zone with a special marker.

Skin surface treatment is necessary. The working area is wiped with a special napkin.

Then the very procedure of cryolipolysis itself starts – the skin with the fat fold is seized by the vacuum cup, where it stays in close contact with the cooling applicator. Usually the temperature of the applicator does not exceed -5 -80C. Gradual decay of fatty fibers begins.

Patients’ feelings

Reviews about the procedure are different, but almost all of them claim discomfort in the area in which cryolipolysis takes place. Usually, after 10 minutes, discomfort disappears and the patient can do something, for example, read a book.

The procedure of cryolipolysis is not accompanied by a tangible discomfort. Perhaps a slight sense of coldness (because only negative fat cells react to minus temperature).

Massage of the treated area is performed to prevent strong swelling, but minor swelling and redness still remain.

Number of sessions

The parts of our body are subject to correction by cryolipolysis with different efficacy. The number of sessions also depends on the individual characteristics of the body and the structure of adipose tissue. For example, coolsculpting of the abdomen produces a result after 10 or more procedures, while for the legs they need less.

Coolsculpting has contraindications, despite the positive image in cosmetology. Coolsculpting can not be performed in pregnant and lactating women, patients with external manifestations of skin diseases in cryolipolysis zones. Contraindicated method of weight loss for patients with hernia, intolerance to cold and severe heart problems. In other cases, there are no restrictions, or they are identified by the doctor when examined on an individual basis.

Among the side effects often there is a slight numbness of the treated area, its redness and puffiness. Symptoms are short-lived and manifest immediately after the procedure. The advantages and disadvantages of the method

Cryolipolysis does not cause fear in patients, and positive feedback allows you to determine the high effectiveness of the method in combating obesity.
Atraumatism, absence of scars, no need for surgical or injection intervention in structural layers of the skin to destroy the lipid layer.

Recently, the tendency to carry out cryolipolysis at home has become popular. This is available thanks to compact equipment that is easy to transport. Therefore, you can lose weight with cryolipolysis at home.

However, there are small disadvantages, which need to be prepared. In order not to wait for miracles and not to be disappointed afterwards.

For example:

  • Coolsculpting does not trigger the mechanisms of weight loss in the body. It acts locally, only on the treated area, and only during the procedure!
  • High cost of the procedure.
  • The method is effective only with elastic skin.
  • Difficulties during treated small areas.
  • Inefficiency in obesity.

Nothing is perfect and never will be. Therefore, among the dozens of methods of getting rid of excess fat, you just have to choose what causes confidence, gives a good result. Non-surgical cryolipolysis with low temperatures has justified itself in this regard. But it is better to first carry out cryoliposuction in the conditions of the cabin before buying expensive equipment without making sure of its safety.
