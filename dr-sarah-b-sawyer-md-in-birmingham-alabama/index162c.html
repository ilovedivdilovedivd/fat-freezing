I have a scar right beside my eye, when I smile the scar puffs up. Why does it puff up when I smile
scar near eye area
Jul 18, 2018
Hi there. That scar is located over a muscle called the orbicularis oris, which is a muscle that squeezes your eye closed. It moves like an accordion, so firmer tissue like a scar moves as a bulk instead of normal skin that would exhibit more elasticity.
For treatment, I think Botox or Dysport would help decrease muscle movement and “bunching”, and microneedling could help improve the texture of the scar. Hope this helps. Dr. Sawyer
