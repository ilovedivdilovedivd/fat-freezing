What advice would you give to achieve optimum, natural results with lip filler?
Natural Lip Filler
Jan 16, 2019
There are a variety of lip fillers available today, some are softer than others. Discuss with your provider the difference between Restylane Refyne, Restylane and Juvederm. It is usually beneficial to place just a small amout in the larger lip so it has a similar "freshened up" appearance to the upper lip, which in your case, would require more.
Is Ultherapy cleared for full neck or submental area on the neck?
Ultherapy for neck
Jan 16, 2019
Ultherapy is cleared for lower face, submental region and neck. It is used to tighten skin laxity around the jowl area and can improve the appearance of the whole neck as well. Not everybody is a perfect candidate for Ultherapy so be sure to get a consultation before you move forward.
Price of injectables to remove slight wrinkles?
Injectables for brow lift
Jan 16, 2019
Botox/Dysport can be used to soften wrinkles and give a slight brow lift simultaneously. Neuromodulators work best for the upper face, including crow's feet, the "11's" between the brows and horizontal forehead creases. Cost for each area in the Northeast is $15 unit, which can mean about $300 per area to start.
