Principle of treatment

Coolsculpting is a method of cold correction of the body with the help of Zeltiq equipment, which is based on the cooling of fatty tissue.

The device Zeltiq is designed to remove excess fat deposits in those areas where it is possible to capture the folds.

Procedure

In the course of a 60-minute session, the treated area is protected by gel pads, on top of which a vacuum applicator is applied, that drawing the the fat fold.

Due to uniform cooling of tissues, fat cells are destroyed, which are subsequently removed from the body naturally.

  • Non-invasive procedure

  • Controlled impact
  • Painless effect
  • Lack of recovery period

Indications

Removal of excess body fat:

  1. on the arms, back and side of the shoulders
  2. on the back, especially under the shoulder blades
  3. in the zone of the riding breeches (outer surface of the thighs)
  4. on the knees
  5. at the waist and abdomen.

Contraindications

  • pregnancy and lactation
  • presence of pacemaker
  • skin diseases in the treatment area
  • hernia
  • cold intolerance.

Feature

If the patient subsequently gaining weight, then in the area of body treatment with coolsculpting, the volumes will still remain small.

Important! Some of the fat cells die after cryolipolysis. Their total amount in the affected area is significantly reduced. No technology or diet can achieve this result, since their action is aimed at reducing the volume of fat cells, rather than their number.

How does cryolipolysis work?

The fact is that when weight is lost with the help of sports and diets, the volume of fat cells decreases, and in violation of sport and nutrition they can again increase in volume.

Under the influence of cold in the fat cells, the physiological process of death begins. The main thing is that only in fat – the remaining cells are not affected, this is an important feature of the technology. Fat cells die without damaging the cell membrane, the body takes them naturally. In the future, even if the weight returns, the option of adipose tissue to growth in the treated area remains sufficiently reduced.

The patient receives a harmonious, harmonious body, free from excess weight and tuberous surfaces. Cryolipolysis was repeatedly recognized by the magazine “NewBeauty” as the best method for treating fat deposits.
