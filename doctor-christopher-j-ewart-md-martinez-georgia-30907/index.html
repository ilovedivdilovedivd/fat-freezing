How Long Does Tummy Tuck Surgery Swelling Last?
Swelling after tummy tuck
May 30, 2019
Tummy tuck swelling can most likely be seen or located in the lower abdomen and/or the mons area post-operative. A majority of the swelling can last up to 6-12 weeks. To help alleviate swelling, you can rest, elevate lower extremities, and ice areas. Keep in mind that it can take up to 6-12 months for best results and all swelling to be resolved.
Will a tummy tuck get rid of pregnancy stretch marks?
Strech marks after pregnancy
May 30, 2019
A tummy tuck will remove the stretch marks that are located from the belly button down to the pubic area and move the ones above the belly button typically down to below the belly button. You would be a great candidate for a tummy tuck combined with liposuction to obtain best results.
