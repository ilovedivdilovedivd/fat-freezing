Does Artefill/Bellafill Migrate?

The short answer is no. In order for a substance to move in the body phagocytosis has to occur. Phagocytosis occurs when the body's white blood cells engulf the foreign substance. The PMMA in Bellafill is greater than 40 µm so the microspheres become encapsulated in the dermis. This is the reason it makes the surface of the skin look better and why it is so long lasting.
