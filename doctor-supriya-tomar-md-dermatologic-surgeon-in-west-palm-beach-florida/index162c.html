My Latisse expired in 2016. Is it OK to use?
Using Latisse after expiration
Jan 13, 2021
While there isn't evidence that expired latisse can cause any adverse side effects due to the expiration, I would still recommend you purchase new product. The main concern would be loss of efficacy, however, there is a very slim possibility that it might still effectively grow lashes. Best to err on the side of caution and purchase new product, and I would avoid buying it in bulk unless you use a lot of it or go through it more quickly than usual. Best of luck!

Scar on jawline. What options do I have?

Nov 10, 2020
Since you have a combination of atrophic and hypertrophic scarring, the first step would be to address the keloids with steroid injections to calm the inflammation.
