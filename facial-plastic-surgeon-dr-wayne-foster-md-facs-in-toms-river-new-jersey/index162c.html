Is it safe to get facial Botox?
Botox after surgery
Apr 20, 2015
Botox Cosmetic is safe after surgery as long as there are no neurological and allergic contraindications. In general, Botox may be given before, after, and sometimes during anesthesia for surgery. Although the discomfort from injections is minimal, it is completely avoidable by combining with other surgical procedures requiring sedation.
Am I a good candidate for CoolSculpting? If so, how many sessions would I need?
If you can pinch an inch....
Mar 26, 2015
If you can pinch an inch you are a candidate for CoolSculpting! Not all patients experience pain. The pain they are experiencing is called neuropathic pain. Nerve fibers may be temporarily stimulated by the extreme cold. You should be given a prescription for a pain medication specifically for neuropathic pain. Patients typically have pain 3-7 days after treatment and it resolves in a few days.
