With today’s fast paced life many people find it difficult to juggle their numerous daily activities, families and careers and may not find enough time to focus on their bodies.

Also as we grow older our bodies change and it becomes more difficult to keep it in shape.

While many people may not afford to hire personal trainers to help them keep their shape freezing fat cells may be a solution to their problem.

Zeltic Coolsculpting is a unique procedure that uses an advanced cooling technology for freezing fat off.

This procedure was discovered by two dermatologists Rox Anderson and Dieter Manstein both from Massachusetts General Hospital Wellman Center for Photomedicine.

The two dermatologists discovered that naturally subcutaneous fat cells are more affected by cooling than other surrounding tissues.

Published in November 2008 in lasers in surgery and medicine, their initial evidence come to the conclusion that prolonged and controlled cooling of local tissue induces selective reduction of fat cells and therefore, the loss of subcutaneous fat without any damage to the overlying skin.

The discovery was named selective cryolysis and led to the development of a patented technology which is behind zeltiq cool sculpting.

How does this fat freezing procedure work?

Zeltiq Coolsculpting procedure uses a non invasive cooling of fat cells to induce the breakdown of the fat cells without damaging other tissues.

Using this procedure the fat in the treated area can be reduced by twenty to forty percent. The results of this procedure are mostly visible after two to four months.

The procedure uses a non invasive applicator to deliver controlled cooling that targets fat cells. The area is then suctioned using two cooling plates and the temperature of the fat cells is reduced to just above freezing, this causes the fat cells to collapse and die.

CoolSculpting Is A 100% Non-surgical Fat Reduction Treatment

The exposure of fat cells to cooling during the procedure causes the body to release some chemicals which are naturally occurring and inflammatory mediators.

The chemicals and mediators then gradually digest the fat cells that have been exposed to cooling for two to three months. The lipids are then released and then transported through the lymphatic systems.

The first few minute after cooling goes to work the patient will feel a combination of pressure and intense cold but the feeling soon disappears and they are able to read or take a nap during the treatment.

Once the fat cells are eliminated from the body the results of the procedure last as long as one keeps fit through healthy eating and exercises to prevent fat from forming in those areas again.

Coolsculpting Selectively Freezes And Destroys Fat Cells

Although cool sculpting results are not immediately noticeable like those of liposuctions, people who wish to avoid the inconveniences associated with surgery such as; pain and recovery time will find this procedure to be a better option.

Who is a Candidate For Zeltiq Coolsculpting?

Cool sculpting is done to people who are relatively fit but find it hard to get rid of some fat bulges in specific areas of their bodies such as love handles, back fat, muffin tops and man boobs.

It is ideal for people who are seeking a spot reduction for a specific area but do not want to go through surgery. The cool sculpting is not meant for the purposes of weight loss but only for people who have specific areas of fat that they want to get rid of.

Advantages of Freezing Fat Over Other Procedures

Coolsculpting is ideal compared to other process such as focused ultrasounds, radiotherapy and lasers as it does not affect adjacent tissue. As the procedure is non invasive it means you can return to your daily activities immediately and you do not have to take sick off for you to recover.

Some patients may experience some redness, tingling, numbness, minor bruising or some discomfort but this is very temporally but most patients are able to return to work after the procedure is done. Coolsculpting is also ideal since you do not need to take any medication, pills or supplements. You also do not have to change your diet or adopt new exercise habits.

Going through the procedure may encourage the patient to gain more healthy habits to exercise more since they feel good about their new look.
