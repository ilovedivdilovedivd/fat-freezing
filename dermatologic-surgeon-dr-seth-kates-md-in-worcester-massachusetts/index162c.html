Can a shaved mole come back years later?
Can a mole recur
Aug 11, 2019
The simple answer is yes.  When a mole is shaved off, only the portion above the skin is removed.  The part that is left below skin level can recur even years later and the recurrence generally has nothing to do with sun exposure.  You can leave it alone, have it reshaved, or even excise it (have it cut out).
