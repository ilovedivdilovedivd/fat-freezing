No!
Apr 6, 2022
Your skin tone is not too tan for IPL skin rejuvenation. All skin types are treated cautiously, and being tanned means we have to treat your skin accordingly. Will Botox smooth a wrinkly smile around eyes?
Yes!
Apr 5, 2022
Botox, and other wrinkle relaxers are injected into targeted muscles, to ultimately soften the movement of this muscle, resulting in less lines, less movement, and a more youthful appearance. The orbicularis oculi is the muscle that is injected when trying to relax, soften, or remove smile lines, and "scrunching" around the eye.

From the information given, I believe you would be a good candidate to trial wrinkle relaxers in the "crows feet", though a proper consultation before treatment...
