Is there anything to help with the discomfort and swelling?
Swelling after Coolsculp
Aug 29, 2017
Swelling and discomfort can occur after Coolsculpting and the neck area is extremely sensitive. I would recommend an over-the-counter anti-inflammatory such as Tylenol or Ibuprofen taken on a regular basis (always take with food, and STOP for any GI upset or bloody stools).
If the swelling and pain persists or worsens, please contact your Coolsculpting provider.
