Can lip filler be removed
Oct 30, 2018
Hyaluronic acid fillers such as Juvederm, Volbella or Restylane can be dissolved. You will want to see a physician that is familiar with using hylenex, wydase and such. Too much can dissolve some of your natural fullness creating divots.
