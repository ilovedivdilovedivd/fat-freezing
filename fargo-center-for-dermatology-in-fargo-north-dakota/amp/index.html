Should my practitioner reuse my Vivace tip on me at subsequent visits? Same tip different treatment day
Vivace Tip Usage
Dec 15, 2021
We do not reuse our Vivace tips and dispose of them after each treatment. We want to ensure the needles are sterile and sharp for each treatment. We will, however, continue to use the tip if treating more than one area on a patient in the same treatment session.
Can Kybella be used to treat excess fat on mons pubis?
Kybella treatment areas.
Dec 14, 2021
Kybella is only FDA approved to treat submental fat (fat beneath the chin). The area that you are interested in treating would not be FDA approved.
