Skin tightening on thighs with IUD
Oct 5, 2023
Hello and thank you for your question! Unfortunately, the copper IUD does exclude you from skin tightening procedures with radio frequency energy like Bodytite or Morpheus8. Your uterus, where an IUD is placed, is very close to the treatment area, specifically upper and inner thighs. My advice would be to have an in-person consultation with a provider that offer skin tightening procedures so you can get all your questions answered. Good luck!
