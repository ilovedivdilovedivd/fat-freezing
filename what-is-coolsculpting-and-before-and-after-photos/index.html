What is the CoolsculptingÂ® procedure?

The patient, wishing to improve the body shape, turns to the clinic (beauty salon), equipped with the device CoolsculptingÂ®.

The cosmetologist consults, examines and probes the patient for consultation, outlining the areas of influence and indications for the appointment of the cryolipolysis procedure.

The ideal candidate for CoolsculptingÂ® has well-defined fat folds, normal body weight, regular exercise and nutrition. Correction of cryolipolysis is accompanied by fat folds on the abdomen, lateral areas of the waist, back, buttocks, hips.

Depending on the size and direction of the fold, assigned to a decrease, the cosmetologist can use one of five applicators, differing in size and shape. Regardless of the selected applicator, the procedure on one fold will last 60 minutes. First, the applicator applied to the skin under the influence of vacuum captures the fat fold in its bowl, then the doctor turns on the cooling.

In the first 5-10 minutes, a slight sensation of pressure and cold is possible, followed by numbness in the treated area and the procedure becomes comfortable, anesthesia is not required. Within an hour, while the procedure lasts, the patient can read, work on a laptop, communicate with a cosmetologist or just nap.

The apparatus for carrying out the cryolipolysis procedure CoolsculptingÂ®, equipped with a multi-stage protection system, carries out constant cooling control inside the applicator bowl and conducts an intensive massage of the skin surface at the end of the hour procedure. At the end of the procedure, the applicator is removed from the fold, a slight reddening and compaction of the treated area disappears after a few minutes.

The patient can start his usual activity immediately after the procedure, the recovery period lasts no more than half an hour, and medical rehabilitation is not required at all. There are few contraindications to cryolipolysis.

The main ones are pregnancy and lactation, as well as intolerance to cold of any origin and manifestation.

Usually the procedure is painless, leaving for the next weeks a feeling of numbness or tingling in the treated area. Rarely, stronger sensations are possible, which in a short time either pass by themselves or are facilitated by drugs prescribed by the attending physician. The process of reducing the thickness of the treated fat fold begins from the first week after the procedure and will continue for the next 1-1.5 months. On average, the fat folds on the abdomen, sides or back are reduced after one procedure by 25%. A month later, a cosmetologist receives an evaluation of the result and, if desired, improves the effect, you can perform a second procedure on the same fold.

Recommendations for the period after the cryolipolysis procedure are simple: to eat right and do not forget about physical activities.
