The accumulation of fat in the upper arm (the so-called bat wing) is a matter of concern for many women.

CoolSculpting uses the natural vulnerability of fat cells to cold. When fat cells are exposed to very low temperatures for long periods of time, the natural process of fat cell destruction begins, called apoptosis.

Gradually, within one to three months, the fat cells “self-destruct” and gently removed. And once the fat cells are destroyed, they disappear forever.

Cryolipolysis is a modern high-tech procedure for correcting the figure and eliminating fat deposits without surgery. This is a unique procedure for cooling the subcutaneous fat layer with a special vacuum nozzle, followed by the destruction of fat cells.

MECHANISM OF ACTION

Fat Freezing Bat Wings Under Arms

The handlebars unit has a special recess, where the skin folds and subcutaneous fat gets under vacuum action. On both sides of the skin fold to be directed cooling elements that reduce the tissue temperature to -5 ° C.

At this temperature, the cells of subcutaneous adipose tissue are destroyed. Splitting fatty tissue under the influence of cold after the procedure occurs gradually, within 2-4 months. The first results you will see after 2-4 weeks. Destroyed cells are removed from the body naturally through the bloodstream to the liver.

PREPARATION:

Bye Bye, Batwings

Before the beginning of the procedure, a medical consultation is conducted for the presence or absence of contraindications.

The cosmetologist determines the areas of the body that need correction, the thickness of the lipid layer is measured. Then, for the sake of clarity, the volume measurements are carried out. A special sliding gel or film is applied to the treatment zone.

Vacuum nozzle, gently draws a skin area with subcutaneous tissue. Cooling begins, which lasts about an hour.

At the very beginning of the procedure, the patient may feel a slight cold at the location of the nozzle, then these sensations disappear.

On average, one procedure lasts about 40-50 minutes. The duration of the full course is determined by the doctor, based on the characteristics of the patient’s body. After the end, the nozzle is removed from the skin, the film or gel remnants are removed. The client can continue his normal life without restrictions.

The procedure is absolutely safe and harmless for internal organs and tissues, the skin areas being worked are protected by gel, and full restoration of color and temperature occurs on the same day. It is better to take an interesting book or laptop with you, because the procedure is long, especially if several areas are to be processed sequentially.
