How effective is CooSculpting if BMI is over 35?
CoolSculpting with BMI 35
May 24, 2019
Hello! If you are seeking a large area removal of fat, CoolSculpting would not be the best option for you. CoolSculpting is most effective for those who are near their goal weight and have areas that are hard to exercise or stubborn to lose such as bra fat or love handles. I would suggest you make an appointment with a board certified plastic surgeon in your area for a comprehensive discussion of your options and goals. Best of luck on your journey!
