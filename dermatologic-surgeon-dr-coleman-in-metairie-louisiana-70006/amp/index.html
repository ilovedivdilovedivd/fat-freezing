Can filler come out of a burst lip?
Fillers can not leak out of a lip cold sore
Mar 13, 2018
Fillers can not leak out of a lip cold sore. The cold sore blisters are very superficial. The filler is deeply dispersed in the lip tissues and can only be removed by dissolving it.
