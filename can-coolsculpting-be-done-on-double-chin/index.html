Can Coolsculpting Be Done On Double Chin?

CoolMini is effective in reducing fat under the chin

The CoolMini has been successful in my practice in reducing the fat under the chin. Results are improved whenever the skin tone is good–hence, an in person examination is important in setting expectations before proceeding.

Typical treatment takes an hour. Advanced techniques can treat the sides with a slight overlap in the center. Be sure to seek a provider that has undergone the advanced training from Coolsculpting University. Other options to liposuction include Kybella injections and the Profound radiofrequency microneedling device. (Vaishali B. Doolabh, MD, FACS, Jacksonville Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting with the CoolMini can be an effective way to reduce unwanted submental fat and improve your profile. When treating the âdouble chin,â the CoolMini offers a relatively painless and minimally invasive option with results achieved in 1-2 treatments.

CoolMini Freeze Away Your Double Chin

One benefit of choosing Coolsculpting is the limited down time. After a treatment, you can expect to be pink or red for about 20 min with mild swelling present for hours to days. Many patients return to work the day after treatment.

The limitation of Coolsculpting happens to be the size of the applicator. The CoolMini attachment is only available in one size, so depending on one’s neck anatomy, the CoolMini may or may not be a good fit.

In addition to CoolSculpting and liposuction, Kybella offers another option. Kybella is the first and only injectable product for treatment of submental fullness.

Kybella works by destroying a portion of the excess fat cells in the neck, allowing us to create an improved contour.

Most patients can expect an improved chin profile in 2-4 treatment sessions spaced about a month apart. The benefit of Kybella is how customizable it is. The injector can map out a treatment area to fit your specific anatomy/needs.

CoolSculpting Essentially Freezes Stubborn Fat Cells

The down side of Kybella can be the post-treatment swelling. Many patients develop significant swelling and require a few days before being considered socially presentable.

There are precautions we take in our practice to reduce swelling, including pre- and post-treatment ice, the injection technique, and the wearing of an elastic chin/neck wrap after treatment.

Liposuction of the neck, Kybella, and Coolsculpting can all achieve good results. It would be beneficial to consult with a board certified facial plastic surgeon or plastic surgeon offering all three treatments, to determine the optimal treatment for you. (Umang Mehta, MD, Bay Area Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Does CoolMini Work?

CoolMini absolutely works to reduce double chin fat, and most people with realistic expectations are pleased with the treatment. However, 2-3 treatments are needed and it does not really improve any loose skin.

Treating Double Chins

CoolSculpting and Kybella are two safe non surgical alternatives to reducing double chins. Both are FDA cleared to treat submental (double chin) fat. At this point the jury is out as to which one is more efficacious so it becomes a matter of patient and physician preference.

Neck Fat Treatment Coolsculpting

The CoolMini applicator (CoolSculpting) was specifically designed to treat submental fat but is limited if the patient is too narrow and proper suction cannot be achieved. This is necessary for the fat to be drawn up inside the applicator.

Kybella is a medication that must be injected properly in order to avoid any temporary nerve damage but if the proper injection pattern is followed this is avoidable.

In my experience Kybella does produce somewhat more swelling and discomfort post treatment than CoolSculpting but not enough that would cause me to always recommend CoolSculpting over Kybella. I believe you would benefit from either procedure. (Ted Brezel, MD, Long Island Dermatologic Surgeon)
