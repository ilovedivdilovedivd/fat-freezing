Is the level of swelling below normal after prp+filler injection and should I be so swollen direct
Swelling after PRP
Jun 23, 2019
Thank you for your question and pictures.
It is very normal to see swelling immediately after PRP injections around the eyes. It can take up to 2 weeks to completely settle.
I would suggest continuing to ice the area, use a longer acting antihistamine such as Certrizine and sleep with your head slightly elevated.
If the swelling persists beyond the 2 weeks you should see your practitioner for re-evaluation.
Good luck.
