Will getting botox and fillers (masseter botox) help me achieve the v jawline?
Jaw treatment
Oct 29, 2018
Thank you for your question. Botox in the jaw can work very effectively for jawline slimming if the cause is from an enlarged masseter muscle. This can be tested by feeling the muscle when you clench your teeth together. If your muscle is not enlarged it is unlikely to make a difference.
A face to face consultation will allow the practitioner to make a full assessment and advise what would work for you.
All the best
Is this normal bruising after lip injections?
Bruising after treatment
Oct 29, 2018
Thank you for your question. Bruising after lip fillers is extremely common. I would always advise patients to avoid alcohol the day before the procedure and following the procedure for a couple of days. Avoid medications such as anti inflammatory medications (Nurofen). Cold compress for 48 hours may help and then swap to warm compress. You could also use arnica which will help with the bruising. If you have any concerns then see your medical practitioner for a face to face review…
