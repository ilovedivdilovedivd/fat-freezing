How does it work?

Cryolipolysis is a patented technology based on an amazing scientific discovery: when the temperature in the fat cells is reduced locally, energy metabolism at the biochemical level is disrupted. As a result, cells die and are naturally removed from the body. The effect will be lifelong, as confirmed by independent clinical studies.
