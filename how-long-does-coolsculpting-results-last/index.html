How Long Does Coolsculpting Results Last?

CoolSculpting results last indefinitely. Number of treatments recommended are often more than 1.

CoolSculpting is destroying fat cells through cryolipolysis, and the affected cells will naturally be removed from your system over the course of weeks following each treatment.

During your consultation for CoolSculpting, the number of treatments recommended will be provided, and in most cases, multiple treatments are recommended for optimal results.

The results of each treatment should reduce the fatty layer 20-25%, and most commonly, 2-3 treatments are encouraged.

These treatments can be spaced as frequent as 4 weeks apart, to further speed the process and enjoy end results which happen over the course of 2-4 months.

The treatment itself takes one hour per application, and if more than one handpiece applicator is involved, it is one hour per applicator.

Are CoolSculpting Results Permanent

The fat cells that are destroyed by the treatment are permanently expelled from the body and will never return. This is identical to the effect of having the surgical fat removal procedure, liposuction.

CoolSculpting is not meant to be a remedy for weight loss or future weight gain. The remaining fat cells in the treated area can expand with lack of exercise and poor diet, which can cause your result to be compromised. (Vinay Aggarwal, MD, Los Angeles Physician)

CoolSculpting Results

Most CoolSculpting treatments take one hour for each area treated. The new CoolSmooth applicator requires a two hour treatment. This new applicator can target the outer thighs and abdomen, areas that may not have been treatable previously with CoolSculpting.

Due to the flat design of the new applicator, the area treated is not drawn away from the underlying tissues. These underlying tissues hold heat. Due to the proximity of the treated area to the warm underlying tissues, a two hour treatment is required to achieve the temperatures to induce results.

With each CoolSculpting treatment, you can expect a 20-25% fat reduction. Even better, this fat reduction is permanent! The cold temperature of CoolSculpting induces a programmed cell death, apoptosis, of the treated fat cells. Over the next two months, these fat cells are excreted from the body through the lymphatic and circulatory systems.
