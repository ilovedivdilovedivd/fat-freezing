Coolsculpting Reno Prices And Photos

Coolsculpting. Stages of execution, indications and result. This manipulation is a non-surgical way to remove fat cells by cooling them. The procedure takes no more than an hour, is carried out on an outpatient basis and does not require any preparation. The main disadvantage of cryolipolysis is its high cost.

The mechanism of action of coolsculpting. The pros and cons of the procedure, comparison with liposuction.

The principle of the procedure under consideration is that the fat cells (adipocytes) are very sensitive to low temperatures. Selective cooling of adipose tissue to -5, -6 C leads to the cleavage of adipocytes naturally. After self-destruction of fat cells, they are recycled and removed from the body with lymph flow.

This manipulation has a long-term effect: fat tissue decreases in volume for 3 months. Although immediately after the procedure, patients can see some changes: the number of adipocytes is reduced, on average, by 20%.

Coolsculpting is characterized by the following features:

The full effect can be seen 2-3 months after the manipulation. When performing the same standard liposuction, the result is visible immediately.

The procedure is performed without incisions, which means there will be no scars in the future. In addition, the elimination of fat surplus is done without the use of anesthesia: there is no harmful effect of medicinal products on the body.

Skin covers with cryolipolysis retain their integrity, due to which the body is quickly restored. After the session, the patient can almost immediately return to the habitual way of life. As for liposuction, there is a high probability of infection, and the recovery period takes a lot of time.

CoolSculpting Involves No Needles, Surgery, Or Downtime

This method allows you to reduce the volume in certain parts of the body, and not even throughout the body. People can choose in favor of this technique, when they just want to “polish” the body shape: if it is impossible to achieve this effect with massages and sports.

Coolsculpting is a very expensive treat, compared to other ways of treating obesity.

Indications and contraindications to Coolsculpting treatment

The technique is used for local weight loss and elimination of problem areas on the body.

The procedure often covers the following areas:

CoolSculpting Is Designed For Those Who Have Unwanted Fat In Targeted Areas

  • Lateral and anterior surfaces of the abdomen. Reduce the volumes in these areas is difficult because of hormones. Under the influence of cold, the energy resources of fat cells in such areas will be depleted.
  • Hips.
  • Knees.
  • Back.
  • The inner surface of the hands.

Also, this procedure is suitable for patients who do not have enough time for recovery. In one session, on average, you can get rid of up to 7 kg.

Cryolipolysis can not be prescribed if the following conditions exist:

  • Supersensitivity of skin to low temperatures.
  • Excess obesity.
  • Strongly stretched skin.
  • Small local areas: chin, cheeks, etc.
  • Pregnancy and lactation.
  • The presence of an electrocardiostimulator or metal implants.
  • Damage to the skin, scar changes, impaired blood circulation, as well as the presence of a hernia in the area of manipulation.
  • Raynaud’s disease.
  • Violations associated with blood coagulability, as well as taking medications that dilute blood.
  • Acceptance of some hormonal medications.
  • Mental disorders.

To implement the method of elimination of fat surpluses considered, the Zeltiq, Freezefat, MED340, CryoMini and other devices were developed. Most clinics today prefer to work with devices of American manufacturers Zeltiq. Unlike other devices, they are equipped with three nozzles: small, medium and large. The remaining instruments often have only a large nozzle in their arsenal.

The nozzle is used more for working with large areas in which a large amount of fat deposits is concentrated. These areas include the stomach, back, thigh area, etc. A small nozzle helps to cope with surplus fatty tissue, which is located in hard-to-reach places: the inside of the hands, thighs.

Adipocytes die, and the body itself gets rid of the decay products.

Stages of cryolipolysis. How Is Coolsculpting Performed?

If a decision is made to perform this manipulation, the patient should undergo all necessary examinations to exclude contraindications.

Women who want to reduce the volume in the abdomen should calculate the timing of the procedure not during menstruation.

An hour before coolsculpting is not recommended to eat food and water. Since 1 session takes about an hour, and all this time the patient will be conscious, you should take a book or laptop.

Stages of conducting:

  • The patient is laid on the couch.
  • The gel is applied to the affected area, or a disposable helium napkin is applied.
  • This is necessary to protect the skin from frostbite and to evenly capture it in the nozzle of the device.
  • Installation of a nozzle on a problem site.
  • The pad placed under the nozzle with the aim of a more reliable fixation.
  • Absorption of fat folds into the nozzle by means of vacuum. The device independently sets the correct temperature regime. For the first few minutes, the patient may feel discomfort in the form of tingling in the area of exposure.
  • Removal of the nozzle after 1 hour, elimination of the gel.
  • The procedure takes place on an outpatient basis. Upon its completion, the patient can return to the habitual way of life.
