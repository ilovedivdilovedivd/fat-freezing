How Are Fat Cells Eliminated After Coolsculpting?
Fat disappears by the process of natural elimination

Coolsculpting’s freezing process crystalizes 20-25% of the fat cells in the treated area. This crystallization does in fact kill the fat cells.

The dead cells are slowly eliminated through the lymphatic system. Since they are not living/viable cells, reabsorption is of no concern and the fat will not “relocate” to other areas of the body. (Gregory Turowski, MD, PhD, FACS, Chicago Plastic Surgeon)

How CoolSculpting Works

CoolSculpting utilizes Cryolipolysis, a technique using extreme cold temperatures to destroy the layers of fat cells.

The surrounding, healthy tissues are unaffected during the treatment, remaining completely safe. Over the weeks following a CoolSculpting procedure, the body will naturally metabolize the lifeless cells.

A CoolSculpting Session Is Performed By A Professional Physician

A 25-35% reduction in fatty tissue can be seen once the area has completely healed. CoolSculpting can be used to treat many different areas including the abdomen (Muffin Top), flanks (Love Handles), upper back (Bra Rolls), lower back, arms, and male breasts.

Three different size applicators are available, allowing each CoolSculpting treatment to precisely address the individual target area. (Paul Vitenas, Jr., MD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

What Happens To Fat Cells After CoolSculpting

Fat cells are permanently killed during the fat freezing of CoolSculpting. Once these fat cells are naturally killed, the cells of your immune system come and and clean up these dead fat cells.

They are transported into the blood stream and ultimately eliminated from your body through your urine.

There is no risk of these fat cells traveling to other parts of your body. (Michael A. Zadeh, MD, FACS, Sherman Oaks General Surgeon)

Coolsculpting mechanism of action

With CoolSculpting, Dead Fat Cells Are Gone for Good

CoolSculpting uses a patented technology called cryolipolysis, which means it doesn’t just freeze the fat, it actually breaks it down until – like Humpty Dumpty — it cannot be put back together again. First, the fat cell is frozen.

Once frozen, it begins to die (this is known as apoptosis). As the cell dies, its components break away and are absorbed through the lymph system, which then eliminates them from the body through normal excretion pathways.

The “fat” cannot be reabsorbed because it doesn’t exist as fat anymore. A board-certified dermatologist can guide you through the CoolSculpting process. (Melda A. Isaac, MD, Washington DC Dermatologic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Process

The CoolSculpting® procedure is a non-invasive procedure that is intended to change the appearance of the treatment area by delivering controlled cooling to target and destroy fat cells at the surface of the skin to break down fat cells that are just beneath the skin The treated fat cells are crystalized (frozen) body naturally processes the fat and eliminates these dead cells which are gone for good.

Results can be seen as early as 3 weeks but the optimal results are seen in between 1-3 months. (Jessica Glenn, MD, Howell Family Physician)
