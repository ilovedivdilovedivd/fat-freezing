Coolsculpting In Fresno CA: Photos And Prices

Therapeutic cosmetology allows you to quickly get rid of excess weight and correct the body shape at will.

Cryolipolysis is one of its popular directions, it is used to improve metabolism and reduce body fat without surgery.

What is cryolipolysis?

Cryolipolysis or cold modeling is the most modern cosmetic procedure for removing local fat deposits.

Its main advantage is complete painlessness. During the session, there is no need for anesthesia, rehabilitation period or other similar actions.

Areas of impact of Coolsculpting

Like pressotherapy, cryotherapy is a non-invasive procedure, i.e., it does not need to make any incisions and punctures for it. In modern therapeutic cosmetology, cold modeling is considered to be the most effective and safe technique by which it is possible to correct deposition in virtually all areas of the body.

Cryolipolysis Cocoon is carried out exclusively on the device Zeltiq, so making it at home will be very difficult. This device captures a certain area of the skin, which is affected by cold. Fat cells are compressed and destroyed by the action of low temperature, after which their residues are removed through the lymphatic system.

The procedure does not have any effect on the liver, since during the removal of toxins and fat residues, they fall immediately into the lymph, not affecting the blood. Results after the session are visible after a few weeks, because the processes of decomposition of fat cells have a cumulative effect. The Zeltiq devices, which is used for cryolipolysis, is equipped with several nozzles:

Small. It is applied to the face (neck and chin).

Medium. It is treated with more extensive areas, for example, the abdomen, sides or legs.

Big. Fat deposits on the back and in some cases on the abdomen are treated with a large nozzle.

But the location of fat deposits is not a key definition of the size of the nozzle. They are also selected according to their number. The more fatty deposits – the better it is to use a large nozzle.

How is cryolipolysis performed?

The cryolipolysis procedure is carried out using a disposable gel pad and liner.

Stages of carrying out coolsculpting treatment:

  • The liner is placed in the nozzle, and the gel pad is placed on the site where fat removal will be performed. Napkin with gel is needed to protect the epidermis from hypothermia, as well as for uniform treatment of the skin with a nozzle;
  • After the device is turned on and adjusted to the desired temperature and type of exposure. Next, the nozzle is placed on the treated area. Suction of the fat fold is rather slow, so the applicator must be carefully rolled over it;
  • On average, the session takes from 40 minutes (around the neck) to 2 hours (in the hip area). This depends on the area of influence and the size of the nozzle;
  • Some doctors during the session, fix the nozzle with a special pillow. This does not affect the effectiveness of the session, but significantly increases the comfort of doctors and patients. To fix the effect, LPG massage or mesotherapy can be performed additionally. These procedures will help to quickly remove the residues of fat cells and accelerate the destruction of their membranes. Repeat sessions are allowed no more than 1 time per week, best with an interval of 10 days.

After the procedure, some negative consequences are possible.

Side effects after coolsculpting treatment:

  • Edema in the treated area;
  • Redness of the skin, hypothermia, local fever;
  • Rarely are headaches.
  • You need to follow the doctor’s advice to consolidate the effect after the procedure.

Recommendations after the cryolipolysis procedure:

  • The first few hours does not recommend drinking. Excess fluid immediately settles in the “empty” areas from which the fat cells were removed;
  • The first day you can not take hot baths and showers. This can reduce the rate of decomposition of frozen cells and cause a low efficiency of the session;
  • Maximize physical activity. After coolsculpting treatment, the body is in a state of stress. Excessive stress can cause headaches and slow down metabolism;
  • The procedure has a cumulative effect. Therefore, distribute the sessions so that there is not a large amount of time between the procedures. Say, if you need to carry out 3 session of coolsculpting, then allocate for this month and divide it into equal parts.

Like ultrasonic cavitation, myostimulation and other similar procedures, cryolipolysis is prescribed to combat excess weight. But the peculiarity of the procedure is that it is used to eliminate local deposits, not common fat masses. But coolsculpting has some contraindications, which are worth remembering.

When it is impossible to carry out cryolipolysis:

  • Allergy to cold. Rare, but the main contraindication to the application of cold modeling. It manifests itself in the swelling and reddening of the zone affected by cold;
  • Damage to the skin in the treatment area. These are scratches, rashes, even birthmarks. By the way, with a large number of birthmarks on the body, cryoliposuction is also not recommended;
  • The presence of stimulants in the body.
  • A sharp temperature drop at a certain point may adversely affect their performance;
  • Syndrome of poor blood coagulability or a tendency to bruising. These are symptoms of problems with blood vessels and circulatory system;
  • Diseases of the lymphatic or excretory system, including diabetes;
  • In diseases of the hormonal system, in particular, if gynaecomastia or other similar disorders have been diagnosed;
  • It is not recommended to have a session during pregnancy, breastfeeding and menstruation.
