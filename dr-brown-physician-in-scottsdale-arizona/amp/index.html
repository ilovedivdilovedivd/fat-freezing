Is Profound safe for thin faces?
Profound Lift for thin skin and thick skin
Nov 3, 2017
Very good question! When I treat a thin skin patient my technique is different then when treating thick skin. Profound is safe for thin faces and very effective and at adding youthful volume to the face. Thin skin patient's usually notice volume loss early and thick skin notices more laxity. I have been equally impressed with results on all skin types. I have not seen volume or fat loss with profound but actually volume improvement.

Profound is very effective at tightening neck area and face. Doing both procedures gives the patient a wonderful outcome. Can I get CoolScuplting done 3 months after pregnancy?
Coolsculpting after pregnancy
Nov 5, 2016
We have treated many patients after pregnancy. We have three CoolSmooth Pro applicators that produce no suction during treatment. Once you come in for a consultation, we can make the best recommendation based on your medical needs. We have done coolsculpting at this practice as early as one month after pregnancy, but we treated thighs, not the abdomen.
