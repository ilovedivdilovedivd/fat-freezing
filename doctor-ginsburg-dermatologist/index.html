What filler(s) would help this boxcar scar?
box car scar
Nov 14, 2022
A filler should improve the appearance of this scar.. I like bellafill but would probably use a hyaluronic acid filler first.
How long until kenalog helps overfilled Bellafill? And more kenalog questions
bellafill
Nov 14, 2022
Kenalog can break up collagen deposits. It is used to treat keloids and hypertrophic scars..Bellafill causes collagen deposition around the beads.. It could take a month or so to see the full effect of one kenalog injection so in order to not dissolve too much collagen it is wise to wait at least a month between treatments. I dont know of any studies or reviews on collagen injections for bellafill overtreatment.
Why was I told Bellafill is not good for atrophic acne scars but Sculptra is better?
acne scars
Nov 14, 2022
I prefer bellafill for acne scars and from your photo it looks like many of your scars could be treated with bellafill.
Does you dermatologist use both Sculptra and Bellafill.? Some prefer one over the other and that could be the reason he hose Sculptra.
How many syringes of Bellafill is needed for a drastic cheekbone augmentation?
bellafill
Nov 14, 2022
I rarely if ever inject more than one syringe in an area per visit.. Its better to go slowly since bellafill is permanent and I dont want to over treat someone. I would inject monthly until the desired result is achieved.
How can I remove Bellafill surgically?
bellafill
Nov 14, 2022
Theoretically bellafill can be removed surgically. You would have to consult a plastic surgeon or maxillo-facial surgeon. Another option to try is injecting cortisone into the areas as bellafill stimulates collagen injection and intralesional injection of collagen can dissolve some of the excess collagen.. It would take several session.
Is the spread of Dysport normal?
dysport
Nov 14, 2022
It can take up to two weeks to see full effect from dysport but I suspect that these areas will need additional treatment. the lines above the eyes are tricky to treat as too much dysport there can drop the brow . It is easy to treat the lines near the hair line. go back after two weeks if they are still present. Not everyone gets lines high on the forehead so some docs dont routinely treat near the hair line. Its better to undertreat and retreat than to overtreat.
2 rounds of Accutane and my acne has come back - what can be done?
accutane
Nov 14, 2022
Acne can recur after taking accutane. accutane can also cause some mood swings and very rarely depression.. this is dose dependent. Females can take spironolactone for acne when it recurs.
Also low dose accutane can clear your face with fewer side effects.. I sometimes put a patient on 20 mg 3 times a week.. This works well but must be continued. Females are still required to have a urine pregnancy test monthly. Go back to your dermatologist and discuss the options.
Is laser lipo the same as Smart Lipo or any other type of laser lipo? Or are they all different?
laser lipo
Oct 20, 2022
Laser lipo or smart lipo was developed to give more skin tightening but after using the laser in addition to lipo did seem to give better results than just tumescent lipo using a power assisted cannula. Burns from the laser were possible although rare.
