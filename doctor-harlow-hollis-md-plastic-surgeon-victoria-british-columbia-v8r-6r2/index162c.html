Why do I have a lump on my forehead 4 days after Botox injections?

Jun 3, 2019
Almost assuredly this is a collection of blood  (hematoma) from a blood vessel being entered by the Botox needle. Location looks a little bit lateral but……..Should resolve spontaneously. A recheck with the person who did the Botox injection would be a good start if you feel the need for more insight. Most hematoma’s become colored  (bruise) and sometimes the color shifts away from the original site due to gravity pull. This is not dangerous.

Will my hair grow back after ten sessions?
Will my hair grow back after ten sessions?
Apr 8, 2019
Probably some regrowth will occur. Laser energy destroys hair roots and bulbs primarily when the hair is in a special/certain phase of its growth. Hairs are all in different phases at any given time so those hairs not in a vulnerable phase will survive. This means that some of the hairs are not destroyed and therefore will continue to grow. So expect some continued growth although it may be more fine and vellous.
