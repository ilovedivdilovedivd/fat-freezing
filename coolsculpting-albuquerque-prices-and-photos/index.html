Coolsculpting Albuquerque Prices And Photos

Coolsculpting is an innovative, painless method of body shaping.

Cryolipolysis is the newest method of dosed and selective cold exposure, with the help of which a correction of the figure is achieved and excess fat is removed.

With the help of this method, it is possible to work on individual problem areas and places of fat accumulation – buttocks, abdomen, forearms and thighs.

The procedure for coolsculpting consists in the ability of the fat to melt already at a temperature of 17-20 C.

With the help of special applicators, the body parts are gradually cooled to -5 C, causing the fat to crystallize.

Lipid inclusions in the crystallization process become solid, and the cell membranes are damaged and die.

Additional Sessions May Further Enhance Your Results

The cryolipolysis session lasts 50-60 minutes, and the number of procedures is determined at the first examination by a specialist. One or two sessions of coolsculpting are required.

For small body fat, and for large volumes, no more than 4 procedures are performed at intervals not more than 1 time per month. Such a break is necessary for the body for a natural mechanism for removing dead cells and decay products.

In one session, 4-8 cm in volume is removed, and the amount of adipose tissue is reduced by 25-30%.

The technique differs from other procedures for correcting the body shape in that the result becomes more noticeable over time, as the process of cell death stretches for 40-70 or more days. The first results can appear in 2 weeks, and the maximum effect will be visible only after 1-2 months.

Indications for the procedure:

  • presence of fatty folds and deposits in the area of shoulders and forearms, abdomen, hips, waist.
  • Presence of excess weight
  • Excess fat on the anterior and lateral surfaces of the abdomen, areas where, due to genetic predisposition, fat removal is most difficult.

Contraindications:

  • Individual sensitivity of the skin to cold.
  • Inflammatory skin diseases in the treated area.
  • Obesity.
  • The period of pregnancy and breastfeeding.
  • Open wounds and skin integrity at the site of exposure.
  • Mental disorders and epilepsy.
  • Presence of cardiac stimulants and metal implants.
  • Infectious diseases and the presence of a chronic foci of infection.
  • Hernias (umbilical).
  • Violation of the process of blood clotting.
  • Kidney disease (kidney failure).
  • Diseases of the liver and gallbladder.
  • Diseases of the endocrine system.
  • Some medications (hormone therapy).

Some time in the area of influence of the device can remain reddening, which completely disappears the next day (no subcutaneous hematoma, edema or painful sensations, as it happens after surgical liposuction, in the case of cryolipolysis does not happen). In the next month or two, the patient will only observe how the treated area gradually shrinks in volume.

After the session, the doctor advises the patient to adhere to the principles of healthy eating, do not abuse heavy, fatty foods and alcohol, drink as much pure water as possible, since the decay products are easier to remove with liquid. There are no other restrictions: you can play sports in usual rhythm, do any additional procedures.

Various wraps and hydromassage are especially well combined with coolsculpting. Hydromassage perfectly activates local lymphatic drainage and promotes the prompt removal of dead fat cells.

Coolsculpting Utilizes A Precisely Controlled Cooling Method Called Cryolipolysis

It is not necessary to conduct mesotherapy in parallel with cryolipolysis, it is better to resort to it after a couple of months, if in areas of weight loss the skin suddenly loses its tone. In general, a competent combination of cryolipolysis with other procedures and their alternation will help to achieve excellent results and a perfect body, but here one can not do without the advice of an experienced and professional doctor.

Safety of Coolsculpting treatment

The method of cryolipolysis refers to the procedure of a high degree of safety. The unique technology of the Zeltiq device completely excludes cold damage to the skin, nerves, vessels and internal organs. In the US, the procedure was approved by the FDA (Food and Drug Administration – licensing agency for health and social services in the US) and became known as Coolsculpting.

The technique is recognized to be 22.4% more effective than other hardware procedures for correction of fat deposits, and most importantly – much more gentle, since the reduction of adipose tissue is carried out gradually and without the formation of toxic decomposition products. Thus, the procedure does not cause the body any harm and actually reduces to zero possible side effects.
