Cool Fat Cryo Before And After

A characteristic feature that distinguishes Cool Fat Cryo is an effective technique for the non-surgical removal of excess fat by cooling.

The effectiveness of cryolipolysis is officially confirmed by the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Using this technique, you can quickly get rid of excess fat and cellulite.

To perform the procedure, you need a special device with a vacuum nozzle. The fat fold is drawn by the nozzle and cooled to the optimally low temperature, while only fat is cooled, the remaining tissues are not affected. After freezing, fat cells decrease in size, and then are removed from the body.

Adipose tissue is reduced gradually, after the first treatment by 25%, after the second leaves have 45-50% of the skin in the affected area does not sag.

To perform the Cool Fat Cryo procedure, anesthesia and surgical intervention are not needed. It has no harmful effect on the liver and other internal organs.

After it is carried out, you do not have to wait, you can immediately return to normal life.

Cool Fat Cryo is used for:

  • to get rid of excess fat;
  • to reduce body volume;
  • Helps to cope with swelling and manifestations of cellulite;
  • improves skin elasticity and firmness.

When will I see the first results of Cool Fat Cryo procedure?

In order to see the first results after the procedure, it is enough to wait 2-3 weeks. The full course consists of 1-4 procedures, after each procedure a month of break is given. The optimal effect appears after 1.5-2 months, this is how much time the body needs for complete release from the products of the decomposition of fat cells.

How is the Cool Fat Cryo performed?

It is not necessary to conduct special preparation prior to treatment. At the beginning of the procedure the doctor distributes a special gel on the problem zone. In the first 10 minutes, after installing the nozzle on the skin, the patient may feel a mild cold. Then this feeling disappears. During the procedure, the patient can sleep, watch TV, read or work on a laptop.

Typically, the procedure lasts 1 hour.

Recommendation: After the procedure, the treated area should be lubricated with a cream or ointment recommended by the doctor.

Contraindications to Cool Fat Cryo:

  • presence of cold urticaria and other acute reactions to low temperatures;
  • blood disorder in the treatment zone;
  • postherpetic neuralgia;
  • problems with blood coagulability;
  • diabetes;
  • hernia, open wounds or scars in the treatment zone;
  • Pregnancy and lactation.

To correct the body shape and remove local fat deposits by non-surgical way, the newest technology called Cool Fat Cryo is very popular today.

This is a unique technique developed by specialists from Harvard University and the Massachusetts General Hospital. As a result of clinical trials, scientists managed to prove that fat cells are receptive to cold. In fact, this discovery became the basis of the cryolipolysis technique.

The procedure itself is absolutely painless, as it is non-invasive (does not damage the skin). The device for cryolipolysis is equipped with a special vacuum nozzle, which draws in the fat fold, while cooling the subcutaneous fatty tissue.

The result of this effect is the destruction of fat cells and a gradual decrease in the fat layer by 20-40%.

A characteristic feature that distinguishes cryolipolysis from other methods of body correction is the absence of the need for additional lymphatic drainage manipulations.

Immediately after the procedure, the fat cells begin to self-destruct naturally, removing from the body in the process of metabolism.
