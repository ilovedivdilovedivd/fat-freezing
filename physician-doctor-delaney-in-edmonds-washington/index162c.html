If you have an auto immune deficiency disorder can you have Botox to help with sweating?
Botox & Selective IgA Deficiency
Sep 2, 2017
Hello Valfiggy,

It is unlikely that there would be a direct problem with someone receiving Botox (onabotulinumtoxinA) for hyperhidrosis (increased sweating) who also has a selective IgA deficiency.

However, that said, a few caveats:
