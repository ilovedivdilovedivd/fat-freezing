How much will I reduce my chances of swelling with a cannula for lips?
Not a big difference
Aug 19, 2018
This is a very good question! I have injected lips with both needles and cannula and I genuinely do not feel there is a significant difference with post op swelling between the two. In my opinion needles are better as they provide razor sharp accuracy, and have personally moved away from cannulas when performing lip augmentation.

How can I get a long nose without surgery and make it smaller?
Dermal Filler
May 18, 2018
Thanks for your question, Im not quite sure what you mean by “longer”, however dermal filler can give the appearance of a smaller and straighter nose. The filler would be injected at different points of the nose, allowing light to reflect/bounce of the skin texture in an “illusion” for it to look smaller. Nose fillers do pose their risks so please seek consultation with an experienced practitioner.
