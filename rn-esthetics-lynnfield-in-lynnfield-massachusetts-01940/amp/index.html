Bruising with Radiesse
Answer from a Nurse Practitioner/Trainer
Feb 11, 2018
Bruising is a common complication of having any Dermal Filler treatment, especially in the lower face area. Using a microcannula with Fillers can certainly reduce the risk of bruising in the future. Other things that can be helpful is avoiding blood thinners like Ibuprofen and supplements like fish oil/chia seeds 3-5 days before injection. These work best for Dynamic lines (those in motion) vs static lines (those at rest). Often for static lines, Neuromodulators are used in combination with Dermal Fillers for the cheek and eye area.
