Nasolabial fold treatment

Headache and Botox
Jan 18, 2019

When I got Botox in between my eye brows I get 20 units and get little surface bubbles. Is this from to
Botox between eyebrows
Jan 18, 2019
Botox is injected into different depths depending on the location. In between the eye and above the medial brow Botox is injected into the muscle. It is common to have some "bubbling" after injection although less common in between the eyes. On average Botox will last 3 months but for some patients it may be less. Using a higher dose may result in longer lasting results. Good luck!
What injection areas does eyebrow drooping and ptosis commonly occur in when getting Botox?
Eyebrow ptosis after Botox
Jan 17, 2019
Eyebrow asymmetry and ptosis are most common after treating the forehead muscle. This is the only muscle that lifts the brows so when treating for the first time it is always advisable to be conservative. More significant is eyelid ptosis which occurs when treating too low and near the midline of the brow. Treatment of glabellar lines should rarely result with brow ptosis when done by an experienced professional who is also well educated in facial anatomy.

Coolsculpting is a very effective treatment for reduction in stubborn belly fat. A comprehensive in person assessment would be best before deciding if Coolsculpting is right for you. Expect a 20-25% reduction. Many patients require 2 treatments for best results.
