How does cryolipolysis work?

With the help of a special device, the doctor-cosmetologist will work on your fat cells with cold. They will be destroyed by cold and within a few months will naturally leave the body.

Other cells will not be damaged by cold, so the procedure of cryolipolysis has no negative consequences and does not require recovery.

With the help of coolsculpting, you will remove the accumulation of fat:

  • in the abdomen and back;
  • in the decollete zone;
  • at the waist and arms;
  • on hips (including get rid of “ears”);
  • on the chin.
