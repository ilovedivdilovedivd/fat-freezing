Where does the fat go? How does your body get rid of it?
What Happens to The Fat
May 16, 2018
Following the Coolsculpting treatment, about 25% of the fat cells in the areas treated begin to go through a programmed cell death. At that point, our body begins to break down those dead fat cells, which are then reabsorbed into the blood stream and broken down further by our lymphatic system. This process may take up to 3-4 months to complete.

May 16, 2018
Swelling in the areas treated following the Coolsculpting treatment is common. This side effect make last up to 3-4 weeks but should be gradually returning to normal with every passing day. If you have any bruising in those areas, the swelling may persist a bit longer. These are all within the normal range of known side effects for Coolsculpting.

May 10, 2018
If your are experiencing enlargement of of the treatment areas that are firm, this may be a result of an extremely rare side effect of Coolsculpting called Paradoxial Hyperplasia.
