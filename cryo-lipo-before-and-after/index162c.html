Cryo Lipo Before And After Photos

Cryo Lipo is a modern innovative technology for getting rid of excess fat. The procedure is based on pointwise exposure to cold on local areas with unwanted fat deposits. Cryo Lipo is considered to be an analog of the already known Coolsculpting by Zeltiq technique, but unlike it has the additional advantage of removing small fat deposits in hard-to-reach places such as the chin, the inside of the knees and hands, the area above the knees.

Indications for use

If the mirror mercilessly shows an unsightly picture – an ugly cushion around the waist, a row of folds on the back and abdomen, a fat riding breeches on the hips, hands with flabby and sagging skin, a swollen area above the knees. Technology Cryo Lipo will help Say goodbye to local fat deposits.

What is Cryo Lipo?

Low temperatures are used that are directed to problem areas of the body during the cryolipolysis procedure,. Thanks to the cold, there is an active breakdown of fats and, as a consequence, a significant decrease in volumes. The procedure is completely painless and does not cause any discomfort to the patient.

Effect of the procedure

Cryolipolysis technology is surprisingly effective: You can get rid of 45% fat accumulation during just one Cryo Lipo session. And the effect is noticeable at once, and in the next six weeks it will only increase.

Advantages of Lipo Cryo

The procedure is completely safe. The effect of cold is only on the problem area, without traumatizing nearby skin and tissues.

The session lasts only half an hour. Accordingly, in one hour you can process two problem areas.

Freeze Your Fat Away And Love What You See In The Mirror

A recovery period is not required. Immediately after the end of the session, you can return to everyday activities. The fat deposits at the site of exposure are reduced by 45%.

Contraindications

You should refrain from the Cryo Lipo procedure when you need the following diseases and conditions:

  1. pregnancy;
  2. the presence of injuries and infections;
  3. Raynaud’s disease;
  4. altered skin sensitivity;
  5. skin diseases (psoriasis, dermatitis);
  6. presence of a pacemaker;
  7. cold urticaria;
  8. paroxysmal cold hemoglobinuria;
  9. an extensive area of scar tissue at the site of exposure;
  10. presence of sites with altered peripheral circulation;
  11. presence of injured areas (bleeding);
  12. the stage of obesity, in which the non-surgical method is useless.

Safety

Using Non-surgical Fat Removal To Give The Slim And Toned Bodies

Unlike aggressive techniques, Lipo Cryo technology is absolutely safe. During the session, the skin is not injured or damaged, and the pigment change is completely eliminated.

Duration of the course

The number of sessions depends on the thickness of the fat deposits. Sometimes it is possible to solve the problem in one procedure, you may need two sessions while working with the belly or waistline. In each case, the number of procedures is assigned individually.
