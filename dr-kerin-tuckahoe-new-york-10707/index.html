I had CoolSculpting done in Nov 2022. I feel I have gotten even bigger in the area
After CS Area Is Bigger
Aug 9, 2023
HI. Thanks for your question. It is possible for an area treated by CS to get bigger, rather than smaller, but this is not common. This is called PAH, which is Paradoxical Adipose Hyperplasia, and occurs approximately 1 in 3000. Your 5kg weight gain could also explain why the area looks bigger.
I suggest you return to the place where you were treated and be evaluated. If there were pre-treatment photos, that can help.
Best of luck.

Can I get CoolSculpting in just my abdomen area to lose that 30lbs?
CS To Lose Weight?
Jan 26, 2021
Hi. Thanks for the question. No, unfortunately CS will not help you lose that 30lbs. Neither will liposuction. With a tummy tuck you can lose weight, but not 30lbs. Weight loss and fat loss are different things. Weight loss occurs throughout your body when the fat cells shrink in size. Fat loss is a focus loss of fat cells – they don’t shrink, they are removed.
See a weight loss physician in your area.
Best of luck.
