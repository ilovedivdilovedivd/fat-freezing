Zerona Versus Coolsculpting

CoolSculpting is a much better option than Zerona. It has proven results in terms of fat reduction. Additionally, there is minimal to no downtime typically after the treatment. (Daniel Shapiro, MD, Scottsdale Plastic Surgeon)

In our experience, results after Zerona with dieting and supplements were sporadic, but after coolsculpting alone they are reliably positive.

Plastic surgeons will usually recommend liposuction, but if you don’t want to go through surgery Zeltiq coolsculpting is the best option.

I have no reason to think that the results will be any more “temporary” than liposuction. (Peter Cooperrider, MD (retired), Kirkland Dermatologist)

Though they were approved at around the same time there are distinct differences between Zeltiq Coolsculpting and Zerona.

Zeltiq targets specific areas of fat that patients are unhappy with by freezing and killing those fat cells(actually a portion of them) and then they are eliminated by the body.

There is no diet or exercise regimen indicated with Zeltiq but it is recommended who are relatively stable in their weight already. The Zerona uses low level laser, in conjunction with the encouragement of a diet and exercise regimen to stimulate overall (throughout the entire body) circumferential decrease in size due to the ’emptying’ of fat cells.

So far, in our practice (we have both technologies), we have found the Zeltiq to be the most satisfying for patients and have seen some amazing improvements in stubborn areas like the belly, love handles, flanks, bra fat, etc.

Coolsculpting vs. Zerona

I would suggest that you be examined in person by someone who performs these procedures to see if you would be a good candidate for one or both. The difference is that Coolsculpting freezes the fat cells and body gets rid of them vs.

Zerona cold laser emulsifies the fat. The biggest catch is the diet that you have to purchase separately when having Zerona to get the best results. Coolsculpting procedure does not require any specific diet afterwards. (Tom J. Pousti, MD, FACS, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting or Zerona

Zerona does not claim to kill the fat cells so the cells that have lost some fat can regain the fat easily. CoolSculpting freezes and kills the fat cells so the results may be more long term.

In both treatments, the residual fat cells can gain fat if one eats more and/or exercises less, so maintain a good exercise and diet plan for both treatments. (Ronald Shelton, MD, Manhattan Dermatologic Surgeon)
