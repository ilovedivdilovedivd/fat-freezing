Does Zeltiq still work if no massage is given after the hour of treatment?
Massage is important immediately post treatment
Jul 17, 2018
While the CoolSculpting is still causing cryolipolysis (death to the fat cell by cold), administering the massage immediately helps to ensure that all possible treated cells die. By not administering the massage until after the area begins to return to regular temperature, you can risk less efficacy of the treatment. At our practice, we make sure the technicians are in the room and ready to remove applicator and perform the massage 1-2 minutes before the treatment ends.
