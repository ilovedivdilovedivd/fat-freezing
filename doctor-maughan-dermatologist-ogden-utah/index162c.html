Is the fat loss from CoolSculpting permanent? Or does it eventually come back?
Permanent Fat Loss from CoolSculpt
Jun 7, 2018
CoolSculpt permanently kills fat cells in the treated area – approximately 20-25% of the cells in this area. Although these cells are gone, your body can deposit more fat (and thus enlarge) the remaining fat cells. Bottom line is that you need to maintain a stable weight to maintain your results. Best of luck!
I have calcified fat in my calves and stomach. It is very hard and lumpy. Can CoolSculpting remove
CoolSculpting is not the answer
Jun 4, 2018
Unfortunately, CoolSculpt targets pinchable fat. The applicator is attached to a vacuum. The vacuum sucks the tissue into a U shaped metal plate that freezes the fat. You will not achieve a good seal if you don’t have tissue that is easily picked up by the applicator. Best wishes!
Will CoolSculpting on the belly displace my IUD?
CoolScupt suction works on external fat only
Jun 4, 2018
Hi, The CoolSculpt vacuum effect is limited to the fat located above your abdominal wall. The vacuum is powerful enough to pull tissue into the CoolSculpt applicator but should not affect your IUD.
