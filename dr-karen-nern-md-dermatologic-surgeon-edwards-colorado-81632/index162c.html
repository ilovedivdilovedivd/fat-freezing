Vaginal Skin Tags?
See your provider to see what they are
Feb 12, 2013
Skin tags can happen around the anus or the vaginal area, but there are other things that can look like tags but are not. Sometimes hemorrhoids can look like tags around the anus. Also, genital warts caused by the human papilloma virus can sometimes look like tags. I would recommend that you see a dermatologist, your family physician or an OB/GYN to determine what is causing them.
