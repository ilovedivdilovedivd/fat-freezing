Gel sizers vs rice sizers?
Breast implant sizers
Sep 28, 2019
A difference of 20cc in implant size will have minimal affect on your result. The difference is .25cm in the diameter. If you are more concerned about being too small, pick 275cc if your concern is being too big, pick 255. I don’t find bra sizers particularly helpful, what an implant looks like in a bra does not always translate into what it looks like in the body. If you trust your surgeon’s judgement, allow her/hIm to pick the size that most appropriately fills the space.

Staged augmentation mastopexy
May 26, 2019

Lumps after liposuction
May 21, 2019
I personally had liposuction and had firm areas for a few months. When we do liposuction, we can’t completely suction out every since damaged fat cell, so cells in our body “eat” those cells and dispose of them, just like it does with a bruise. It can take 6 months for those areas to soften and eventually disappear. Have your surgeon about your concerns on your next visit.
