Description of the Coolsculpting procedure – the essence and mechanism of action

Modern technologies and developments allow you to reduce body fat without resorting to exhausting workouts, diets or surgical intervention.

This procedure was developed by American scientists and presented to the world in 2008. The procedure had a number of tests.

Carrying out of cryolipolysis is regulated by a special instruction, it is carried out in several certain stages. The essence, mechanism of action and description of the procedure

The coolsculpting treatment in Tampa, FL is often called cryoliposuction or cold liposculpture. Its essence lies in cryogenic splitting of subcutaneous fat not in an invasive way.

This is due to the start of the process of apoptosis. This is a natural process of the body, in which the destruction of fat cells and the gradual removal of them from the body through the blood and lymph.

A Noninvasive, Nonsurgical Skin-care Treatment

The mechanism of coolsculpting treatment is to start apoptosis, that is, the natural destruction of fat cells. This is due to the cooling of a specific area of the body in which a reduction in body fat is required.

Fat cells, in contrast to vascular cells, nerves and muscles, are less resistant to lower temperatures. It is thanks to this fact that during the process of cryolipolysis under the influence of cold, fat is split, and the skin, vessels and muscles are not damaged in any way.

The temperature for achieving such a process should vary from normal body temperature to zero degrees. The standard temperature is 5 degrees. To start apoptosis in the body will take about one hour of exposure to cold fat layer. The procedure assumes local action. This means that a reduction in the fat layer is possible only in one part of the body: the abdomen, thighs, knees, etc.

One procedure of cryolipolysis involves exposure to one zone by cold with a special device with a vacuum applicator. Previously, the specialist marks the area of exposure and lubricates the gel. One of the nozzle of the apparatus is applied to this zone and captures the fold. A vacuum is created. The exposure to cold lasts about 60 minutes. A small discomfort is possible.

The procedure is strictly regulated. The device for its implementation is developed and released by Zeltiq. This device does not have analogs for today. The procedure is carried out only with such equipment. Any other apparatus is not applicable for cryolipolysis.

Zeltiq Coolsculping is the full name of the device for carrying out a cold procedure. Only its use is approved for coolsculpting treatment in Tampa, FL. This apparatus has passed all clinical trials and is effective and not dangerous for the splitting of fatty deposits.

CoolSculpting Is A Reasonably Priced, Nonsurgical Option

To date, Zeltiq Coolsculping has only 6 different vacuum applicators:

  • CoolCurve;
  • CoolFit;
  • CoolMax;
  • CoolCore;
  • CoolSmooth;
  • CoolCurve+;

All these attachments differ in the area of application and the intended use. So, you can classify them as follows:

Vacuum nozzles CoolCurve and CoolCurve + are characterized by the smallest area of influence and are applicable for the processing of small folds on the bends of the body and in hard-to-reach places.

The CoolFit and CoolCore coolers have a slightly larger surface, 18.5 and 19.2 cm, respectively. These attachments are used for small and medium fat folds on the abdomen and sides, as well as on the inside of the thighs and on the back.
CooolMax is considered to be the largest and is used for the largest folds and fat deposits.

Zones of correction

Virtually any part of the body can be corrected by cold. The most common areas of application of the device for cryolipolysis are the following zones:

  • Stomach.
  • Sides.
  • Hips.
  • Buttocks.
  • Area over the knees.
  • Fatty folds on the back and arms.

Depending on the area of the fat fold and its location, various attachments of the Zeltiq apparatus are used. This allows the most efficient procedure.

Approximate prices

Coolsculpting is distinguished by its high cost. In different clinics, the cost varies from very low prices to very high. At the proposed price for the service, you can determine what procedure is going to be provided: using real technology on the Zeltiq device or one of the unchecked analogs.

On the device Zeltiq on the original technology one session using a small applicator can cost about 300-400 $. In the case of using a large vacuum applicator, this cost increases to $ 500-600 per session. If several parts of body fat are planned to be processed at one time, the price for one zone can be reduced by 10-20%.

The procedure at home

The device for coolsculpting treatment and the procedure for its carrying out are unique.

Do not try to conduct it without special equipment and training yourself at home. This can lead to unpredictable consequences, a number of side effects and complications. For example, frostbite, damage to the skin area, etc.
