CoolSculpting for muffin top

CoolSculpting is commonly used to reduce fat on the flanks (muffin top). A single treatment session will normally destroy about 20-25% of the fat cells in the treated area. Some people need more than one treatment session to obtain the desired result. Ultimately, an examination of you would be needed to determine if you would be a good candidate for the treatment or not. (Michael I. Echavez, MD, San Francisco Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting is an amazing procedure that permanently reduces pockets of fat by 20-30% with each application. It is not weight loss but rather is body contouring for areas where superficial fatty tissue is resistant to diet and exercise.
