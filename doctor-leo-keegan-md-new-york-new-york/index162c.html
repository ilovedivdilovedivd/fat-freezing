Could I have diastasis? Is a tummy tuck the only option to correct it?
Abdominal Contour after Pregnancy
Jul 23, 2014
Your photos seem to show a significant convexity or bulging in your lower abdomen. This is fairly common to varying degrees after pregnancy. In your case, the bulging is significant and may be due to a generalized laxity of your musculo-fascial layer or to a diastasis recti. In either case, correction of the diastasis and tightening of the muscle and fascia can help restore your contour.
