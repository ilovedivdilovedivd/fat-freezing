Cryolipolysis. How does it work?

Cryolipolysis on Cryo Lipo device in the clinic is a controlled cooling of adipose tissue. Correction of the body is as follows:

Fat fold is captured in a special vacuum applicator, due to which it is cooled immediately from two sides.

CryoLipo Lowers Temperature Of Skin And Fat

  • Low temperatures cause death of fat cells, since they are not adapted to long-term cooling. As a result, they break up into separate bodies (this process is called apoptosis).
  • Vacuum exposure enhances the effect, as it disrupts the circulatory processes in the fat fold.
  • Double exposure (low temperature and vacuum) destroys fatty tissues from the inside.
  • The products of decay are excreted from the body independently through the lymphatic system.

Safety

A specialist working on the Cryo Lipo apparatus only affects fat cells in a certain area. The surrounding tissues are not affected, so they are not destroyed or damaged.

Today, cryolipolysis is recognized as one of the safest hardware procedures for modeling a perfect body.
