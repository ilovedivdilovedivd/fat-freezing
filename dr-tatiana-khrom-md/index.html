Fillers and cold sore
Feb 23, 2015
If you are prone to cold sores whenever you have any
treatment of the lips or surrounding area it may reactivate it. So you always have to inform your provider
about your history so we can give you prophylactic treatment for the outbreak
which usually involves taking a few antiviral pills. Other treatments that may
reactivate the virus are chemical peels and lasers.
