There are a few different treatments that may help address your concerns. Can Coolsculpting be used for fat in the under arm area?
CoolSculpting for male bra fat
Sep 19, 2023
CoolSculpting is used to treat the upper back and bra fat area very frequently in men and women. For more information about CoolSculpting and the areas that are able to be treated.
Coolsculpting + Intermittent Fasting - will that hasten the elimination of dead fat cells?
CoolSculpting and fasting
Sep 18, 2023
There are no studies to show that fasting after CoolSculpting will improve results. To achieve best results your CoolSculpting provider should be doing a two-minute post-massage immediately after your treatment. The post-treatment massage is the only thing that has been studied to increase CoolSculpting results.
Cryliposis after lipo to improve shape of torso?
CoolSculpting after Liposcution
Sep 18, 2023
CoolSculpting can be used after liposuction to help reduce any leftover areas of stubborn fat. I would recommend reaching out to your plastic surgeon and asking if you're cleared to have the treatment done if you had the liposuction within the past year.
Electrolysis one day after Coolsculpting Elite. Is this enough time between or should I reschedule?
CoolSculpting before Electrolysis
Sep 18, 2023
I recommend asking your CoolSculpting and Electrolysis providers if they are comfortable with your treatments being 24 hours apart.
24 “applicators” average cost?
CoolSculpting Cost
Sep 18, 2023
CoolSculpting treatments (or applications) usually retail for around $800 per treatment depending on the size of the area. Generally, practices will offer package price discounts when patients are purchasing multiple treatments. If you need a total of 24 treatments spaced out between two sessions that would cost on average about $6,000 per session.
Coolsculpting Elite or TruSculpt ID, which procedure delivers better results in the flanks and love
CoolSculpting Elite for permanent fat removal
Sep 18, 2023
CoolSculpting Elite is a great treatment for permanently reducing stubborn fat in the flank area. For best results, the fat in the area should be soft and pinchable.
Can you have laser hair removal on face whilst taking Azithromycin?
Laser hair removal and Azithromycin
Aug 24, 2023
Undergoing laser hair removal while taking Azithromycin is not recommended. Azithromycin can be photo-sensitizing. This medication should be discontinued a month before your treatment.
Can a dark spot appear after laser hair removal?
Laser hair removal and dark spots
Aug 24, 2023
Any type of laser treatment can cause hyperpigmentation. Ensuring that your treatments are being done by a licensed and well-trained professional as well as following your provider's pre and post-care instructions can help minimize the risk.
Laser hair removal safe during pregnancy?
Laser hair removal and pregnancy
Aug 24, 2023
Laser hair removal is not recommended during pregnancy. There are no studies to show if it is safe for the mother or baby.

While using topical clindamycin and tretinoin gel you will want to avoid any type of laser treatments to your face. Areas where you are not applying these topicals are sage to treat.
Can I get laser hair removal if my hair bulb is white?
Laser Hair Removal
Aug 24, 2023
When undergoing laser hair removal the lasers target the melanin. If you have a white bulb then there is no melanin and laser hair removal may not be the best treatment option for you. Electrolysis works well for light, grey, and white hairs that lack melanin.
