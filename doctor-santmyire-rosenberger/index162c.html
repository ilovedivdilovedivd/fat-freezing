If I got my forehead injected today with Botox, can I do crows feet in 3 weeks?
Botox treatment spacing
Nov 9, 2018
In our practice, we do not retreat the SAME area within 3 months if it is fully relaxed, but new areas are fine. Generally, I would avoid bouncing around if you can. If you do move between providers, I would have each provider give you a record of what area was treated each time so you keep everyone on the same page and avoid over treating.
Does CoolSculpting always remove 20-25% of fat regardless of how much fat there is to be
Percentage of cells removed with a cycle
Nov 9, 2018
Indeed, the average number of cells removed per cycle is 25%. This is the total number of cells within the treated area within the applicator. As the sizes of the pocket of fat decreases, you may need a smaller applicator to correctly treat the area. In a few patients we have treated small areas that remain with Kybella. Using Kybella off the chin is off label, but effective.
Is 10-20 minutes of a CoolSculpting application enough to freeze fat?
Coolsculpting timing
Nov 9, 2018
During each coolsculpting cycle, the treated are reaches the desired temperature in the 29-31 minute period of a 35 minute cycle. Treating less than this time will not adequately trigger apoptosis, the desired death of the fat cells.
How soon after microneedling can I use retinol on my face?
Retinol and microneedling
Nov 9, 2018
I recommend waiting 2 weeks or until all redness and peeling have resolved. Most microneedling procedures have specific post care instructions. Follow those instructions for 2 weeks then resume your regular skin care regimen.
