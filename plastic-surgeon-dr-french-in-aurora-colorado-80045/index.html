Long-term Effects of Coolsculpting?
PAH
Oct 28, 2017
I agree with many of the answers listed below. I would like to add, however, that there is an extremely low risk (but it does exist) of PAH or paradoxical adipose hypertrophy. What is this? A situation in which the fat cells treated by Coolsculpting become larger and the area looks fuller. This is very rare, but consumers should be aware of it's possibility.

Should I massage a scar?
Timing of scar massage
May 10, 2015
Depending on the condition and healing of the scar, I typically reserve recommendations for massage until close to the 4-6 week mark. Scars take 8 weeks to regain their full strength, which is about 80% as strong as never-injured skin, so rough massage prior to this time may cause widening or irregular healing. I recommend SPF 30 or higher and a scar cream/gel with silicone/dimethicone such as you are using. Best of luck!
