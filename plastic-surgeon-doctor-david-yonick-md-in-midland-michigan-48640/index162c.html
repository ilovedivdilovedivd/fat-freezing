Does CoolSculpting Really Work?
Coolsculpting Does Work
Jun 14, 2018
Coolsculpting works by delivering controlled cooling to destroy fat cells underneath the skin without harming any surrounding skin or tissue.The fat cells are crystalized and then die. Those fat cells are naturally eliminated within the next 3 days after treatment. Coolsculpting destroys about 20-25% Fat cells each time an applicator is applied on the targeted area. Is coolsculpting good for drooping skin?
Coolsculpting is a non-invasive fat reduction procedure
Jun 13, 2018
Coolsculpting delivers controlled cooling to permanently destroy fat cells that are targeted through treatment. Coolsculpting is not skin tightening as with cryolipolisis we are strictly targeting fat cells without harming surrounding skin and tissue. There are many surgical and non-surgical methods for treating skin laxity, such as SkinTyte.
