Where in the body are grams of fat that are consumed after CoolSculpting deposited?
Fat consumption after CoolSculpting
Apr 1, 2017
CoolSculpting will induce premature cell death of the fat cells in the treated area called apoptosis. Once these fat cells are gone they do not regrow.  In order to achieve the optimal results it is important for patients to be in neutral calorie intake.  If you consume excess calories it will be stored as fat in the remaining fat cells of the body.
