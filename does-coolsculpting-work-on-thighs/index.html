CoolSculpting and CoolSmooth Can Eliminate Inner-Thigh Fat

CoolSculpting works by freezing the fat cells and killing them without adversely affecting any of the other skin, tissue or muscle in the area.

Until fairly recently, you needed to have a “pinchable” amount of fat of about 1 to 2 inches so that it could be sucked up between the two cooling plates on the CoolSculpting applicator.

However, a new applicator called CoolSmooth has now become available. If the fat is too thin for pinching, the CoolSmooth applicator is simply run over the area to be treated. I would need to see you in person to determine which applicator is best for your inner thighs.

Of note, you may also want to be treated with CoolAdvantage – which uses a lower temperature than CoolSculpting and cuts treatment time by about 45%. Therefore, a session that used to take 60 minutes now takes about 35 minutes.

Reach out to a board-certified dermatologist to learn more about your options. (Melda A. Isaac, MD, Washington DC Dermatologic Surgeon)
