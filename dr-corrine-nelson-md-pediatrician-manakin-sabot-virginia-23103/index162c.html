Waiting for Results
Dec 6, 2016
I often tell my patients that waiting for your CoolSculpting results can be the most “painful” part of the process. It takes your body a good 2 months to naturally process and remove the fat cells and can take up to 4 months. The process happens slowly so it’s difficult to “see” it happening, and for that reason the before and after photos are crucial. So be patient and make sure you follow up for your after photos.
