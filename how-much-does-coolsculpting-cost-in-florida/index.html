How Much Does Coolsculpting Cost In Florida?

The price for coolscupting is priceless compared to having lipo suction which is invasive and requires down time. You can’t put a dollar sign on the amount of time saved and pain you will not have by having coolsculpting compared to lipo- suction.

I once had a patient who had lipo suction and she said she would rather have her fat back because she was in so much pain. The actual price is dependent on how many applicators are used to kill the fat cells you intend to destroy.

The average cost for each applicator is anywhere from $750 – $850 per applicator site, pending on the site you want to vanish say for example your lower abdomen that would be two applicators and based on your Free consultation you may need 1 or two treatments to the site. (Rosalind Jackson, MD, Miamisburg OB-GYN)

Cost of Coolsculpting in Florida

In general, most practices that offer coolscultping are asked by Zeltiq to price small applicators at $750 each (think 2 applicators-one on each side- for love handles) and large applicators at $1500 each (commonly used for the lower abdomen).

After package pricing, most of our patients pay between $650-$1250 per applicator. We encourage all patients to treat each area of concern 2 times for the best outcomes as each treatment will elimate approximate 20% of the visibale fat.

Two treatments eliminating approximately 40% of fat or more is the gold standard. We offer package pricing for those interested in multiple treatments. This option requires pre-payment, but lowers the per-treatment costs to you. (Mauricio J. Castellon, MD, Melbourne Plastic Surgeon)

CS costs vary

While prices are largely dependent upon the number of applicator cycles required, there are some additional ways to cut the cost. Some practices offer membership into a “Cool Club”-when you purchase 10 cycles, it drops the individual price of each cycle that can be applied immediately and in the future.

Other practices offer other combinations of savings, so be sure to get several consultations as you would with any procedure. (Vaishali B. Doolabh, MD, FACS, Jacksonville Plastic Surgeon)

The price for CoolSculpting is actually regulated by Zeltq, the company that makes the machines. You will find that prices are all within a range between 600-750 per area treated. In some cases where lots of areas are being treated the price can be a little lower.

The fact is, every doctor providing CoolSculpting is very unlikely to reduce their prices below these guidelines. You should visit a couple practices and see where you feel most comfortable. Then freeze away your unwanted fat!. (Adam Rubinstein, MD, Miami Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpting Pricing

The company restricts how low you can advertise coolsculpting. Coolscupting treatments at our practice start at $600. The cost for Coolsculpting for a small area starts at $600 for a cycle of CoolCore, CoolCurve, or Coolfit. The cost for Coolscupting for a large area will be $1200 for a cycle of Coolmax.

A patient may need multiple cycles to achieve the optimal treatment. You will need to get assessed in person to get a proper understanding of full costs. (Kris M. Reddy, MD, FACS, West Palm Beach Plastic Surgeon)
CoolSculpting typically starts at $1500 per area treated (ex. lower abdomen, upper abdomen, love handles, etc.). The new CoolSmooth applicator starts at a higher price point due to the treatment lasting two hours, instead of one.

Your CoolSculpting provider will do a thorough evaluation of your areas of concern and develop a treatment plan and price quote. Most patients require between 1-2 treatments of CoolSculpting to achieve maximal results. (Kent V. Hasen, MD, Naples Plastic Surgeon)
