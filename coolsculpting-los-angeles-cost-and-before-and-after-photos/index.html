Coolsculpting is the most effective way to get rid of excess fat deposits. Do you want to put yourself in order for the upcoming summer season as quickly and safely as possible?

Tired of torturing yourself with diet and exercise in the gym? Are you afraid of pain, and you are not attracted to the idea of liposuction with the help of plastic surgery?

Our clinic is ready to offer you one of the most effective, safe and relatively fast ways to combat surpluses of subcutaneous fat with the help of an innovative technique called cryoliposuction or cryolipolysis. Cryolipolysis allows you to get rid of hated fat deposits in problem areas for a short period of time, without surgical intervention, without painful or other unpleasant sensations, without a long and difficult recovery period, without any complications.

Body Contouring Just Got Easier

The essence of the unique in its effectiveness procedure of coolsculpting is the cleavage of fat cells under the influence of cold. As a rule, from 3 to 5 procedures of cryoliposuction are prescribed with an interval between each in one month, therefore a complete disposal of the fat layer can be expected after the full course.

However, the effect will be noticeable after the first cryolipolysis procedure after a certain period of time, required to remove the fat cells that have broken up under the action of cold.

The main characteristics of cryolipolysis are soft impact and high efficiency. The equipment intended for coolsculpting is distinguished by a high level of reliability and safety, and allows to control the level of cooling of fat cells with a high degree of accuracy for achieving maximum effect.

Cryolipolysis is an absolutely painless procedure, during which you can feel a slight numbness and a feeling of cold. During the time of one session of cryolipolysis, fat folds decrease, at least by 25%.

The method of coolsculpting is attractive in that it does not have a long recovery period and side effects, but its use is limited by a number of contraindications, which you can learn about from our clinic specialists by registering for a consultation.
