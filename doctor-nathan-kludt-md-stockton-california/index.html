Can anything be done for a tummy tuck that is still extremely tight and rock hard after 5 years?
Tummy Tuck
Dec 26, 2018
Thank you for your question and sorry to hear about your concerns. It is unlikely that things will change significantly 5 years out from surgery. I would recommend you seek a second opinion consultation from a board certified plastic surgeon, so they may determine what options you have. An in person examination and review of your previous medical records will be important. Best of luck, Dr. Kludt
Why are my hair follicles on my pubic mound sensitive after my tummy tuck?
Healing after TT
Nov 13, 2018
There is always swelling after surgery that can cause your nerves to not function properly. As the swelling goes down the nerves begin to “wake up” and you can have some different sensations until things return to normal. Be sure to bring this up with you plastic surgeon at your next visit, so they may address your specific concern after examining you. Best, Dr. Kludt

Soreness after surgery
Sep 10, 2018

How can I tell if my doctor did a full muscle repair above and below the navel area?
Abdominal Plication
Sep 6, 2018
Normally plication of the abdominal muscles is from the xiphoid (just below the ribs) down to the top of the pubic bone (not including the pelvic floor). Your surgeon should be able to tell you how they performed your operation. If you are considering a second opinion, be sure to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon. Best of luck, Dr. Kludt
Horrendous scars after Benelli lift – can anything be done?
Scarring
Sep 6, 2018
Unfortunately, this can occur with the type of lift that was performed. After one year the scarring will likely not change significantly. In order to “revise” the scar you would likely need a revision lift. Be sure to consult with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss your options.Best, Dr. Kludt
