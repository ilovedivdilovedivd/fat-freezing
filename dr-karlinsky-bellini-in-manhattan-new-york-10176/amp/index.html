There is no problem with using a small amount of dermal filler to soften your folds.  Just make sure to go slowly and not do more then one syringe to start with .  Most likely that is all you will need for full correction.

Advise on facial fillers? Issues: jowls when not smiling, crooked nose, minimal jaw or c
Advise on fillers.
Sep 26, 2019
First of all, you are a beautiful woman and do not need to look like anyone else.  The mild volume loss you mention in your face can be corrected with small amounts of filler.  You can also use dermal filler to correct your minimal nasal deformity as well.  
Does “scarless mole” removal really work? If so which techniques minimize scarring?
Scarless mole
Sep 24, 2019
The removal of any mass on the face will typically leave minimal scarring due to vast vascularity in the face.  The skin on the face is very forgiving and heals very well.  With that said, any mode of removal of the mass will leave you with a mark of some sort which will fade over time.  In many cases, it is better to have a clean linear scar then a wide mark from a laser.  Discuss with your doctor what may give you better aesthetic results.
Can you advise on a mole on side of my face that is getting pretty big?
Mole on face
Sep 24, 2019
While this mole looks benign on the photo, in general, any growth on the body that starts to grow, change color, become irregular in its borders, should be immediately removed.  
What’s the best treatment to remove brown, age spots?
Age spots treatment
Sep 24, 2019
In my practice we get excellent results with BBL Forever Young and Halo Pro.  BBL is a very powerful  light which draws out the brown pigment and Halo is a laser which helps to further improve the color and texture of the skin.  
Will resurfacing create keloids?
Keloids with lasers
Sep 23, 2019
I would be very careful with lasers if you have a history of keloid scars.  Make sure you go to someone who has allot of experience with lasers.  Consider doing very mild treatments multiple times vs. one aggressive treatment.  Do a test treatment in an area which can be easily concealed if an issue arises.
