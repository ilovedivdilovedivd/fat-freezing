Ultrasound And Lipofreeze

US liposuction is a non-surgical method of the body correction. Ultrasound liposuction (non-surgical liposuction) is convenient, comfortable, painless and very effective.

Description of the procedure

Liposuction without surgery is the removal of fat deposits by ultrasonic waves in men and women with medium and excess weight. It is carried out on the newest equipment UltraShape Contour I of the newest generation. All procedures are painless.

Indications for the procedure Ultrasound Lipo:

Indications for non-surgical liposuction are fat deposits in various areas in men and women.

Our specialists will help you to determine whether the equipment and technology for Ultrasound liposuction procedure is suitable for you.

The technology of ultrasound liposuction:

The procedure is performed in the clinic on the apparatus of the newest generation UltraShape Contour I. Under the influence of ultrasonic focused waves, membranes of fat, up to 500 ml per procedure (2-4 cm in volume) are destroyed.

Destroyed fat cells are removed from the body through the liver in a natural way, since there is no difference between fat after the procedure or coming from food. The recommended course is at least three procedures.

To achieve the maximum possible effect of ultrasound, it is recommended to adhere to a certain diet without fried and fatty (so that the liver can effectively remove the fat cells destroyed during the procedure), as well as several procedures for general massage or lymph drainage (for faster removal of fat cells from the problem areas to the liver).

Results after the procedure of ultrasound liposuction:

Liposuction without surgery is a safe weight loss by the natural forces of your body. Fat cells, which are destroyed by ultrasonic waves in problem areas, will never recover, so they do not grow new fat deposits. After the first procedure, the problem area is reduced by 4 cm. The subsequent procedures are carried out 3 weeks after the previous one.

Advantages of ultrasound liposuction:

  • Without surgery;
  • Without pain and discomfort;
  • Outpatient (between sessions you lead a habitual way of life);
  • Visible result after the first procedure;
  • Absence of bruises, scars in the area affected;
  • Safety for the body – ultrasound does not affect other tissues, organs, blood vessels.

Contraindications to non-surgical ultrasound liposuction

  • Pregnancy,
  • an artificial pacemaker,
  • prostheses of the hip, knee joints,
  • metal structures in the impact zone
  • hernia of the abdomen and divergence of the rectus abdominis muscles,
  • damage to skin in the affected area,
  • severe diseases of the immune system,
  • violation of blood clotting,
  • chronic hepatitis,
  • renal insufficiency.

The course of the procedure of ultrasound lipo:

The problem zone on the patient’s body is marked with a marker and medical tape. Treating this area with a special gel, the doctor directs the circular nozzle through which the ultrasound “hits” on the fat cells.

Although the procedure is carried out only by experienced doctors, but the intelligent device also heals the patient in addition. The computer calculates the impact zones to within a micron so that the directed wave that “shoots” the fat cells does not hit the same target twice.

If you point the device at an already processed point, it simply does not turn on. This protects the patient from the so-called “washboard” effect, which is often manifested after surgical liposuction due to the fact that the fat has been removed unevenly. The sensation in the patients during the procedure is an easy tingling or none at all.

Cryolipolysis is selective and strictly controlled cooling of adipose tissue without physical destruction of cells during the procedure. LipoFreeze is an innovative device that allows you to reduce the amount of fat tissues with a high degree of safety and comfort by cryolipolysis. Adipose tissue is reduced by 25% in one session!

How does LipoFreeze work?

Lipofreeze performs first heating for 5 minutes, then controlled cooling of fat tissue for one hour. Such a double effect allows selective action on individual parts of the body and for 1-2 sessions to achieve a sustained reduction of even the most persistent fatty deposits. Since the cleavage of fat cells occurs naturally and gradually, there are no problems with sagging of the skin or the formation of its irregularities.

How often should I do the procedure?

For one zone, only 1-2 procedures are sufficient with a periodicity of 2 months! 2 weeks after the procedure, you can already observe a decrease in volume. But the best results will be visible in 6-8 weeks after the procedure.

How long does the result last?

The thickness of the fold does not increase for two years. Obviously, in this situation we can give a standard recommendation. If you want to keep the result for longer – it’s better to lead a healthy lifestyle.

What sensations can be in the moment and after procedure?

The procedure of cryolipolysis lasts 45-60 minutes, depending on the area of exposure. This technique is painless. In the first few minutes, you may feel some discomfort due to the tension of the tissue with a vacuum applicator.

Also, there may be a slight feeling of cold. After the end of the coolsculpting procedure, slight redness on the skin and an increase in sensitivity on the treated area are possible. These manifestations take place within a few hours. Immediately after the procedure of cryolipolysis, you can lead a habitual way of life without any discomfort. In some cases, there may be small bruises that dissolve within the next 1-2 days.
