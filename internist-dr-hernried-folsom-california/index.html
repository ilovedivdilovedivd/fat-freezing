Is it safe to use my infrared sauna one week post IPL now and will it help with healing?
sauna after IPL
Apr 21, 2019
I would not recommend using a wet or dry Sauna so close to an IPL. Your skin remains in a healing phase and is vulnerable to thermal injury. Think of IPL as a “controlled burn”–you wouldn’t want to add intense heat after a burn. Also, you want to avoid/minimize flushing if the IPL treatment was used to treat red vessels. Let everything settle down….I would recommend one month.
