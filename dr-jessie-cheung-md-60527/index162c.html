Is Xeomin a good filler for lines around both top and bottom lips lines?
Fillers and Xeomin
May 23, 2019
Xeomin is not a wrinkle filler, but a wrinkle relaxer. Injecting Xeomin, or any other botulinum toxin, to the thin vertical lines around the lips will relax the underlying muscle, which will to prevent the lines from getting any deeper. A filler can also be injected to fill in the actual wrinkles, or even deeper, to help smooth the lines out. Xeomin and filler are a great combination for this area, since Xeomin will help the filler last longer.
Should cheeks fillers be done under or over the muscle?
Cheek Filler
May 7, 2019
Great question - we find that when fillers are injected under the muscle, such as with the Y-Lift technique, the fillers last much longer and appear more structural. However, it is technically much easier to inject fillers over the muscle, and most providers aren't trained to inject under the muscle. You will have great results with either technique, but if you're seeking longevity, seek out a Y-Lift provider.
