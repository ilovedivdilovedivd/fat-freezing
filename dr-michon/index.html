Should I get cheek filler even tho it moved the first time?
Cheek filler question
Nov 26, 2020
Dermal filler migration after injections at the cheek area is rare, but not impossible. If it was fine and you are now developing lumps , likely you have reactive late-onset nodules, with a prevalence rate at about 0.5% ( or less) after dermal fillers injection. I recommend that you consult with your aesthetic practitioner for an assessment, diagnosis and treatment plan. It is possible to get them dissolved and have future injections. All the best,
Dr Michon
