Coolsculpting Upper Abs

Cryolipolysis upper abs is not a way to lose weight, so your weight should be close to ideal before the procedure.

If you still have extra pounds, wait. Cryolipolysis will be more useful towards the end of a long journey called “weight loss”. Coolsculpting will help to achieve the ideal outlines of the body and add the finishing touches to your work on yourself.

For the procedure to be effective, it is necessary to have a sufficient amount of soft fat. The fat fold is drawn into the applicator during cryolipolysis. There are nozzels of three different sizes.

How many procedures will be needed?

Some people are helped by one procedure: patients leave happy with a reduction in the fat layer by 20%.

Other people need to go through three procedures and more, repeatedly performing them every two months or less.

Tell the doctor about your expectations. The doctor will draw up a plan of procedures. And you will also learn how much money you will need to spend.

About 10 percent of patients experience pain after the procedure. You can feel just fine all the time, but pain can occur anywhere from a few days to several weeks. Know that you always need to be prepared for any side effects.

Those patients who experience severe pain, doctors recommend taking painkillers. Only one part of the body (for example, the upper or lower abdomen, one side, one side of the waist) may be subject to an hourly procedure.

You will simply sit or lie down throughout the procedure, so if you want to process several zones, the time will increase substantially. Bring a book or tablet with you, so as not to get bored.

After the procedure

Pay attention to your body. Some side effects are absolutely normal: pain, itching, swelling. But if you are concerned, tell your doctor about it. Still it is necessary to remember constantly that side effects vary depending on a zone of influence.

The most problematic zone is the abdomen. Patients most often complain of numbness, tingling and pain just after the procedure on the abdomen. The procedure in other areas, such as the hips, back and waist, usually does not cause almost no side effects.

CoolSculpting Applies A Vacuum To Your Fatty Area

To see the result, it will take some time. So it may be hard to notice that the procedure is working. Often take photos of the zones on which cryolipolysis was done, then compare them and notice that the changes still have a place to be.

Have patience

Results can become noticeable in one month, and after four. You can start to notice the results soon after three weeks, but really noticeable changes will appear in two months. Your body will still get rid of fat cells and will continue to do this for 4-6 months after the procedure.

So it’s important to stay patient and give your body time to get rid of fat cells.

Long-term result depends only on you

As with any cosmetic procedure, the duration of the results will depend on your behavior in the future. If you do not eat properly and do not exercise, cryolipolysis will not save you from weight gain (although it may help not to store fat in the places where the procedure was done).

If you want to look great in the long-term perspective, stick to proper nutrition and lead an active lifestyle.
