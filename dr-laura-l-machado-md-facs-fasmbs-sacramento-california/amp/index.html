Is it possible to run the CoolSculpting applicator for less than the full 50 mins?
CoolSculpting Cycle Times
Jun 24, 2019
The CoolSclupting cycle is based upon years of research and development. The cycle times are specific for each applicator. If your are worried about too much of a reduction, the applicator size should be adjusted, never the cycle time.
P.S. I have never had a patient complain of too much reduction. It is important that your physician is experienced in the correct application to avoid any contour defects.
I have Protein S deficiency and never had any clots. Can I get CoolSculpting?
Protein S deficiency
Jun 24, 2019
Protein S deficiency increases your risk of blood clots. You would receive special medications to prevent excess clotting after surgery. Coolsculpting is non-surgical and there are no contraindications for you. Good luck!
Will 30 minutes on each leg be enough to reduce my saddle bags?
Outer Thigh Treatment Times
Jun 24, 2019
The outer thigh application is 75 minutes for each side. This is one of the areas we find is highly reactive, meaning patients see some real changes from the first treatment that appear over 3 months. It is likely that you can get the results you are looking for in one treatment, but time will tell. The outer thigh applicator is the coldest applicator and when placed correctly and can yield excellent results!

When one is affected with PAH post CoolSculpting, is it obvious?
PAH
Jun 17, 2019
PAH is a known, but rare event after Coolsculpting. It needs to be evaluated and diagnosed by the physician associated with your treatments. It tends to occur most commonly in patients of Polynesian descent. It is quite obvious when present and is treated by liposuction. I would definitely seek evaluation and follow up if you have concerns.

How best to use “treatments”
Jun 17, 2019
In general, you need to completely treat an area to get the best results. Don’t try to split treatments and spread it out too far. Any experienced provider can give you a realistic idea on the number of sessions needed for a given area. Pictures would help us give you a better idea.

How Long Does the Coolsculpting Procedure Last and How Many Treatments Are Needed?
Coolsculpting Treatments
May 29, 2019
The Coolsculpting treatments are an FDA approved procedure that destroys 25-30% of the fat cells in the treated area permanently. However, you still have fat cells in the area that can continue to grow unless you follow a good healthy diet. The amount of treatments depend on the amount of fat in the targeted area. Consult your nearest Certified Coolsculpting provider for a consult of the areas you are concerned with.
