I had CoolSculpting done in Nov 2022. I feel I have gotten even bigger in the area
After CS Area Is Bigger
Aug 9, 2023
HI. Thanks for your question. It is possible for an area treated by CS to get bigger, rather than smaller, but this is not common. This is called PAH, which is Paradoxical Adipose Hyperplasia, and occurs approximately 1 in 3000. Your 5kg weight gain could also explain why the area looks bigger.
I suggest you return to the place where you were treated and be evaluated. If there were pre-treatment photos, that can help.
Best of luck.
