Optimal Results Will Be Seen At 3 Months

Coolsculpting – How soon do you see results

It would be unlikely that you are seeing a result from Coolsculpting in just one week, and I agree that you are probably just noticing the relative difference from the swelling going down.

Coolsculpting works by freezing the fat to a critical temperature until the fat undergoes apoptosis (cell death) and then it is removed from the body by way of the lymphatic system.

This process takes about 3 months to be complete. We do have many patients saying they see beginning results at 2 to 4 weeks and we use a radiofrequency device to heat and manipulate the fat to accelerate the process and tighten the skin.

But it still takes at least 2 weeks to really start to see the meaningful results that Coolsculpting produces. So be patient and soon enough you will see your new body taking shape. (Paul Flashner, MD, Boston Physician)

If you were to gain a considerable amount of weight it might be noticed more elsewhere. Cool sculpting is ideal for people within 20-30lbs of their ideal/goal weight. Most physicians will do a free consult for you to discuss your body specifically. (Nicholas Gentry, MD, Fayetteville Physician)

CoolSculpting permanently removes fat cells in the treated area. If weight gain occurs, the remaining cells in that area can get larger, but will not multiply.
