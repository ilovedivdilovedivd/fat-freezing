This is a large tattoo and likely will have some left after treatment.
Dec 18, 2017
Tattoos are difficult if there is significant amounts of ink and this one seems to have quite a bit.  While removal is definitely possible, there will likely be some tattoo left whatever the case.  And it will require multiple sessions that are challenging and costly.  Having said that, there is no reason not to try, but it won't be perfect.  Best wishes!
