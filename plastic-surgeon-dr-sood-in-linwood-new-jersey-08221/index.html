Laser Hair Removal
Nov 2, 2018
No, threading will remove the hair follicle completely from the skin at its root - rendering the IPL useless. In order for the light to target and destroy the hair, you must leave the root beneath the surface of the skin. Shaving the area is the only appropriate form of preparation for any type of laser hair removal, including an IPL treatment.
