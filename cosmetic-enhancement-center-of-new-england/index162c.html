Will Coolsculpting on my lower abdomen affect my IUD (Mirena)?
No deep effects of CoolSculpting
Jul 13, 2017
Hi wik9248. No, absolutely not. The CoolSculpting device only treats the superficial fat layer. In other words, it will not affect the muscle or underneath the muscle in the abdomen which is where your uterus is located and your IUD is placed.
