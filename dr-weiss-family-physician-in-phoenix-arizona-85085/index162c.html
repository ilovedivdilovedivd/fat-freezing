Clear & Brilliant Orginal Vs Permea, any suggestions?
Either wavelength can improve pore size
Jul 9, 2017
The Clear + Brilliant Original (1440 nm wavelength) is intended to stimulate collagen, thus reducing pore size and improving fine lines/texture. That said, in my practice, we also find that the Permea hand piece (1927 nm wavelength), which is intended to treat pigmentation, such as melasma/sun spots, also improves texture and pores. Therefore, if you have enlarged pores as well as desire correction of skin pigmentation, I would suggest using the Permea. Good luck!

Clear and Brilliant and IPL together?
Combination therapy is safe and effective
May 3, 2017
For many patients, depending upon Fitz skin type, the combination of IPL and Clear + Brilliant can be safely performed. Be sure to have these treatments performed by a provider who is experienced in the use of combination therapies with these two devices. I would recommend having your IPL done before the C+B treatment for best results. This is a safe and effective method to treat dyschromia as well as stimulate collagen.

Yes, you can safely combine the Hydrafacial MD with microneedling the same day. We would recommend treating with the Hydrafacial MD first, prior to microneedling, as a skin ‘prep’ so-to-speak.
