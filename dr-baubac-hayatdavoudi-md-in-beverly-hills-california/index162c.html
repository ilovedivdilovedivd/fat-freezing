Dr. Hayatdavoudi has a keen interest in both the artistic and the humanistic aspects of hair restoration. The combination of his artistic eye, surgical skills, and attention to detail has resulted in him being one of the most sought-after hair restoration surgeons in his field.

He and his staff work to ensure that each one of their patients’ goals will not only be met, but will be surpassed with the highest level of care, compassion, and excellence. His stellar reputation is a result of the passion and dedication he has for making sure every surgery creates the best possible outcomes.

During his undergraduate studies at the University of California, Santa Barbara, Dr. Hayatdavoudi immersed himself in a variety of artistic philosophies while pursuing photography. This helped him to develop his artistic eye, a skill that would later lend itself to his surgical work.

Dr. Hayatdavoudi is board certified by the American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery.
