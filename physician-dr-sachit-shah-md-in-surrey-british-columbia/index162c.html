Can I get Juvéderm Voluma on top of Radiesse?
Be careful about mixing your fillers
Oct 11, 2019
If you have had your radiesse done and then you put voluma on top just be careful. It has to be layered very carefully. If there is any mixing with the vycross (voluma) filler it can be troublesome. There have been reports of increased nodules. I would get a plan together of exactly what you want to achieve and plan it out over a period of time that financially works for you. Doing one syringe of different fillers at a time may lead to problems.
Best Wishes
