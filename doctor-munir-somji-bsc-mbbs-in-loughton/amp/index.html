6 weeks post laser hair removal burns. Unfortunately 4% hydroquinene is only available privately
Hydroquinone for laser hair removal burns
Jun 3, 2019
4% hydroquinone is widely available at most reputable clinics. I commonly prescribe hydroquinone for post inflammatory hyperpigmentation. There are also however other de pigment creams which can also be used in the area. My advice is to consult with the doctor at your clinic or have a consult elsewhere.
What does the scalp look like immediately after surgery?
Scalp post surgery
Jun 3, 2019
The white bumps post op are normal. It represents the epidermis surrounding the follicle. Over the next few days this will subside. Sometimes if you soak the grafts excessively this makes it look worse. Some patients bleed more than others. As always my advice is to send pictures to your surgeon for inspection or go back to the clinic for a review if you are concerned.
How deep must Botox be injected for masseture reduction?
Botox in masseter
Jun 3, 2019
Generally Botox should be injected into the muscle belly which is not superficial. My advice is to wait 2 weeks for the effects and top up if necessary
Over 1 year post op hair transplant, do I have bad results? Should I remove some grafts with IPL
Bad results
Mar 4, 2019
I disagree that your results are terrible. You have good growth of grafts. There is a lack of transition to your temple area which can be improved with a blending surgery. Implanting follicles into strategic areas will soften the hairline. This would convert your hairline from a hairline which is regularly irregular to becoming irregularly irregular which is much more natural.
Do you think my hair will grow back in this area?
Overharvested donor area
Mar 4, 2019
Unfortunately the area appears to be overharvested. This is unlikely to occur if you went to a reputable clinic. It is unlikely that you will get much more growth in the area. You will have to change your tact and attend a reputable clinic for correction. This may consist of corrective surgery and/or scalp micropigmentation.
