How many doctors in this website are acknowledging that hyaluronic acid fillers persist longer than claim
Truth
Dec 15, 2022
HA fillers do last longer than stated.Sometimes we use MRI but MRIS are hard to get approved and take a long time. We are now using Ultrasound more and more.
Is this swelling filler and why would this be happening nearly two years later?
Delayed edema
Dec 15, 2022
Delayed swelling after filler is a well known side effect. Even small amounts of filler can migrate down and accumulate fluid. You should see your board certified dermatologist. The eczema should be evaluated as well.
Voluma in my chin - was this a poor job? Should I go back and get more Voluma?
Botox
Dec 12, 2022
This might be from muscle pulling your chin down. I would discuss neuromodulators with your doctor for this area.

Fillers
Dec 12, 2022
All of the restylane fillers are hyaluronic acid. You should try to see if you can get a Vycross product like Volbella which lasts longer.
Can you help with chronic issue/dry lips after Restylane Kysse?
Cheilitis
Dec 12, 2022
Hyaluronic acid fillers actually help hydrate the lips. I wonder if you truly had Kysse? If you did you might be having contact dermatitis and need patch testing. I would see a board certified dermatologist.
Coolsculpting vs other treatments to look into for my abdomen?
Liposuction
Dec 12, 2022
I think for permanent results I would consider looking into liposuction. It’s permanent and can be done safely in the office. Often times it can be cheaper if not the same cost as coolsculpting.

Dec 12, 2022
It’s hard to say if it’s muscle. Does it tense like muscle? I would try higher doses of Botox with direction insertion into the muscle. Definitely see a board certified dermatologist!
