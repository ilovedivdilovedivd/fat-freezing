Coccon is a modern equipment for non-invasive fat removal using cryolipolysis technique.

Coccon fat freezing methodology is based on controlled local cooling of adipose tissue and additional vacuum effects.

Fat cells have an increased sensitivity to low temperatures, so when the tissue site cools and the vacuum effect slows the blood flow, the apoptosis-cell death process begins.

In this case, the surrounding tissue is not damaged in any way. Studies have also demonstrated that when cooled to -2 ° C to 2 ° C, fat cells are destroyed forever.

At a temperature of about 7 ° C, most cells are metabolized, and at a temperature of about 14 ° C there are no changes.

Technology

Cryolipolysis by the Coccon device is a new procedure, based on precise and controlled cooling of subcutaneous fat. The technique is designed to reduce unwanted local fat deposits and simulate body contours WITHOUT invasive interference.

The method is based on the increased sensitivity of adipocyte cells (adipocytes) to cold. During the procedure, the subcutaneous fat is cooled to a certain temperature and the blood flow in it decreases due to the vacuum, as a result of which the apoptosis process begins.

Frozen Fat Removal With Cocoon

This is a gradual programmable physiological cell death (Selective Cryolysis). Low temperature damages fat cells, while surrounding tissues and internal organs are not injured. The decomposition products of subcutaneous fat are gradually eliminated from the body during natural metabolic processes.

Thus, the final effect after the procedure is estimated after 1-3 months (on average, up to 80% of adipose tissue in the area of exposure is destroyed). The duration of one procedure depends on the indications, but on average 50-60 minutes for one zone.
