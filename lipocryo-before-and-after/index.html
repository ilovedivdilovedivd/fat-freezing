LipoCryo Before And After

LipoCryo is the technology of the ultimate disposal of fatty deposits! It is the only analog of Zeltiq in the whole world!

The LipoCryo system works on the principle of controlled selective cooling of fat cells, which results in a very noticeable and natural decrease in the fat layer in the waist, abdomen, back, arms, buttocks and sides, during the atraumatic sampling procedure.

How does the LipoCryo machine work?

A special sensor is placed in the selected area to combine the vacuum action to cover the subcutaneous adipose tissue and the selective action of the cryotechnology to cool it.

Subcutaneous tissues and fat cells are cells that are saturated with fatty acids, and therefore they are particularly sensitive to cold; and adjacent elements, such as the peripheral nervous system, blood vessels, fibrocytes, melanocytes or unsaturated fat cells, unlike fat cells, are much less sensitive to cold.

This characteristic to a certain extent affects the removal of adipocytes (fat cells) without various damages to surrounding tissues.

LipoCryo Is A Fat Freezing Process Which Helps To Freeze Fats At Target Areas

How does the process of removing fat cells?

The process of removal of adipocytes as a result of the action of the LipoCryo device takes place by crystallization, followed by a stage – apoptosis and further dissolution of cells with a gradual release of fats over a period of two to six weeks.

How many procedures are needed?

It all depends on the thickness of the fat deposits and getting the desired result, it will take from one to three procedures. The necessary time between the two procedures is about 45 days.

How soon will I see results after Lipo Cryo treatments?

Depending on the initial fat thickness, the average loss will be approximately 3 to 15 mm; in percentage terms the equivalent of fat loss is about 25% to 45%. Approximately 40% of the result will be visible after only 15 days.

But it will be necessary to wait, at least six weeks, in order to see the average result. At this time, and will decide on the appointment of the next session.

LipoCryo Is The Only Non-surgical And Patented Fat-reduction Procedure

How long does the treatment last?

Depending on the size of the treatment area, more than one procedure on the Lipo Cryo device may be required to process the entire zone.

For example, you may need two procedures for the abdominal area, one for the waist on both sides, and two for the lower back.

Each procedure lasts approximately 25 – 35 minutes, depending on the thickness of the fat deposits. Although this procedure can last from 20 to 180 minutes, depending on the size of the problem area and the thickness of fat tissue.

Who is the ideal candidate for Lipo Cryo procedure?

The ideal candidate for Lipo Cryo procedure is not a patient with general obesity, but simply a person who wants to slightly reduce body fat at certain specific locations, for example, in the abdomen, lower back, waist, arms and buttocks.

LipoCryo Procedure Involves Controlled Cooling Of The Adipose Tissue

What can the patient feel during and after the procedure on the Lipo Cryo device?

This procedure is not painful for patients, they will notice only a slight stress near the area of exposure due to the vacuum effect, and also a local intense cold sensation, especially at the beginning of the procedure. And after the procedure, you can observe redness and numbness in the area of exposure, which can last several hours. It is also possible a mild hematoma, which disappears within a few days. The patient can return to normal life immediately after the procedure without much discomfort.

What is the main distinguishing feature of Lipo Cryo compared to other procedures?

The Lipo Cryo system for selective and controlled cooling of fat cells results in a noticeable natural reduction in body fat, eliminates the traumatic nature of invasive methods (such as liposuction), and prevents the possibility of destructive effects of radio frequency, localized ultrasound or laser signals on surrounding tissues.

Are the results of the procedure specific?

Adipocytes that have been removed with the Lipo Cryo device will first gradually release the fats before they are absorbed during the natural metabolism of the body.
