How many CoolSculpting sessions can you have in one area?
Inner thighs
Mar 4, 2019
You sound like the ideal candidate for Coolscupting. But if your inner thighs were treated in the same area all three times I would not continue to treat again with coolscupting. I would have the area re-evaluated to determine if the issue is fat or laxity. If fat is the issue I also use Kybella or liposuction in this area. Can I have Emsculpt done with a copper IUD?
Emsculpt with IUD
Feb 21, 2019
Emsculpt uses noninvasive HIFEM (High-Intensity Focused Electromagnetic) technology to induce powerful muscle contractions. The depth of penetration is 7cm. The company recommends no metal in the body, but your IUD depth is well beyond 7 cm and so you should be fine to have the treatment. Hope this helps.

Hope this helps
Is pink skin normal after revepeel?
Peel healing
Jan 5, 2019
It is normal to be pink right after the peel. Please continue the balm and be sure to avoid any sun exposure. The skin may then turn brown and then peel off. Don’t pick, just let skin flake off.
