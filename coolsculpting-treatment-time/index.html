We have available many different sized and shaped applicators, so would need to work out what combination is most appropriate for you and your concerns.

Also, you may need 2 sessions(a few weeks apart), depending on how much is there. So, you could need 2,4, or even 8 applications, depending on your problem. CoolSculpting has been a fantastic addition to my practice, and we have really happy patients. You will only know what is best for you if you go and get a skilled professional assessment. (Mark Edinburg, MBBCH, FRACS, Sydney Plastic Surgeon)

Coolsculpt for a few hours and see results within 3 months that can last a lifetime

How long you need to be at your dermatologist’s office for coolsculpting depends on the areas you want treated and the number of devices in the doctor’s office. The suction end of the device is called an applicator.

There are many different applicators of different sizes and shapes to fit different area of fat – large, small, over a flat area, slightly rounded area or deeply curved.

The time an applicator needs to be on to complete the ‘cycle’ (treatment) varies between 35 and 120 minutes.

With two machines (dualsculpting), two areas that don’t overlap and don’t pull on each other can be treated at the same time. For example, both hips or one hip and the opposite upper abdomen can be treated simultaneously in the same cycle time.

However, it would be unusual for an upper and lower abdomen to be treated simultaneously. So if you want to do your upper and lower abdomen and flanks, depending on your size and shape, the treatment could take the time of 3 or 4 cycles with dualsculpting.

Your physician and her staff can give you more precise details based on the plan they make for you.

Remember to factor in the time between cycles for the staff to either manually massage, or use radiowave (the z wave zimmer we use in our office) to break up the ice crystals to start the process of destroying the unwanted fat.

Some patients like to get as many cycles done in one day they can, others break up the treatments over weeks based on their schedules. However you do it, in about a month you will start to see the fat reducing and by three months get your results.

If you like what you see and want more, that is the time to retreat those areas to truly noninvasively sculpt your body. (Heidi A. Waldorf, MD, New York Dermatologist)

Permanent Fat Removal

CoolSculpting is an FDA approved device. Please make sure you choose a an experienced physician and medical spa to perform your treatment. Depending on the area and the lifestyle of the person, the amount of time it lasts can vary.

The device is attaches to the abdomen and flanks (upon other areas of the body). Each area takes one hour of “freezing” time. Cooling is applied causing the fat cell to undergo apoptosis (death of a cell).

The fat cells begin to die and break down. The broken down cell is then digested by Macrophages, and your body will naturally eliminate the dead cells. Once the Fat cells are destroyed they will not come back.

If you gain weight after CoolSculpting the remaining fat cells may enlarge as they absorb excess fat. Most patients see results after one treatment, but certain people may require more treatments depending on desired results. (Stephen T. Greenberg, MD, Woodbury Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting – time of treatment and longevity of results

Until recently all CoolSculpting treatments required one hour. Zeltiq, the makers of CoolSculpting, then introduced CoolSmooth Pro – a flat panel for treating outer thighs that takes 1 hour 15 minutes. Last year CoolMini was introduced for the submentum (under the chin, affectionately known as the “turkey neck”), which is a 45 minute treatment.

This year the traditional applicators have undergone a transformation and are now known as CoolAdvantage applicators — which take either 35 or 45 minutes each (depending on the size). So basically your treatment could range anywhere from 35 to 75 minutes, depending on the applicator.

Some offices, such as ours, offer DualSculpting where we can attach two CoolSculpting devices at once, so you can have double the treatment in half the time. The number of fat cells we have is from adolescence (and pregnancy).

The fat cells that are frozen during CoolSculpting go through a process where they die off and are eliminated from the body. Therefore the fat is gone for good. CoolSculpting has been proven in FDA studies to eliminate at least 20% of the fat cells in the affected area.

Twenty percent may not seem like much to you, but viewing before and after photos will give you a better idea of the significant results! (Joseph E. Mark, MD, Rochester Plastic Surgeon)
