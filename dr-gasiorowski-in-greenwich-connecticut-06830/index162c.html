Ultherapy for jawline and jowl lines at 34?
Jul 22, 2015
You are cursed by an artistic eye! Most don't even notice the beginning of sagging until it is well on the way. By starting a therapeutic modality early, your results will be better and longer lasting. The reason is that your collagen is not as broken down and can react during the healing process in a more powerful manner. For the most part, the younger our patient, the better the result.Henry C Gasiorowski MD

What are the consequences/symptoms of Radiesse being injected too superficially?
What are the consequences/symptoms of Radiesse being injected too superficially?
Jul 15, 2015
Lumps from the injection of Radiesse can be common if the dilution of the substance is not correct.or if placed too superficially in the skin. A cortisone injection or a combination of cortisone and 5 flurouracil can be very helpful in the dissolution of the lump. Physical pressure with a sharp needle tip can also help to break up the nodule. Eventually, it can take over a year, the nodule will disappear.H C Gasiorowski MD
