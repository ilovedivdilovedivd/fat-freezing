Results over cost

Most facilities will offer free consultations to see what would meet your needs. (Michalynn Farley, MD, Redding Physician)

Purchasing a treatment package not only gives the patient a price break, it gives the patient excellent results. The larger applicator can treat about 2.5X the area of the smaller applicators and is usually priced around twice the smaller applicator.

If you are treating your stomach and are a candidate for the large applicator it is offers great results and good value. (Sarah A. Mess, MD, Columbia Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting in the MidWest, US Starts at $1500

Coolsculpting represents a significant advance in the treatment of problem fat collections. The technique has been clinically proven and has FDA approval. The cost of coolsculpting is dependent upon multiple variables, but generally starts at about $1500 when a single area is treated with a large applicator.

Significant discounts occur when multiple areas are treated and when single areas are treated multiple times. Unfortunately, price shopping is difficult, because each provider structures fees differently. In many cases it becomes an apples and oranges comparison.

Not only is coolsculpting cost effective, but is also safe and has virtually no downtime. In cases where larger amounts of fat are present, other techniques like liposuction may be more cost effective.

The best way to determine the real cost of coolsculpting is to consult a plastic surgeon who can provide the complete spectrum of therapeutic options for treating localized fat collections. (Richard J. Bruneteau, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)
