Slim Lipo vs. Cool Sculpting for Belly Fat and Love Handles?
Slim lip of and Coolsculpting are completely different treatments
Jul 8, 2017
Slim lip of and Coolsculpting are completely different treatments. Slim lipo is invasive and is more akin to traditional liposuction. Coolsculpting is non-invasive. The candidates are completely different too. Slim lipo can remove larger areas of fat and has downtime. Coolsculpting is better for smaller localized areas of fat and has no downtime. You should have a consult to get accurate pricing and see what makes more sense for your body.

Long-term Effects of Coolsculpting?
Coolsculpting works by freezing fat
Jul 8, 2017
Coolsculpting works by freezing fat but since fat freezes at a higher temperature, the overlying skin is not injured. Plus it does this in a controlled manner to limit any side effects. The device also naturally feathers the area so there shouldn't be any lumps of applied correctly. Smaller individuals may notice more improvement since the treatment is meant for fat contouring, not for larger fat reduction.

Lump Left After Coolsculpting?
It is possible for a lump to appear in the treatment area several weeks after your procedure.
Apr 18, 2017
It is possible for a lump to appear in the treatment area several weeks after your procedure. It could be inflammation that will gradually dissipate or, as your doctor said, it could be fat. It is best to allow some time to determine whether the lump will resolve on its own. If it's still an issue after 4-6 months, your doctor should take a look at it again and see if there is a need for a repeat procedure. For now, I recommend monitoring the lump before having a second procedure.
