Is it better to get CoolSculpting when thin or heavy?
CoolSculpting works best when you are close to your ideal weight.
Aug 28, 2017
CoolSculpting works best when you are close to your ideal weight, and you have areas of fat that are out of proportion to the rest of your body. You will see about a 20 % reduction in the areas that are treated. But CoolSculpting is not a method of weight reduction.How effective CoolSculpting will be on any area has to do with the nature and amount of fat in these areas.
