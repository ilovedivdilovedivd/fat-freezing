What Is The Cost Of Coolsculpting By Zeltiq?

Price of Coolsculpting by Zeltiq

Zeltiq costs $750 to $1500 per treatment depending on the area and the size of the applicator.

If you only have a small area on the stomach, then it is $750. If your abdomen needs the larger adaptor then the price is $1500.

Love handles, back fat, buttock areas, are all $750 per side. With the new Cool-Advantage adaptors the procedures in most areas take only 35 minutes.

There is a special adaptor for the neck called the Cool-mini which takes one hour and there are special adaptors for the inner and outer thighs as well.

The Cool-mini takes under one hour and costs $1,000 for the area. It is a great safe procedure and my patients have been thrilled with the results. Most of my patients have returned to do other areas because they were so happy with the results.

When treating multiple areas many physicians will offer discounts. (Michele S. Green, MD, New York Dermatologist)

The Cost of Coolsculpting

The cost of Coolsculpting can vary from person to person. The typical cost of a cycle is $750 and is highly protected by Zeltiq. What does this mean to you? I can say that the cost per cycle will be very consistent across the board.

Where the variation occurs is in how many cycles you will need. Are you looking for subtle results or a total transformation? Maybe something in between?

Many locations do have some savings in place for purchasing multiple cycles, including us at Rejuvenate Austin.

We currently provide customized treatment packages, while staying within the guidelines provided by Zeltiq. You will definitely save when purchasing multiple cycles. Care Credit financing is a nice option for deferred interest financing of your procedure.

Consultations are typically complimentary. You should always go to an office that has a doctor on site. (Jessica Wright, MD, Austin General Surgeon)

Cool Sculpting Costs in Atlanta

The cost of fat reduction depends on how much fat you want to lose! It also depends on how you calculate the hidden costs of other treatments for fat. Unlike any type of liposuction, there is no downtime.

There are no garments to wear. No pads to change as you drain fluid. No scars. No cost to laser the scars. There is also no instant (within 3 days) gratification unfortunately. The direct cost of Cool Sculpting is related to the size of the applicator and the number of applications necessary to freeze all the fat you want to reduce.

There are different applicator sizes and the one that is right for you depends on your size and the area you want to treat. The company charges the physician several hundred dollars for each application so the minimal price is fixed by Zeltig.

If you do several areas at one time, most of us discount our list price since we don’t have to clean the room between each session, you can use the same disposable clothing, we only have to take one set of “before” photos, etc.

Our list is $750 for one small applicator and $1400 for one large applicator. The large applicator can freeze more than twice as much as the small ones but if the large one does not fit, you can’t use it.

We take before and after photographs. It is a free consult to determine if Cool Sculpting is right for the patient. We also offer Smart Lipo so we can make sure that our patients have options when considering treatment for fat reduction. (Rutledge Forney, MD, Atlanta Dermatologic Surgeon)
