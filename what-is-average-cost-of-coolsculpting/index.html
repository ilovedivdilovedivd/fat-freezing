Patient factors best determine cost

CoolSculpt patients will pay for “cycles” to treat their particular areas of concern. What a cycle is is basically a small area that is treatable by one of the CoolSculpt applicators.

While the price per cycle ($750 or $1500 depending on the applicator used) is protected by Zeltiq, the number of cycles needed per patient will vary.

The best way to determine your total cost would be to have a consultation appointment with a provider. During your visit, the provider can perform an exam, discuss your concerns and desired outcomes, and answer any questions that you have.

At that point the provider can then let you know how many cycles would likely be needed to accomplish your goals. Keep in mind that each patient may respond differently and an average of 20-30% reduction in the fat cells in the area treated is a reasonable expectation.

Some patients may need to have a second cycle done on areas with more fullness (i.e. the abdomen). The possibility of the need for more cycles should be reviewed during the consultation visit. (Theodore Staahl, MD, Modesto Facial Plastic Surgeon)
