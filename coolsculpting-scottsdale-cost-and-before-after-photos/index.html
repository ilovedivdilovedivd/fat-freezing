Cryolipolysis or cryoliposuction is a hardware method of cosmetology aimed at weight loss and weight reduction by the effect of cold on adipose tissue.

The method is based on the development of American scientists who have shown the weak resistance of fat cells to cold at temperatures from -1 ° C to -5 ° C.

Moreover, all remaining body tissues (nerves, blood vessels, healthy cells) to this cold exposure insensitive.

The principle of the device is to influence the problematic areas on the patient’s body with a special manipulator that generates cold. Under the action of vacuum, the skin is drawn into the manipula and cooled, the natural process of destruction of fat cells is triggered – apoptosis.

It should be noted that the process of losing weight after cryolipolysis occurs smoothly without disrupting the natural functioning of organs and systems, therefore the first results of exposure to problem zones should be expected no earlier than 2-3 weeks after the procedure.

The organism will calmly complete the processes of apoptosis of fatty and / or cellulite cells and will start to please you with noticeably decreased body volumes.

Cryolipolysis is used for:

  • local weight loss;
  • getting rid of cellulite (in combination with ozone and carboxytherapy methods);
  • non-surgical liposuction;
  • body shape correction;
  • elimination of problem areas on the body.

Unlike other equally effective and popular method of losing weight – myostimulation – Coolsculpting allows you to “lose weight locally”, i.e. in those places where it is necessary. Genetically determined places of fat accumulation on the body, and heavily accessible zones for working out can be amenable to correction:

  • stomach;
  • sides at the waist;
  • buttocks;
  • back of thighs;
  • the lateral surface of the thighs is internal or external (so-called “ears” on the hips);
  • the anterior surface of the thighs (the area above the knee);
  • Shoulders (the inner surface of the hands);
  • back.

Coolsculpting is non-invasive, painless and has a minimum of contraindications for carrying out, and therefore is pleasant and comfortable. After a cryolipolysis session, the patient can immediately return to the usual rhythm of life, without falling for several days, as after an operation intervention.

On average, the rate of cryoliposuction is from 3 to 8 sessions; the final number of procedures is selected individually and depends on the initial state of the patient’s skin, its weight, the area on the body that needs correction.

For example, to lose weight belly may need from 6 to 10 procedures cryolipolysis, slimming legs – from 4 to 6 sessions. Cryoliposuction is performed with an interval of 5-7 days, observing the drinking regime and gently correcting the patient’s diet.

You can combine cryolipolysis with procedures of lymph drainage, mesotherapy, training on the vibration platform ProEllixe. In this case, the impact on the body will be complex, and the effect of a combination of procedures – more pronounced.
