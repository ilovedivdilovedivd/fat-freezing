Is It Safe to Get IPL Treatments when Pregnant?
IPL and pregancy
Nov 6, 2015
No, safety on pregnant women and babies have not been studied that well yet, but also hormonal changes may have the risk of more pigmentation or side effects. Avoid unnecessary cosmetic treatments during pregnancy, maybe you may need them later, together with bleaching creams, because of melasma.
How Much Does IPL Cost?
IPL price
Nov 6, 2015
Depends on your geographic location, the brand of IPL, and the person who does it. It is not the same that a Dermatolgist or Plastic Surgeon performs the treatment, compared to a non physican, aesthetician, PA or nurse. Make sure the person doing it is a Dermatologist or Plastic Surgeon, if not, at least that the person doing it is under the supervision of the specialist for better and optimal results, and also for less risks of side effects.
Is IPL Effective for Reducing Enlarged Pores?
IPL for pore size reduction
Nov 6, 2015
In my experience (with Lumenis an BTL IPLs, and some other IPL brands as demos) no, even a lot of IPL manufacturers promise pore improvement. What improves more the pore size are retinoids and azelaic acid, together with fractional laser, which I offer in my practice.
Is PTF or IPL epilation safe for people with Hashimoto's thyroiditis and hypothyroidism
Safety of IPL on thyroiditis
Nov 6, 2015
Yes, it is safe. Just make sure to mention which medications are you currently taking to determine if they are not phototoxic
