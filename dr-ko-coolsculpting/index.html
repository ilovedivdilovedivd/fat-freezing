Can I take antibiotics after CoolSculpting?
Taking antibiotics after CoolSculpting
Jan 5, 2018
Taking antibiotics should not affect the results of CoolSculpting. However, antibiotics are not needed after a CoolSculpting procedure either. The results of CoolSculpting are usually seen at 12 weeks after the procedure. I hope this helps.
