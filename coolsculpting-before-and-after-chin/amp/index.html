Coolsculpting Before And After Chin

Double chin is a serious aesthetic problem, which so often afflicts both women and men. The second chin can appear even in young and slender, because excess weight is not the only reason for its formation.

With the help of diets or exercises to get rid of fatty deposits in the lower third of the face is impossible and the only effective method is Coolsculpting.

Our clinic offers you the procedure of Coolsculpting is the newest technology of non-surgical liposuction of the chin with the help of the Zeltiq apparatus, which can rid you of the second chin without surgery and pain.

The purpose of coolsculpting of the chin is to destroy excess fatty deposits, to rid the patient of the second chin and return to her a young, clearly outlined face.

Coolsculpting Chin Procedure Photo

CoolSculpting is a unique technology of non-surgical liposuction, which destroys fat cells by affecting them with cold. CoolSculpting can effectively destroy and reduce local fat deposits, without traumatizing surrounding tissues.

The technology of cryolipolysis of the chin allows you to cope with excess fat in the lower third of the face without resorting to surgical liposuction – without incisions, swelling and bruising.

The method of coolsculpting is based on the selective effect of low temperatures on fatty tissues. Scientists have found that fat cells are much more sensitive to cold than skin cells and muscles.

Under the influence of low temperatures they are more quickly damaged and split. That is why, after treatment by cold, fat deposits on the chin are markedly reduced.

CoolSculpting procedure is absolutely comfortable, after such liposuction on the face there are no scars, edema or bruises, and you can go to work or any event immediately after the procedure.

How is the CoolSculpting of the chin performed?

An applicator is applied to the fat fold, which captures it with a vacuum. Then, the fat fold is treated with cold, while the temperature of fat tissue falls to -4 degrees, which is harmless to the skin, but is destructive for adipose tissue. During such a chin liposuction, the patient may feel cold and slight tingling in the area of exposure. The procedure of cryolipolysis of the chin lasts about 30 minutes, and after it on the skin there may be slight reddening, which disappears after 2-4 hours.

CoolSculpting can be carried out at any time of the year. The technology used in the clinic with the use of the new Zeltiq apparatus allows, in one procedure, to remove up to 40% of body fat. The effect of the procedure increases for 4 to 6 weeks, as the body displays the fat cells that were destroyed as a result of cryolipolysis. To completely get rid of local fat deposits may require 1 to 3 sessions of coolsculpting.

Advantages of cryolipolysis of the chin:

  • Absolute safety procedures: affecting fat cells, cold without traumatizing the skin and surrounding tissues;
  • Absence of the recovery period: the procedure does not require any rehabilitation and the patient can return to the usual cases immediately from the cosmetologist’s office;
  • High efficiency: in one session, fat deposits in the chin zone are reduced by about 30-40%!
  • Skin lifting: the effects of vacuum and cold have an additional stimulating effect on the skin.
  • After the procedure, it becomes denser, the process of producing your own fibroblasts and collagen synthesis is activated.
  • With the help of CoolSculpting technology, you can get rid of not only the second chin, but also remove fat deposits on the hips, abdomen, waist, back, hands and knees.
