Best enzyme for dissolving Juvederm
Jul 18, 2018
Hyaluronidase is an enzyme that is sometimes used to dissolve hyaluronic acid fillers, such as Juvederm and Restylane. Of all of the available options, I feel Hylenex is the safest option, since there is no risk of an allergic reaction with this particular product. The good news is that Hylenex does not appear to affect the body's natural hyaluronic acid, instead being selective only for the filler.
