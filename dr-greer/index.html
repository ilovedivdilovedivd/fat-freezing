Mons lift after weight loss?
Monsplasty with tummy tuck
Nov 7, 2023
This is a great question! I do monsplasty as part of all my abdominoplasty surgeries- I do lipo over the area if needed, and make the abdominoplasty incision lower so that the mons is lifted.

But... not all surgeons do this. And if you have surgery with someone who isn't "mons aware" it could result in this area looking puffy below your newly flat tummy. I definitely recommend bringing this up at your consultation!

,My question is would I be a candidate for lipo surgery after 2 heart attacks and stints
Liposuction after heart attack and stents
Apr 9, 2019
The answer to your question depends on what kind of stents you have. If you have drug-eluting stents you may need to stay on aspirin for the rest of your life. This would make liposuction unsafe. If you have bare metal stents then you may be able to come off aspirin for surgery. I would check with your cardiologist before seeing a plastic surgeon, and if you have had a heart attack within the last year it may be best to wait on any elective surgery. Best of luck to you!

Is cryolipolysis effective on muscular calves?
Treating calves with CoolSculpting
Mar 19, 2019
CoolSculpting is a great technology, but as a surgeon who does both a significant amount of CoolSculpting and liposuction, I would not recommend CoolSculpting for the lower legs. It is very likely to leave an indentation. Lower legs are best treated with liposuction by a surgeon experienced in lipo, as they can be very difficult to obtain a nice result on. I recommend seeing a board certified plastic surgeon to see if you are a candidate. Best of luck to you!
How do I get rid of a seroma quicker?
Complications after traveling for surgery
Mar 19, 2019
Traveling for surgery can save you money, but as you are unfortunately finding out, if you have a complication you may be stuck. Your best option is truly to return to your operating surgeon. Seromas need to be drained, often several times, before they heal. There is nothing you can do on your own to treat it. And I would not recommend working out at the gym as this could make a seroma worse. Best of luck to you.

Follow-Up Question: How would you know if you had permanent nerve damage after a facelift?
Is numbness permanent after facelift?
Mar 19, 2019
Numbness over the ear is a risk of facelift surgery. Most of the time the numbness is temporary, but it can take months for the sensation to return. If it's only been a month, try to be patients. You'll know if the nerve is recovering if you start to feel pins and needles, or if the numb area becomes gradually smaller. Best of luck to you.

What causes appendicitis?
Mar 19, 2019
Appendicitis is an infection in the appendix, which is a little outpouch from your colon. A tummy tuck only treats the skin and muscles of your abdominal wall- it does not enter the abdominal cavity, and cannot cause appendicitis. Sorry you are going through this, and I hope you feel better!
