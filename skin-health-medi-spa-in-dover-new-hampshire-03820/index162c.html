Would CoolSculpting be effective on my back fat?
100%
Feb 19, 2021
Do it. This is one of our favorite areas to treat with Coolsculpting. Results are not immediate but it should really shrink these areas down in 8-12 weeks.
Would I be a candidate for CoolSculpting or a Laser Assisted lipo?
Coolsculpting first
Feb 19, 2021
I think you’re a a very good candidate for Coolsculpting. It might not “spring” back the skin 100% but I’ve had some pretty great results in patients like yourself. Try that first…it’s much less expensive than Lipo and I think it’ll do the trick!
CoolScuplting & COVID: is it safe to have treatment after the vaccine?
Very likely yes!
Feb 19, 2021
Congrats on receiving the vaccine. For every person vaccinated, that’s one less “link” in the chain of transmission. So I’m happy you received it.

In terms of Coolsculpting, there honestly should be no reason to NOT receive treatment. You might want to wait a couple of days because some people do feel aches and pains after the vaccine so you wouldn’t necessarily want to add a treatment when you’re not feeling great. But after a few days, I saw go for it!
