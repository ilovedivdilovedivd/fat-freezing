Can CoolSculpting be used on back of thighs below buttocks?

CoolSculpting can be used on the back of the thighs just below the buttocks. This is not an unusual area to treat with CoolSculpting. (Michael I. Echavez, MD, San Francisco Facial Plastic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting can be used for this area that is frequently referred to as the “banana roll.” CoolSculpting uses technology that targets and freezes adipocytes, or fat cells. These fat cells die after being frozen and are removed through the bloodstream.

The fat cells are permanently removed, with approximately 20 – 25% of the cells removed at each treatment. The banana roll area most frequently can be addressed in one session. CoolSculpting is perfect for addressing problem areas such as this. (Pamela Kimbrough, MD, Ardmore OB-GYN)
