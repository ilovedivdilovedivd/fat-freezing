Coolsculpting Pittsburgh Cost And Photos

Lose weight quickly, without effort and exactly in the right zones, probably every man dreams.

The cryolipolysis technique on the Zeltiq device is the ideal solution to the problem. Throughout the world, it is recognized as the most effective and fast non-surgical method of reducing fat deposits in problem areas.

Researchers at Harvard Medical School have proven that fat cells are extremely sensitive to cold.

They found out that the “freezing” or local reduction of temperature to 25 degrees causes the natural death of these cells.

In this case, such a temperature does not affect vessels, nerve endings and internal organs.

This discovery allowed scientists to create a completely new technology of the body correction, which was implemented in a unique Zeltiq device. With it you can correct the shape without surgery, pain and discomfort, and with a guaranteed result.

The cryolipolysis procedure on the Zeltiq device is ideal for those who want to get rid of the hated creases on the abdomen, waist or in other areas.

Its effectiveness can be compared with classical liposuction, but without incisions, scars and long-term recovery. In addition, the technology of the procedure is recognized by the FDA and 22.4% more efficient than other hardware methods.

During the procedure, which lasts only an hour, a special vacuum applicator is applied to the problem zone, under which a special gel pad is placed. It ensures uniformity of action and protects the skin from hypothermia.

The applicator draws the fat fold and starts to slowly cool it to the desired temperature. Simultaneously, a roller massage is performed inside the applicator.

It is necessary for the effect to be applied to every fatty cell along the entire depth of the fold.

For one procedure, up to 25% of the fat in the affected area is eliminated. The final disintegration and removal from the body of the slag takes from 3 weeks to 2 months. This is a physiological process that does not affect the liver and kidneys. The procedure is well tolerated and does not require recovery. It is important that the correction zone after coolsculpting looks very natural and smooth. And the results are preserved for life, provided that there is no significant increase in weight.

Up to 98% of patients are fully satisfied with the result of the coolsculpting by Zeltiq, and this is significantly higher than after classical liposuction and other hardware procedures.

SIDE EFFECTS:

  1. a feeling of cold and a slight soreness in the area of the skin fold;
  2. bruises in the treated area, as a reaction to the action of the vacuum.

Duration of result

At the site of exposure, 25 to 50% of all fat cells are destroyed. The thickness of the fat fold decreases by 3-5 cm. In the future, these cells are not replaced with new ones, because the effect of the procedure is stable.

Combination with other procedures:

Coolsculpting is designed in such a way that the patient does not have indications for additional procedures in the recovery period. But they are not contraindicated.

On the contrary, the combination of several differently directed procedures will strengthen the effect of weight loss and local correction of the body.

Ultrasound-cavitation affects the more superficial layers of subcutaneous fat, which contributes to a significant reduction in visible manifestations of cellulite.

  1. Radiolifting helps to smooth and reduce the skin, which is an excellent prevention of sagging.
  2. Mesotherapy improves the condition and appearance of the skin.
  3. Lymph drainage massage and pressotherapy contribute to the elimination of toxic products that are formed after cell death.

This combination of procedures allows you to accelerate the process of losing weight and get the body of dreams!
