Is Smartlipo a good choice for getting rid of my banana rolls or something else?
Smartlipo can be effective but only if used carefully
Jun 15, 2015
SmartLipo can be used to reduce reduce banana rolls, but your surgeon must be very careful to not to be too aggressive in suctioning. If they over-suction, the ligaments holding up the buttock muscle may be damaged. We treat this area all the time, but we make sure to use small-diameter suction cannulas and work very hard to not be too aggressive.
Will smart lipo be effective for a roll-free back or would I get better results with a more intens
SmartLipo for back rolls
May 16, 2015
SmartLipo is an excellent way to improve the area in question. It is relatively simple and safe and is more "intensive" than regular lipo because it simply adds a fat melting/skin tightening laser treatment to the fat prior to suctioning it out. There would be definite improvement. It is really just a question of "how much".

Smart Lipo question
Apr 22, 2015
It is a little difficult from these pictures to tell how much is loose skin and how much is actually fat. If indeed there is residual fat of course lipo would help you, especially with the smart lipo laser done before suctioning which would also help with any skin tightening. I barely felt any pain during Cool sculpting. Does this mean I shouldn't expect much in the way of
Pain and Coolsculpting
Apr 22, 2015
Although many patients feel pain or numbness during or after CoolSculpting, it is not necessary in order to produce results. Results depend upon how cold you get the fat and I am assuming the applicators were placed properly and the temperatures achieved were correct. Results will take 2-3 months to see.
Is it common for Coolsculpting to make surrounding areas carry more fat, or make the untreated are
Uneven Coolsculpting results
Apr 22, 2015
No. You shouldn’t carry more fat in surrounding areas. However, if you flatten a specific area with a cool sculpting applicator then it may appear visually that a non treated adjacent areas looks “fatter”.Of course, if you do either cool sculpting or liposculpture and gain weight afterwards then interrupted areas will gain fat because they have residual fat cells.
Are there different results of cool sculpting if I get the second procedure done three weeks later
Better to wait.
Apr 20, 2015
It is definitely better to wait 8 weeks to see your results before doing another treatment. It doesn’t make sense to put the applicators on a tender swollen area that was just treated 3 weeks prior.

Liposuction should not have a 10% complication rate especially since you probably dont have a lot of fat. Ultherapy of Infini for marionette lines?
Revolumization and Marionette Lines
Apr 16, 2015
Ultherapy, which is focussed ultrasound, does indeed induce tightening of the neck, lower face and upper face, but does not work very well for the marionette folds that run from the corners of the mouth to the chin. Infini, which is focussed radio frequency (RF) is similarly ineffective for these lines.Marionette lines require revolumization, especially in the cheeks and pre jowl sulcus so fillers/volumizers work better.
