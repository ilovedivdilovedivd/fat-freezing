What’s the best treatment for my acne scars mostly on my forehead?
Acne Scarring
Mar 1, 2019
After looking at your photos, you have mostly "box car" acne scars. "Box car" acne scars are oval to round with elevated vertical sides. They leave the skin with a pitted and uneven appearance. Laser resurfacing with a carbon dioxide laser and/or microneedling with radiofrequency (eg. Infini) would help.
