Revision tummy tuck or just muscle repair? 2 years post op.
Question regarding tummy tuck
May 23, 2018
I would recommend full revision tummy tuck. The rectus diastasis is above the belly button and therefore the dissection needs to go that high anyway. After repair there will be more redundant skin that will need to come out at the lower abdominal incision.

abdomen
Jan 1, 2018
Thank you for your question. You likely had a seroma that was not drained by the the drain that was placed. You do have wound dehiscence. This will heal with wet to dry dressing changes. The question is will this heal faster with a wound VAC. In the end you will heal - question is how long it will take you to heal.

breast reduction

Do I have infection and need antibiotics? 10 days post breast reduction.
infection
Jan 1, 2018
Thank you for your question. While it is hard to tell from just a picture it looks as though you have small skin separation and associated drainage from this which can be green at times. This does not mean you are infected. Dressing changes and time will heal this. If breast turns red and painful with increasing drainage this may be a sign of infection.
Would it be possible to have both breast reduction and liposuction in one total surgery?
same time
Jan 1, 2018
Thank you for your question. If the total combined surgery will take less than 8 hours then yes the surgeries can happen at the same time. After 8 hours preoperative complication risk steps up significantly. I would tell you that an overnight observation in a hospital or surgery center is probably warranted after a lengthy surgery and would definitely inquire about this if it is over 4 hours.
