Does Lipo Freeze Really Work?

CoolSculpting does really work! It provides approximately a 25% permanent fat reduction in the area of treatment.

It is best suited for individuals that live a healthy lifestyle and just have “pockets” of fat that want to be removed.

It can also be used to enhance curves. It takes 2-4 months to see full results. (Janet Allenby, DO, Delray Beach Dermatologic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting really works for the right candidate. It uses a technology called cryolipolisis to freeze and destroy fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by the body.

Patients can expect a 20-30% reduction in fat after one treatment; multiple treatments may be needed to achieve a patient’s goals.

It is important to have CoolSculpting done only if you are a good candidate for the procedure and if you are able to maintain a steady weight, as gaining weight can interfere with results.

I recommend visiting a board-certified plastic surgeon with experience performing lipo freeze treatments for an in-person consultation to determine if you would benefit from the procedure. (John Diaz, MD, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)

Does CoolSculpting Really Work to Remove Fat?

Absolutely, CoolSculpting does a brilliant job at permanently removing fat cells from the body. Each area that is treated will see a 20-25% reduction in the fat cells of that area. The treated cells will die over a three day period post treatment.

They are then removed from the body by the lymphatic drainage system over a period of a few months The average adult has a set number of fat cells. Most adults have areas where fat will deposit naturally.

When adults gain weight, with the exception of actual obesity, the fat cells are simply getting bigger. If we lose weight, they get smaller. When these cells are destroyed and are removed from targeted treatment areas, this brings about a dramatic change in the patients countenance.

If the patient then gains weight, it will be more evenly distributed throughout the body, and will not be automatically seen in the areas that have been treated. CoolSculpting by Zeltiq really does work, and it is permanent.

And, what is really amazing is that CoolSculpting can treat so many areas of the body. Coolsculpting can treat upper and lower abdomen, love handles, bra bulges, inner and outer thighs, arms, banana rolls, knees and even under the chin.

The newest generation of applicators are incredibly comfortable, deliver more powerful results and take as little as 35 minutes for treatment. Also, keep in mind that CoolSculpting is the only non-invasive treatment for the removal of fat cells that has been approved by the FDA.

The ONLY one. (Edward Stokes, MD, West Hollywood Physician)
