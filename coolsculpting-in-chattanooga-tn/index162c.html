Coolsculpting In Chattanooga TN

Coolsculpting at a modern method of the body correction. Excess fat folds grieve and annoy their owners. They create a lot of problems and give out the flaws of the body.

What to do if you need to urgently bring the body into shape, but do not want to lie under the knife of a plastic surgeon. There is a way out for such a case!

This is coolsculpting, which became literally a discovery of American doctors. Coolsculpting is also called cryolipolysis.

This procedure allows you to get rid of excess fat without surgery. The effect on fat cells is carried out by cold with the help of a vacuum device. As a consequence, fat deposits are destroyed by a natural way, which are non-traumatic for the organism.

How does this technique work? Physicians who work in the clinic at the medical faculty of Harvard, with the help of experiments have found that by affecting the fatty cage with cold, the fat tissues die.

When a person becomes cold, he begins to tremble. Fat reserves of the body at this time actively give off thermal energy and warm the body. The layer of subcutaneous adipose tissue melts at this moment. And this means that if you apply cold for a long time to problem areas, then you will say goodbye to excess centimeters.

Cryolipolysis is successfully conducted in various medical centers. The procedure is carried out using a special equipment with vacuum nozzles. On the problem site of the body, a specialist applies a vacuum applicator.

Then the device draws a fold of fat inside the applicator and treats it with cold for approximately one hour. The patient does not feel anything, in addition to a pleasant coolness.

During the procedure, you can lie, sit, read books or listen to your favorite music, i.e. relax with comfort. At the end of the procedure, you can immediately return to your usual activities.

After cryoliposuction, you do not need any tedious rehabilitation, no medications. The procedure of cryoliposuction is absolutely harmless and safe for the body.

The skin is protected from hypothermia by a special gasket, which is located between the body and the vacuum applicator. With its help, the cold is distributed evenly over the entire surface of the treated area.

The surrounding tissues do not suffer during the cryoexposure to the fat cells. Another important point: since the destruction of fat cells is gradual, there is absolutely no load on the liver and the excretory system of the body.

Coolsculpting treatment makes it possible to remove fat folds on the sides, buttocks, back, outer and inner thighs, the inner surface of the hands, in the “riding breeches” and on the knees.

Apparatus for coolsculpting allows simultaneous cold exposure to paired areas (for example, on the hands), which saves considerable time for the patient. As noted above, the procedure for cryoliposuction is natural, so a quick result should not be expected.

Everything is done gradually. In one procedure, the patient loses 20 to 30 percent of fat cells. To completely get rid of excess fat in some particular area, it will take somewhere a month or a half.

Usually the course of cryolipolysis consists of three procedures, between which an interval of one month is mandatory. After the procedure, the fat burning processes in the body continue for another 9-10 months.

According to specialists, the complex program of combination of coolsculpting treatment and pressotherapy procedures is more effective and less expensive. It allows you to achieve a much better result. Coolsculpting has very few contraindications, they are clarified during preliminary consultation with a doctor. Precisely, this procedure can not be done for pregnant and lactating women.
