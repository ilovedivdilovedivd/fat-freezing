Doctors

Shari Sperling, DO, FAOCD, FAAD

Name: Shari Sperling, DO, FAOCD, FAAD
Statement: Dr. Shari Sperling is a board-certified dermatologist based in Florham Park, New Jersey, and affiliated with the St. Barnabas Medical Center in Livingston, NJ. She is known for her outstanding skill and expertise in all areas of dermatology, including nonsurgical facial rejuvenation treatments, ranging from injectables to skin resurfacing and rejuvenation.

When Dr. Sperling founded Sperling Dermatology in 2016, she had one major goal in mind: to help her patients look and feel incredible in their skin. Since then, her office has become one of New Jersey’s most recognized dermatology practices.

She offers her patients a wide range of innovative procedures, including Botox, dermal fillers, Kybella, chemical peels, microdermabrasion, PRP injections (aka Vampire Facials), near-infrared skin tightening, and IPL photofacials, as well as laser skin resurfacing, laser hair removal, and tattoo removal.

In addition to skin rejuvenation, Dr. Sperling performs hair restoration treatments, such as Neograft hair transplant surgery, laser light therapy, and platelet-rich plasma therapy.

Dr. Sperling is also known for her mastery of noninvasive body sculpting procedures, such as CoolSculpting, Emtone, Evoke, and Emsculpt. In fact, Sperling Dermatology quickly became the number-one CoolSculpting provider in New Jersey. By 2019, it was named the number-one provider in the entire United States.

After graduating from Binghamton University, Dr. Sperling went on to earn her medical degree at New York College of Osteopathic Medicine. She then completed an internship at Lutheran Medical Center in Brooklyn, New York, and two residencies in family practice and dermatology at St. John’s Episcopal Hospital in Far Rockaway, New York. Dr. Sperling was chief resident during both of these residencies.

Dr. Sperling holds the title of fellow in both the American Academy of Dermatology and the American Osteopathic College of Dermatology. She is also a member of the American Medical Association and the Dermatological Society of New Jersey.

Throughout her career, she has been sought out for her knowledge and expertise. She has contributed to various medical journals and serves on the panel for the National Disease and Therapeutic Index.

Dr. Sperling has also been honored with many awards, including two Doctors’ Choice Awards given by her peers, Vitals Patients’ Choice, and Compassionate Doctor Recognition.

When she is not helping patients at her Florham Park practice, Dr. Sperling supports her community by providing free skin cancer screenings.
